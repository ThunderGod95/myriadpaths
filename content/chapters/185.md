# Chapter 185: Heavenly Canopy

The suppressive force a Rank 8 had over a Rank 9 was undeniable, but that layer of golden body was truly a headache.

Li Qi thought it over and over; he just felt that layer of golden body was far too familiar, but he couldn't recall where he had seen it no matter how hard he tried.

A good while passed.

He watched Zhang Lan thrashing Zhang Dailu around, producing _dong dong dong_ sounds, much like the tolling of a bell.

Li Qi shuddered. He finally remembered why this sound was so familiar!

Wasn't this the exact same sound as the striking bell he had heard at the Heavenly Peace Temple?

Li Qi shuddered again! Cold sweat instantly broke out. Many pieces of information in his mind connected together, and a sudden flash of inspiration struck him like a bolt of lightning!

"Brother Zhang! I remember now! Buddhist light! This thing is Buddhist light!"

"I have heard that the six-tusked white elephant[^1] carries Shakyamuni[^2] through the void. With golden trays and spiritual shrines, one molds the Shramana body[^3], washing away all worldly dirt and filth. A thousand pores and a hundred orifices all sense the fragrant spirit. As long as one restrains the mind and guards the Dao, they can dwell in the world forever without change, immortal and invincible!"

"This golden body should be the Buddhist light of a Shramana body! What is going on? This should be something exclusive to the Buddhist Sect! Isn't this thing a characteristic of Rank 7?!"

Li Qi shouted in astonishment.

It was once said that the human body is a boat, carrying the divine soul to roam between heaven and earth, enduring the wind and waves of the outside world. When the boat is battered by wind and waves, it will be damaged. When the boat finally breaks, the divine soul leaks out. It will either be beaten to death by the wind and waves, its soul scattering completely, or it will fall back into the cycle of reincarnation, washing away everything to board a new boat.

A cultivator extending their lifespan is continuously fortifying this boat. Rank 9 and Rank 8 are both processes of reinforcement, gradually acquiring methods to replace its parts.

These external wind and waves are called the 'Sea of Bitterness' by some Daoist traditions, where the body is the boat to cross the Sea of Bitterness. Other Daoist traditions call it the 'River of Time,' believing that decay is caused by the erosion of the waters of time.

By Rank 7, this boat can exist for a long time under normal circumstances. The damage caused by external winds and waves can no longer outpace the speed of its self-repair, freeing it from the constraints of lifespan.

There are many ways to fortify and repair this boat. Shen Shuibi's Unsullied Frame is one of them. For a Rank 7 of the Shaman Dao, when the internal deities reach perfection and the personal cosmos exists completely independently, that is also another.

And now, what lay before them was one of the Rank 7 cultivation methods.

Rank 7 of the Buddhist Sect: the Shramana body. By walking this path, one can ultimately condense different fruits of attainment based on their own circumstances. There are four types: the Srotapanna fruit[^4], the Sakridagamin fruit[^5], the Anagamin fruit[^6], and the Arhat fruit[^7]. This is the Dao path to becoming an Arhat.

Being able to condense a Shramana body means that one's physical flesh has been cleansed of all worldly dirt, becoming immortal and invincible, no longer limited by lifespan. Normally, only those of the Buddhist Sect at Rank 7 or above possess such power.

Hearing Li Qi's words, Zhang Lan whipped his head around, utterly stunned.

He didn't understand anything about this Shramana body stuff, but he clearly heard the words 'Rank 7'.

He clamored, "This thing? It's a Rank 7 thing? Then how could it be on a Rank 9 lackey? Even if it's a dharma treasure, he wouldn't be able to drive it, right?"

For someone at Rank 9, being able to activate a Rank 8 dharma treasure was already their absolute limit. Even then, it required the right person. If their attributes weren't compatible, they couldn't even activate a Rank 8.

Li Qi's Rank 8 metal qi—he still hadn't fully mastered its use yet. He was still waiting to refine his primal spirit before he could control it flawlessly.

A Rank 9 activating a Rank 7 dharma treasure—was that even possible?

This completely exceeded the capabilities of a Rank 9!

Zhang Lan looked thoroughly confused.

However, Li Qi, in that very instant, suddenly figured everything out.

The suddenly appearing Buddhist Sect, the landscape grand array, and the Rat Soil Mound that Shen Shuibi had told him about before.

Added to all the various strange things he had encountered previously—including why the Hundred Forests Kingdom abandoned resistance and retreated to guard the passes, and why he was being targeted.

Furthermore, Li Qi figured out one crucial matter.

Why Wang Boyan wanted to help him. He hadn't been able to figure it out before, but now, seeing the Buddhist light on the envoy serving the Minister of State, everything clicked into place.

"It's not a dharma treasure, it's a blessing! The Heavenly Peace Temple! It's that Chanzhi monk from the Heavenly Peace Temple! The gold token is a smokescreen. This person appeared here just to deliver a message!"

"Brother Zhang! Let him go, we need to run, quick!"

Li Qi roared loudly, and then urged Old Ma to gallop directly toward Shen Shuibi's direction.

He had already warned Zhang Lan, and he trusted that this Rank 8 bro would definitely run faster than he could.

Right now, getting tangled up with Zhang Dailu was utterly meaningless. He hadn't come here to deliver orders at all; or rather, delivering orders was just a convenient excuse!

Did they still remember the legend of the Rat Soil Mound?

The king of a small nation devoutly offered sacrifices to the Rat Soil Mound. When a large nation dispatched troops to attack, the Rat Soil Mound intervened, helping the small nation crush the large nation's invasion.

However, without sacrifices, the Rat Soil Mound would not stir.

The Rat Soil Mound was a swarm of exotic beasts from the Western Regions.

Throughout the entire Vast Yang Prefecture, or even the Heaven-Devouring Demon Nest ahead, who had come from the Western Regions?

The ascetic monk, Chanzhi!

Only Chanzhi could bring the Rat Soil Mound from the Western Regions to this place!

Next was the blood sacrifice that Li Qi had heard so much about that his ears were growing calluses. Who was the blood sacrifice for?

The landscape grand array? Li Qi had seen the components of the landscape grand array on Lord Huangshan's side; a blood sacrifice was entirely useless there.

Then who else could make use of it?

Nonsense, of course, it was still the Rat Soil Mound! As long as sacrifices were offered to the Rat Soil Mound, the Rat Soil Mound would intervene to protect this place, cooperating with the landscape grand array to resist the fiends.

And the original sacrificial process—that was when those three Rank 8 fiends from the Rat Soil Mound came to claim their own sacrifices—had been blocked by the passing Zhang Lan and Li Qi.

Li Qi and Zhang Lan, these two meddlesome busybodies, had thwarted the original sacrificial process!

This directly caught the Vast Yang City faction completely off guard, leaving them no time to even react.

That Vast Yang Minister of War wasn't just ignoring military affairs. In reality, he probably knew all about the failure to supply military rations and the detention of the White Rhino Army commander. He just hadn't anticipated that passersby would save these hundred thousand people!

Therefore, the identity of Wang Boyan, who had helped Li Qi, was also readily apparent.

What kind of person would come to help Li Qi?

What had Li Qi done to warrant a stranger coming to his aid?

What Li Qi had done was directly oppose the Vast Yang City government. Clearly, synthesizing the matter of the Rat Soil Mound, and looking at Wang Boyan's actions and faction, he too was an enemy of the Vast Yang City government.

He helped Li Qi because he noticed what Li Qi was doing right now. Thus, he also wanted to save these hundred thousand people.

Looking back on it now, was it truly impossible for this person to produce eighteen thousand tonnes of grain all at once? He controlled the entrance to that minor world! Even if he was disliked by the Heavenly Dao and couldn't plant or cultivate crops there, if he was truly willing to pay a price and endure that minor world's Heavenly Punishment, surely he could just plunder grains to give to Li Qi?

But none of that happened. He had Li Qi communicate with the Heavenly Dao for him, obtained that world's permission, and _then_ gave Li Qi the grain.

What did all this explain?

His Dao path!

His Dao path did not allow him to do such a thing; otherwise, he had no reason not to do it.

Combine that with the faint, almost imperceptible sense of constraint around Wang Boyan.

Li Qi suddenly saw the light.

The Human Dao! Wang Boyan was a Rank 9 of the Human Dao! He was a person of the Human Dao, and he likely knew of the Shaman God Mountain's existence; otherwise, he wouldn't have repeatedly emphasized the phrase "a true shaman" to him.

This so-called emphasis was actually a hint to Li Qi!

Because he believed that only the direct lineage of the Shaman God Mountain could be considered "true shamans."

So, inferring from his motives, what would be the outcome of him helping Li Qi save these hundred thousand people?

Without receiving the sacrifices, the Rat Soil Mound chose to stand by and refuse to act. Vast Yang City would then be forced to directly confront the forces of the Heaven-Devouring Demon Nest.

In such a confrontation, Wang Boyan certainly had his own schemes as well. Although Li Qi didn't know what they were, just his background in the Human Dao was enough to make one realize what it might entail.

All the previously fragmented clues: the devout Buddhist faith of the Vast Yang Minister of State, the minor world of the Golden Wave Sect, the special circumstances of the landscape grand array he encountered on the road, and the three Buddha statues in that five-level Buddhist Pagoda.

The one in the middle looked like a Ghost King, inexplicably terrifying.

Thinking about it again, what was this conveying? Associating it with the many things he had heard about in his past world, such as... the Buddhas of the three times, divided into the past, present, and future.

The statue in the middle, representing the Buddha of the present, looked like a Ghost King.

What kind of Buddha statue would intentionally be designed like that? Moreover, he had also seen the numerous precious vessels displayed underneath it, even things like a Kapala bowl.

In the past, Li Qi had almost forgotten the things from his other world, because many of them did not hold true here. The myriad of discrepancies meant they fundamentally couldn't be applied.

However, if he applied the theory of the Buddhas of the Three Times and connected it to the current scene...

Now, under the illumination of this single golden body's Buddhist light, all the clues completely linked together. They formed a faint shadow submerged deep underwater; although still blurry, it was enough for people to perceive the shape beneath the surface.

The moment Li Qi perceived this shadow, he immediately chose to retreat.

Yes, he had left backup plans, but running away was the top priority at this moment.

A gentleman does not stand under a collapsing wall. At the very least, he couldn't stay here!

When trapped in a situation, the first thing one must do to break out is jump outside the board.

Li Qi had no desire to play chess with others. If he could use underhanded, off-board tactics, he absolutely wouldn't play fairly against someone.

Meanwhile, upon hearing Li Qi's words, Zhang Lan—although he didn't know what Li Qi had figured out—carefully thought it over. That bastard had just said he was waiting for him to die, and Li Qi had said this was a characteristic of Rank 7 Buddhist light.

Although he still didn't know what Buddhist light was or what a Buddha was, he understood the words 'Rank 7'.

Perhaps there was a Rank 7 existence standing behind him?

Zhang Lan immediately felt a headache coming on. He didn't linger any longer. Exerting force once more, he smashed Zhang Dailu into the ground with one move, and then swiftly retreated.

Since he had decided to disengage from the battlefield, he didn't delay. The speed of a Rank 8 martial artist was even faster than Old Ma. He shot backward explosively, rapidly withdrawing.

However, right after he left the battlefield, at the exact moment he felt he was safe...

A martial artist's instinct and sixth sense made him instantly freeze in place.

A shuddering sensation swept through his mind. It made Zhang Lan stop immediately and then shout toward Li Qi in the distance, "Stop! Stop!"

Unfortunately, sound didn't travel that fast.

This made Zhang Lan anxious. He directly swung his sword, slashing out a beam of sword light to send a message.

The sword light was dazzling. Even in broad daylight, it shone with a radiant blue brilliance.

Li Qi also saw the sword light.

He immediately realized that this was Zhang Lan warning him, but he didn't know what the warning was about.

Someone was coming?

Or was it something else?

However, Li Qi quickly found out what Zhang Lan was trying to warn him about.

Because he saw it. After Zhang Lan left, countless streams of qi suddenly rose up in a ring all around them.

This qi... was extremely mixed. There was demon qi, human qi, mountain qi, water qi, qi of the five elements, qi of the Eight Winds, earth vein qi, qi of the four seasons, heaven and earth qi, and so on and so forth.

Heaven and earth qi intersected; ten thousand forms of qi mingled in harmony!

Ten thousand forms of qi floated into the sky, a thousand rays of light converged in the highest heavens. A canopy formed in the heavens, draping a rosy gown over the void. A light screen composed of numerous forms of qi rose from all directions—like a mosquito net, like a canopy, but even more like... a fishing net used to trap fish.

It covered an extremely vast area, enveloping a radius of about ten miles.

As it happened, Zhang Lan had just taken a step outside of this fishing net. After stepping out, it was as if he triggered some switch, causing the heavenly canopy that enveloped the surroundings to rise.

However, although Zhang Lan had stepped out, the White Rhino Army, the hundred thousand commoners, Li Qi, Old Ma, and others were all trapped inside.

At this moment, a voice echoed out of thin air.

"The Righteous Sword Palace has always been on good terms with the Hundred Forests Kingdom. Therefore, I will leave you a path of survival. I hope you conduct yourself well in the future. Go now, and do not commit such lawless acts again."

Obviously, these words were spoken to Zhang Lan.

Zhang Lan hailed from the Righteous Sword Palace. This sudden, unknown existence seemed to have chosen to let him go precisely because of his background.

Hence, it was only after Zhang Lan took the initiative to step out that the owner of this heavenly canopy activated all of this.

Meanwhile, trapped inside, Li Qi raised his head.

Riding Old Ma, he hovered in the air, looking up at the heavenly canopy.

That voice—he had heard it before.

Inside that five-level Buddhist Pagoda, he had conversed with this voice.

A Rank 7 mighty figure—the Vast Yang Minister of War!

Wasn't this bastard addicted to the Buddhist Dharma?!

Why had he run all the way over here!?

The White Rhino Army, which had just finished forming up, immediately fell into a panic upon hearing this voice.

The lieutenant general immediately assumed a military salute, then roared at the sky, "Lord Minister of War! Where is General Lie? Is he in Vast Yang City? Before he left, he issued a military order for us to protect the commoners and retreat. What is the meaning of this current situation?!"

In the sky, however, the voice spoke coldly, "Lie Kedi committed high treason. He forged military orders and disobeyed commands. I have already stripped him of his rank and thrown him into prison to await punishment. The White Rhino Army is now under my command. All troops, heed my order! Turn around. The rear guard becomes the vanguard, and the vanguard becomes the rear guard. March southeast, return to the Vast Yang City military camp, and await further instructions."

Hearing this, the lieutenant general was stunned.

His respected superior, the true commander of the White Rhino Army, Lie Kedi, had actually been thrown into prison just as Mr. Li had said, on charges of insubordination?

Seeing the lieutenant general standing motionless, the voice in the sky urged, "Why are you standing there blankly? Quickly execute the order. After the army returns, rewards will be distributed according to merit. The next commander of the White Rhino Army will undoubtedly be you."

But the lieutenant general did not move. Instead, he raised his head, gritted his teeth, and asked, "Lord Minister of War, why?"

[^1]: In Buddhism, the six-tusked white elephant is a highly auspicious and sacred creature. It is most famously associated with Bodhisattva Samantabhadra (Puxian), who rides it to represent the power of wisdom and diligent practice. It also features in the conception myth of the Buddha; his mother, Queen Maya, dreamt of a six-tusked white elephant entering her right side before she became pregnant. It represents supreme purity and divine weight.
[^2]: A title for the Buddha, Siddhartha Gautama. The name translates from Sanskrit as the "Sage of the Shakya clan." He is the central figure and founder of Buddhism.
[^3]: "Shramana" is a Sanskrit term that historically referred to a wandering ascetic or spiritual seeker in ancient India (which included early Buddhists).
[^4]: **Srotapanna fruit:** The first of the four stages of enlightenment in Theravada Buddhism, known as the "Stream-Enterer." It signifies someone who has severed the first three fetters of the mind and has firmly entered the "stream" that inevitably leads to Nirvana.
[^5]: **Sakridagamin fruit:** The second stage of enlightenment, known as the "Once-Returner." A practitioner at this stage has significantly weakened worldly desires and ill will, meaning they will only be reborn into the human realm one more time before achieving full liberation.
[^6]: **Anagamin fruit:** The third stage of enlightenment, known as the "Non-Returner." At this level, the practitioner has completely overcome human sensual desires and anger. Upon death, they will not return to the human world, but will instead be reborn in the Pure Abodes to achieve Nirvana there.
[^7]: **Arhat fruit:** The fourth and final stage of enlightenment in the Theravada tradition, known as the "Worthy One" or the "Perfected Person." An Arhat has eradicated all fetters, achieved complete liberation (Nirvana), and broken free from the cycle of reincarnation entirely. In this novel, achieving this fruit represents the pinnacle of the Shramana body's path.
