# Chapter 176: "Coincidence"

The moment Wang Boyan came down and saw Li Qi, his expression brightened into one of sheer jubilation. He walked over with light, easy steps, cupped his hands in greeting, and said joyfully, "Brother, it's only been a few hours since we last met. I didn't expect to see you again so soon! It seems the Buddhist Sect's talk of 'destiny' holds some truth after all. Look at us, isn't this precisely destiny?"

He wore a beaming smile, appearing genuinely thrilled. "When we parted last time, you were in such a hurry that I didn't even get the chance to ask your name. May I inquire as to how I should address you?"

"Ah, I didn't expect to see you again so soon either, Brother Wang. I am Li Qi. I had urgent matters to attend to earlier and was a bit discourteous. You have my apologies." Li Qi immediately stood up to reply, a smile plastered on his face.

Deep within his eyes, however, lingered caution and suspicion.

_What is going on? This person is the sect master of the Golden Wave Sect?_

_Could there really be such a coincidence?_

The two entered the room while the others stood guard outside, pulling the door tightly shut.

Wang Boyan pulled Li Qi along and sat down. "Word went around that someone wanted to do big business with us, and I was wondering who it could be. So it was you, Brother Li! I never expected such a coincidence. Come, let's talk inside. We hit it off immediately last time, and I wanted to chat more, but you were so busy I couldn't force you to stay. This is great. To think I actually have a way to help you now. Please, speak freely. If there's anywhere I can lend a hand, I absolutely will not decline!"

"Many thanks, Brother Wang. You must have already heard the details from Di Danyi, right?" Li Qi asked as he took a seat.

Since things had already reached this point, there was no use agonizing over whether it was a coincidence. He might as well have a proper discussion with Wang Boyan.

"Yes, the moment I heard his report, I knew it had to be you. After all, at a time like this, who else but Brother Li would care about the life and death of over a hundred thousand commoners? Your righteousness is truly admirable, I am in awe," Wang Boyan said with deep praise.

Li Qi shook his head. "Please, no more flattery. You're making me blush. Let's discuss the grain. You may control the Golden Wave Sect, Brother Wang, but you likely can't produce eighteen thousand tonnes either, can you?"

Getting back to business, Wang Boyan smiled and replied, "Indeed. Eighteen thousand tonnes is neither a massive amount nor a small one. It's certainly enough for standard daily circulation, but if forced to produce it all at once, practically every family would have to weigh their options."

"However, the Zhang family is different. They have their own exclusive channels. They can definitely bring out eighteen thousand tonnes. In fact, you could say that amount is nothing to them."

Hearing this, Li Qi noticed the absolute certainty in Wang Boyan's tone.

"Why do you say that, Brother Wang? What exactly are their channels?" Li Qi asked, mildly curious.

_Why does he believe so strongly that the Zhang family can effortlessly produce so much grain?_

Wang Boyan leaned in mysteriously, lowering his head to whisper into Li Qi's ear. "I wonder if Brother Li has ever heard of the outer-realm worlds?"

_Outer-realm worlds!?_

Li Qi's eyes widened. He had not expected such an answer.

"The minor outer-realm worlds are myriad bizarre existences lying beyond All Under Heaven. As far as I know, the Zhang family controls an entrance to one of these minor outer-realm worlds. Their grain comes from that very place. It is because of this that they can provide tens of thousands of tonnes of grain despite their limited sphere of influence," Wang Boyan explained.

Suppressing his shock, Li Qi feigned composure and asked, "I see. In that case, if I go straight to the Zhang family, will they sell me the grain?"

"Of course not. Your identity is unknown, and your background is unclear. Why would the Zhang family sell grain to you?" Wang Boyan chuckled. "Even if I were to introduce you, it would be impossible. Grain is just grain in small amounts, but in abundance, it becomes a strategic resource."

"Furthermore, there is something you are likely unaware of, Brother Li. By trying to save these commoners, you have violated a grave taboo. People might come looking for trouble with you soon. The Minister of State stands behind the Zhang family, and the Minister of State has no desire to see those people live."

This rapid succession of revelations left Li Qi thoroughly stunned.

Frowning, Li Qi asked, "What exactly do you know, Brother Wang? And why are you telling me all this?"

"I am merely sharing things that outsiders like you are unaware of. I have operated in Vast Yang City for a long time, whereas you have only just arrived. There is much you do not know, so it falls to me to explain it all to you," Wang Boyan said.

"But why?" Li Qi couldn't understand.

Wang Boyan let out a hearty laugh, grasping Li Qi's hands happily. "Why? Haha, Brother Li and I are kindred spirits. I'd swear an oath of brotherhood with you right now if I could! Why wouldn't I? Besides, you are rushing about to save over a hundred thousand people. Your heart is filled with profound righteousness. How could I sit idly by? Those who follow the Dao find many helpers. The moment I heard of your deeds, I viewed you as a true confidant. Naturally, I will offer my full support!"

Having his hands held by this man sent a chill down Li Qi's spine, but he kept his face impassive while his mind raced.

_Is he telling the truth?_

_Not necessarily._

_But can he actually provide help?_

_Yes._

_Then I'll just listen as if it's the truth for now._

With that decided, Li Qi smiled in return. "Since that is the case, my mind is at ease. Please, Brother Wang, tell me. How much more is there that I don't know?"

"The main issue, as you likely know, is that Vast Yang City abandoned the town pass, forsook the commoners, and ordered the army to immediately fall back to defend the city. This was actually the Prefect's idea. They believed the pass couldn't be held, so they planned to retreat a certain distance and use the power of the intervening mountains and rivers to lay down a massive formation, stopping the demon invasion."

"Therefore, to see this plan through, the Minister of State will absolutely not allow you to slowly evacuate and save those people. As for slowly transporting grain over there, that is even more impossible," Wang Boyan concluded.

"That makes no sense. Evacuating the populace shouldn't conflict with setting up a formation," Li Qi said, his brow furrowing.

Wang Boyan shook his head, his tone laced with a dark implication. "But what if I told you the commoners are a part of the formation?"

_Bang._ A fissure cracked down the chair Li Qi was sitting on, and the stone tiles beneath his feet shattered into several pieces.

His expression turning dark, Li Qi forced out two words. "A blood... sacrifice?"

"Exactly. The Prefect is a Rank 7 expert of the Shaman Dao. Naturally, he knows the methods of blood sacrifice. So, do you understand now? Why you've been hitting a wall at every turn? You are a bright and perceptive man, Brother Li. Think about it. Under these circumstances, if you go to the Zhang family and ask them to sell you grain, is it remotely possible?"

"What he wants is the total annihilation of the populace near the pass. He needs every last one of them to die, otherwise, the formation cannot be completed."

Wang Boyan spoke with absolute finality, then looked at Li Qi. "What do you think, Brother Li?"

Taking a deep breath, Li Qi spoke slowly. "The Zhang family belongs to the Minister of State. They will never sell me grain, and approaching them would only alert the enemy. Just like right now—if you were also the Minister of State's man, Brother Wang, my whereabouts would have already been exposed to him. Knowing that I am still running around trying to fix this, he might just order my elimination to prevent me from ruining his plans."

"Precisely, that is exactly the logic. Although I am not part of the Vast Yang City government and utterly despise their methods, the Minister of State stands behind the entire Grain Alliance. He is a Rank 8 expert, and he can mobilize several other Rank 8 cultivators. Brother Li, you surely understand the despair of facing multiple Rank 8 opponents?" Wang Boyan said, flicking his fan open.

"Thank you for the warning, Brother Wang. However, you aren't just telling me this to convince me to give up, are you?" Li Qi asked.

Wang Boyan had told him all of this for a reason. It couldn't be just to make him retreat, otherwise, this whole conversation was pointless.

This man definitely had his own motives. Revealing such sensitive inner workings alone was enough to make Li Qi feel that something was amiss.

Seeing Li Qi's penetrating gaze, Wang Boyan closed his folding fan and spoke in a deeply earnest tone. "Of course not. If I just made you retreat, not only would you be unwilling to accept it, but I would be unwilling as well."

"Originally, I was agonizing over how to resolve this matter. But upon hearing that we share the same aspirations, an idea struck me immediately. Why can't the two of us simply go to the outer-realm worlds to procure grain ourselves?"

"If we find a minor outer-realm world and extract the grain needed to save those people, wouldn't we bypass them completely? This way, the Golden Wave Sect could secure a status equal to the Zhang family, and you could obtain the grain you need to save all those lives. It kills two birds with one stone, doesn't it?"

After finishing his pitch, Wang Boyan looked at Li Qi with hopeful anticipation, waiting for a reaction.

Li Qi, however, was dumbfounded.

He felt like he had been stunned more times today than in the past half a year combined.

"Aren't you oversimplifying things, Brother Wang? How could finding a minor outer-realm world possibly be that easy?" Li Qi shook his head.

The outer-realms did possess countless worlds, but traveling there was not something a mere Rank 9 cultivator could achieve.

"Would I speak of this if I wasn't confident? I just so happen to hold a clue to an outer-realm world. My only problem is that I lack an expert to help me subdue that minor world's Heavenly Dao. Could Brother Li perhaps—?" Wang Boyan offered.

Hearing this, Li Qi's internal alarms blared.

_Another coincidence?_

_Who is he trying to fool?_

The fact that this man was the sect master of the Golden Wave Sect was already absurd enough. Their interests aligning was barely believable. But now, Wang Boyan was claiming he actually had an entrance to an outer-realm world on hand?

_Is this a joke?_

_Does a mere Rank 9 have the qualifications to meddle with the outer-realm worlds?_

"Looking at your expression, Brother Li, you don't believe me? Then take a look at this." As he spoke, Wang Boyan produced a jade pendant.

Engraved upon the jade were two characters: "Hundred profound."

With just one glance at the pendant, Li Qi suddenly felt a wondrous stream of qi surging toward him.

Pondering for a moment, he sensed that this qi lacked the ability to harm him. Thus, he opened his personal cosmos and accepted the wisp of qi.

And then, he understood.

This trace of qi was called scholarly qi, a type of qi capable of carrying divine will and information.

The moment the scholarly qi entered his body, Li Qi instantly comprehended the jade pendant's function.

The pendant was the key to a cultivator's cave dwelling.

That cave dwelling was a fated opportunity left behind by a senior cultivator. Upon reaching Rank 7, he was about to leave the Baiyue region. Deciding to gift his former residence, along with unwanted furniture and dharma treasures, to future generations, he scattered the keys for those destined to find them.

And within that cave dwelling existed an unranked minor outer-realm world.

Unranked, this world could not sustain any cultivators or support the advancement of techniques. It was populated almost entirely by mortals, with only a scattered few unranked individuals.

Such minor worlds produced almost no qi and possessed negligible resources. To that Rank 7 paragon, it had merely been a potted plant.

A tiny world kept at home, purely for ornamental purposes.

Yet, there was no doubt that it contained grain, and that grain was perfectly edible.

"I had intended to enter it long ago. However, even though that world's Heavenly Dao is only Rank 9, its resistance is fiercely intense. I couldn't conquer it alone, nor did I want to reveal this to the rest of the Grain Alliance. So, I had been searching for a helper. It was only when I met you, Brother Li, that I made up my mind to share this secret," Wang Boyan explained.

"Why me?" Li Qi asked.

"I have long heard of your name. Shaman Li Qi, who came from the south, performing righteous deeds and acts of charity all along the way. Your reputation is widespread. Now, you are single-handedly shouldering the burden of these abandoned commoners without seeking anything in return. To be honest, with a character like yours, I can trust you."

"Working with you, as long as we agree on the terms beforehand, I am certain you will not break your word. But collaborating with those wolves and tigers in the Grain Alliance? One would have to constantly watch their back against being stabbed, devoured, and wiped clean."

"This is what they call 'a gentleman can be duped by his own righteousness.' It is only with a gentleman like you that I dare to reveal such an item without fear of being schemed against." Wang Boyan looked at Li Qi with a beaming smile.

At those words, a realization dawned on Li Qi.

_Last night at Heavenly Peace Temple, when this man struck up a conversation, had he already recognized me then?_

That made things barely plausible, but still... it was slightly too coincidental.

_Could it really just be chance?_

Doubts swirled in Li Qi's mind, but he couldn't be certain.

After all, it genuinely could be a coincidence.

After deliberating for a few seconds, Li Qi finally gave his answer. "Taking such private action remains a poor plan. I will go see the Zhang family first. If they refuse, then I will accompany you to brave the outer-realm world, Brother Wang."

"Very well. I will remain here for the next few days. Come whenever you are ready, Brother Li, and we shall leave immediately." Wang Boyan stood up and addressed Li Qi.

Li Qi also rose and nodded in acknowledgment. Then, he walked out the door, mounted his horse, and galloped away.

He would try both options. If the Zhang family was willing to sell, that would naturally be for the best. If not, he would return.

Standing behind, Wang Boyan watched as Li Qi disappeared into the distance.

Di Danyi walked over, trembling with fear. Dropping to one knee and cupping his hands, he looked up and said, "Boss... why must we..."

"Call me Sect Master." Wang Boyan's jovial expression abruptly shifted into a mask of cold indifference.

"Yes, Sect Master. What about the former Sect Master?" Di Danyi asked tentatively.

"Tell him to scram as far away as possible. He is not allowed to return within a month," Wang Boyan said coolly.

"Understood!" Di Danyi immediately scrambled up and dashed off into the distance.

The manor returned to total silence, leaving only Wang Boyan to leisurely begin brewing tea on the stove.
