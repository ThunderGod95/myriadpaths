# Chapter 113: The Mirror

The Golden Crow used her own body to block the attack of the two demon kings, her feathers scattering in all directions.

Li Qi saw this scene with crystal clarity.

Sure enough, someone had helped him block the fatal blow.

But by doing so, the Golden Crow would inevitably lose her ability to resist. The time she could hold on was now drastically shortened.

The mountain god of White Snake Mountain was still trying to attract Shaman God Mountain's attention. Li Qi had to maintain the ritual so the god could draw the gaze of the Grand Diviners. Otherwise, his actions would very likely be ignored.

It was just... how could the Bear Slaughter King possibly stand by and watch this happen?

Seeing Yang Ning acting as a shield, rendering him temporarily unable to break through, he took another look and realized the Invocator hadn't even entered the ranks yet.

In that case, he didn't even need to attack.

"Roar!" The Bear Slaughter King bellowed furiously. Murderous intent erupted, pouncing toward Li Qi!

This wasn't an attack. Even the sound waves had been isolated by Yang Ning.

However, the Bear Slaughter King knew that just a single leaked wisp of murderous intent would be enough to terrify this Invocator to death.

Meanwhile, on Li Qi's end.

The murderous intent washed over him.

Li Qi felt his soul and body alike tightly gripped by excruciating agony!

"Ugh—" He let out a muffled groan as blood flowed from his seven orifices. His heart felt like it was about to burst!

The blood almost formed a geyser; his nosebleed was practically shooting out!

A low buzzing hummed in his ears as intense terror and boundless pressure converged in his mind!

He could feel cockroaches, lice, and rats growing inside his body. They gnawed at his already rotting internal organs, seemingly having lived there for a long time!

His divine soul was being dragged out!

The surrounding chill tightly gripped his heart, and everything seemed to be screaming silently. Li Qi began to tremble, even his mind freezing over.

A vicious flame scorched his soul.

Li Qi could sense a colossal entity. It was enraged. It was enraged at him!

That unimaginably massive will was staring at him with terrifying focus.

It wasn't a careless glance.

It was a highly focused, furious wrath!

Li Qi shuddered violently, on the verge of being crushed.

At this moment, Li Qi felt the entity gazing at him, and within that gaze was unbridled fury.

Within this fury-filled gaze carried curses and words.

It didn't say a single word, yet Li Qi had already learned everything through this gaze alone.

The murderous intent of a Rank 6 demon king blanketed his heart like the overarching heavens.

There seemed to be something inside his body! Something suffocating! The agony was devouring his spirit and destroying his will.

The maddening pain was like a raging flood, filling Li Qi's entire divine soul to the brim with curses.

A raging fire ignited within his divine soul. His consciousness, memories, and even his very personality were forced to burn!

Yet, Li Qi remained standing, maintaining the ritual.

Someone had already laid their life on the line to block the main attack for him. This was merely pure murderous intent. Could he really not even withstand this much?

True, a Rank 6 entity was like a celestial immortal to him. But even if it was a celestial immortal, it was impossible to make him yield, kneel, and give up with murderous intent alone.

Unfortunately, some things couldn't be resisted with willpower alone.

A few seconds later, Li Qi sprayed out a mouthful of fresh blood and collapsed.

An endless nightmare where everything twisted its form, forcing him toward the extreme of madness. It was like having his head submerged in water, unable to breathe, left with no choice but to drown!

Drowning in an endless tide of fresh blood, a profound, dark nightmare—Li Qi felt that there was something inside his head!

Flitting around his ears were noises like a clamoring crowd in a bustling market, as if a million ducks had suddenly taken flight and started quacking.

It became increasingly difficult to control his mind. Helpless and with no alternatives left, he drifted and fluttered like falling into the mortal dust.

His hands and feet were out of his control.

His body was forced to kneel on the ground.

His fingers dug into the earth, scraping until they were bloody. His nails even flipped backward from the excessive force, yet it was all to no avail. There was no way Li Qi could resist.

The gap of a single rank was already considered a world of difference.

For an unranked Invocator to try and withstand the murderous intent of a great Rank 6 demon—it was simply wishful thinking.

The power projection of the White Snake Mountain god weakened slightly.

A ritual could tolerate minor errors, but it couldn't be flawed for long. If it continued to be interrupted and ultimately failed, they wouldn't be able to attract a Grand Diviner's attention.

Li Qi knew this as well.

"Come!" With a furious roar, he unexpectedly drew his willow branch and ruthlessly whipped himself with a _crack_!

A portion of his divine soul was instantly whipped away. The intense agony caused the veins all over his body to bulge, his body stiffening as he stood straight up.

He was in so much pain he couldn't even make a sound. He could only force out low, breathy groans of 'Heh... heh' from his throat.

The severe pain of his damaged divine soul had already robbed him of his mobility.

But at the same time, this pain allowed him to temporarily suppress the murderous intent. Murderous intent was ultimately illusory; compared to the very real act of self-mutilation, it still fell short.

"Rabbit, help me up..." he muttered indistinctly through gritted teeth.

Shen Shuibi hurriedly rushed forward and supported him.

Li Qi stood tall once more, but then immediately went soft. He could only barely lean against Shen Shuibi to maintain a standing posture, using it to sustain the ritual.

"I can't hold on. Just prop me up," Li Qi murmured to Shen Shuibi while barely remaining standing.

"Okay!" Shen Shuibi looked at Li Qi with concern, then cast a glance at Yang Ning in the near distance, who was bitterly holding out against the two demon kings.

Even she herself had lost all her cultivation and shattered her Unsullied Body.

_Please don't let it all fall short at the final hurdle..._

She looked at the trembling White Snake Mountain, her heart filled with anxiety.

However, ten-odd seconds later, the Golden Crow couldn't hold on any longer.

The great golden-red bird plummeted from the sky with a mournful cry. If someone were to deal her one last blow now, she would truly die, her soul extinguished.

But the two demon kings had no time at all to finish her off. What they needed to do was destroy the ritual to avoid drawing the gaze of the Grand Diviners!

"Die!" The Curse Demon King shouted at the top of his lungs, unleashing a hundred and twenty percent of his power to strike directly at Li Qi.

Purple curse power surged forward.

Li Qi stood rooted to the spot, unmoving, and continued to maintain the ritual.

Moving was useless anyway. He might as well face the demon king and die standing.

With his head held high, he watched the purple curse power spread across his body.

In an instant, his body turned a purplish-blue. Even the blood he coughed up was purplish-blue.

Yet, a Grand Diviner still hadn't acted.

Things had gone completely south.

He was completely screwed now.

Li Qi closed his eyes and waited for death.

However, after keeping them closed for a few seconds, he realized his consciousness was still intact.

_That shouldn't be possible, right?_

_It wasn't an instant kill? And there's no pain either?_

Confused, Li Qi opened his eyes to look.

To his shock, he found that the previous valley, the demon soldiers and generals, the two demon kings, and the myriad anomalies had all vanished without a trace.

Before him lay only a 'mirror.'

For a hundred miles, the land was as flat as a mirror, so smooth it felt like one could slip while walking on it.

Just what... was going on?
