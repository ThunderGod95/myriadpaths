# Chapter 191: The Serious Bunny (II)

In the end, Wang Boyan still agreed to the meeting.

He understood how inconceivable this event was. Because of that, he simply had to see who this new Horse King really was.

The factions lurking in the dark corners of every city were like cockroach nests. Perhaps they weren't strong in the eyes of experts, but unless every last person was slaughtered, it was absolutely impossible to eradicate them completely.

Within a single day, without any intelligence agency noticing, she had subjugated countless factions across the entire Lower Nine Gates in a mere eight hours. She tore down the old Horse King and took the throne herself, her position as steady as Mount Tai. She even made the former Horse King willingly act as her pawn, driven at her command.

What kind of ruthless capability was this?

Just what kind of person could accomplish such an unimaginable feat?

Wang Boyan shuddered just thinking about it. If this person became his enemy in the future, would he be able to withstand it?

Today she wanted to be the Horse King, and she took the position in just eight hours.

What if tomorrow, she decided she wanted to be the 'Boss' in his place?

He had to thoroughly figure her out. He had to see for himself what kind of figure she was.

Striking while the iron was hot, Wang Boyan arranged everything for the meeting within fifteen minutes.

Everything was in perfect order. In less than an hour, their meeting was set.

Another thirty minutes passed.

Wang Boyan arrived at the designated location.

It was at the Red and Green Pavilion, the largest den of indulgence in the Lower Nine Gates.

The name was derived from the phrase 'red and green cosmetics', hinting at the nature of its business.

This place was extremely luxurious and covered a vast area. Upon entering the building, one could see the weeping shadows of azure woods, the rippling patterns of green waters, and artificial mountains suspended in bright light, with intricately carved lattice windows facing the eaves.

There were red terraces and green pavilions connected by floating walkways. Spell arts were arrayed inside to control the temperature, living up to its claim: 'The four seasons exist outside, but neither winter's chill nor summer's heat can be found within.'

Beyond the eaves lay artificial mountains and pools, adorned with bamboo, pine, orchids, and angelica cascading down the steps. They held the wind and gathered the dew, exuding flowing fragrances and rich perfumes. Brilliant light shone everywhere. There wasn't much in the way of solemnity, but the omnipresent pavilions of drifting scents stirred the heart with romantic thoughts and made the corporeal soul and ethereal soul ripple with desire.

From the outside, it was a continuous, elegant stretch of green walls and vermilion pillars. Walking inside, one could see endless beauties with silken garments dancing in the wind and light gauze fluttering. Strolling down the corridors, the resounding blast of cymbals, the mournful turns of reed pipes, the harmonious chiming of zithers, and the ceaseless singing and dancing filled the ears.

Bamboo blinds and emerald curtains, clear pools and small hills, flowers and trees half-hidden amidst crimson railings and curving pillars.

Just as Wang Boyan arrived, a young servant stepped forward to bow, then led him into a secluded room, serving him with golden goblets and entertaining him with rare delicacies.

Once inside the room, singing girls and dancing women struck lutes and blew reed pipes. The music of strings and woodwinds played in continuous succession, tirelessly echoing day and night.

Wang Boyan was the first to arrive, walking into the building.

He laughed heartily as he entered, sitting down naturally. "Haha, I once heard that in this Red and Green Pavilion, there is a beauty named Yuehua who perpetually tops the floral rankings. She excels at playing the konghou[^1] and can sing _The Bright Consort Departs the Frontier_, moving all who hear it. My blessings are shallow, and I have yet to see her. Since the Horse King is playing host today, might I have the opportunity to feast my eyes?"

The welcoming servant immediately replied with a smile, "Since the Boss has graced us with his presence, how could a few women refuse to see you? Tonight, the Red and Green Pavilion welcomes no other guests; we open our doors solely for the Boss. All twenty-eight renowned beauties on the Book Immortal Floral Rankings are present!"

Wang Boyan put on a delighted expression, tapping his folding fan. "Excellent, excellent."

"I've long heard the fame of this Book Immortal Floral Rankings. It is said that in a single day, thousands of gold pieces are squandered just to see these beauties display the utmost extremes of their charm. Today, I shall certainly broaden my horizons," Wang Boyan said with a laugh.

Hearing this, the servant clapped his hands, sending a secret signal outside.

Outside, young women barely sixteen stepped lightly to a melodious tune, singing softly as they danced. Accompanied by zithers and lutes, they followed the rhythm perfectly. They were like the bottomless clarity of bright ripples, like fresh dew yet to dry. Their emerald scarves brushed gently, and the fragrance of their garments assaulted the senses.

There were those whose every step captivated the gaze, occasionally displaying elegant footwork, serving barefoot.

There were those of peerless appearance, possessing a unique and solitary elegance that naturally exuded a transcendent charm.

There were those with exceptional temperament, like crimson clouds bordering the heavens or white cranes soaring amidst the clouds.

There were those with soft, enchanting voices, like new willows swaying in the wind or delicate orioles hidden beneath the flowers.

There were those who appeared frail and innocent, too weak to withstand the wind, so tender they seemed to shy away from the sun.

There were those skilled in music and song, able to blow melodies through leaves and play to the wind while embracing the moon.

There were those who danced like spinning flowers, their golden lotus steps brushing the floor, their waists delicate and slender.

There were those with the ultimate singing voices, dressed in plain clothes with light makeup, their voices ringing clear as young phoenixes.

There were those skilled in composing poetry and lyrics, outstanding and aloof, brilliant and pure as ice and snow.

The twenty-eight beauties, each with their own unique characteristics, created a dazzling spectacle. One could only see their autumn-water eyes glancing over, brimming with unspoken words. Their spring-like visages were profoundly elegant, and every movement was a delight.

Those who played instruments played, those who sang sang, and those who danced danced. As soon as they displayed their skills, the listeners were entranced, and the viewers' hearts swayed.

The servant continued speaking into Wang Boyan's ear, "Does the Boss know? Normally, although these beauties are skilled in song and dance, they refuse to lightly sing unless it is for an intimate gathering of true connoisseurs. Even if praised repeatedly, they will not easily display their dancing forms. There are wealthy magnates in the city who do not hesitate to spend thousands of gold pieces just to add a touch of color to their lives, yet they might never even catch a glimpse of their fragrant visages."

"But the moment these beauties heard the Boss was coming, they pushed aside all their scheduled appointments. They rushed to dress and apply makeup, waiting only for the Boss to come and admire the flowers. This alone shows the prestige of the Boss's name."

Wang Boyan laughed heartily. "Hahaha, you certainly have a sweet mouth. Men, reward him!"

Even as he spoke of rewards, a genial smile on his face, he merely swept his gaze over those beauties without lingering. Then, swaying with each step, he made his way to the main hall.

However, the moment he stepped into the main hall, he froze.

In the main hall, there was another beauty waiting for him.

Wang Boyan could tell that, unlike the vulgar powder and cheap cosmetics feigning nobility outside, the woman sitting here possessed an aloofness and nobility that seeped from her very bones.

She hadn't come out with those vulgar women; instead, she waited inside.

At a single glance, it was like seeing a bright moon and a clear breeze, or the evening wind brushing through willows. She was as delicate and refreshing as a cup of light tea, her demeanor leisurely, her appearance gentle. Gazing upon her washed away all mortal cares, like the clear radiance of the moon sweeping over him. She lacked even the slightest trace of mortal dust, causing Wang Boyan to momentarily lose his wits.

Others might not see it, perhaps only thinking she was a pretty girl, but he could spot this woman's unforgettable qualities at a single glance.

Aloofness in the heart, unfettered freedom in the bones, charm in the flesh, and beauty in the skin.

This was what truly counted as a beauty!

Those outside were nothing more than a pile of skin and flesh.

Wang Boyan was utterly astounded by her celestial appearance, forgetting even to breathe.

Several seconds passed before he finally snapped his folding fan shut and asked the servant beside him, "Which one on the Book Immortal Floral Rankings is this? Why have I never heard of her?"

Hearing this, the servant's face instantly turned ashen, cold sweat breaking out from fright.

He hurriedly knelt and kowtowed, his head thumping against the floorboards. "Boss, please do not joke! Please be careful, this is the new Horse King of our Lower Nine Gates!"

The new Horse King!?

Wang Boyan was stunned.

He had seen that this woman was absolutely out of the ordinary, assuming she was one of the welcoming courtesans outside who had merely fallen into the mortal dust. He never imagined that she was actually the new Horse King.

This was the person who had unified the Lower Nine Gates in just eight hours?

She was the ruthless overlord who had taken complete control of Vast Yang City's underground factions in a mere half a day?!

While Wang Boyan was still trapped in astonishment, the woman spoke.

"Wang... Boyan, right?" the woman began.

Her voice was also beautiful.

However, Wang Boyan immediately snapped back to reality and replied, "Exactly. I wonder what the Horse King invited me here for? It is our first meeting, so if I have caused any offense, I ask for the Horse King's forgiveness."

As beautiful as she was, he still clearly knew how to weigh what was truly important.

Then, he watched as a hint of radiance slowly appeared in the eyes of the woman before him, almost as if... she had just woken up.

The previously dull and vacant look in her eyes quickly reverted to a sense of lively agility, and she sat up straight. "There's no need to pay attention to those trivial matters. Everyone, back down. Who told you to arrange so many people?"

The kneeling servant hurriedly said, "But wasn't this personally arranged by you, Horse King?"

"When did I arrange this?" The new Horse King frowned.

"You said everything should be as usual. In the past... this was the standard," the servant said, his tone panicked.

"Then get rid of them right now. I need to speak with Wang Boyan alone. Everyone else, get out," the new Horse King scolded.

The servant hastily dismissed all the banquet attendants in the surroundings, leaving only the new Horse King and Wang Boyan.

Wang Boyan didn't feel much about it, only that this new Horse King acted somewhat strangely.

She didn't know what she had arranged?

Could it be that she wasn't as shrewd and capable as he had imagined?

Then how had she managed to flip the heavens and change the earth within eight hours?

Wang Boyan was filled with doubt but didn't say much. He just watched as all the servants and beauties in attendance withdrew.

Then, he saw the new Horse King sweep a glance over him and mutter, "The Wang Family of Jiannan? Why do you have people here too..."

Wang Boyan's eyes darted up, flashing with a sharp, terrifying light.

Killing intent welled up in his heart, wiping away any feelings of tender affection for the fairer sex.

How did this person know?!

His background had been exposed?

When? Where?

How was that possible!?

But the new Horse King didn't seem to notice any of this. Instead, she entered the main topic on her own accord. "My name is Shen Shuibi. From now on, I will speak first. Do not interrupt me."

"Li Qi sent me to find you. There are a few things I need to tell you."

Wang Boyan's entire body trembled. He immediately took a deep breath, suppressing the shock in his heart.

Li Qi? She was Li Qi's subordinate?

Brilliant. Truly worthy of being a direct disciple of Shaman God Mountain. To have thoroughly grasped his bottom line and background just like that! Was she here to show him who was boss? Did Li Qi already know of his plans?

Truly formidable. He had underestimated the capabilities of Shaman God Mountain disciples.

In his heart, Wang Boyan secretly raised his alertness towards Li Qi to the highest level. However, Shen Shuibi didn't care at all. She just continued speaking on her own, "The matter concerning Chanzhi has been exposed. Right now, to protect the hundred thousand refugees from being used as a blood sacrifice, Li Qi took the initiative to deal with Chanzhi. He told me to find you, saying you'd have a way."

"Next, I need to find a way to reach Li Qi. Give me whatever information you have on Chanzhi."

"Someone has also gone over to the Righteous Sword Palace. I also have some information regarding the appearance of a Rat Soil Mound within the Heaven-Devouring Demon Nest."

"Bring out all the resources you have on hand and place them under my command. I am going to save Li Qi."

"Finally, if you cooperate honestly, I can give you the _Luofu Moon-Coursing Visualization Art_."

Shen Shuibi's words were concise and comprehensive, quickly stating her needs and the reward she could offer.

Although Wang Boyan was very surprised by the earlier words, he had kept his composure.

It was only the final sentence. Upon hearing the words _Luofu Moon-Coursing Visualization Art_, he stood straight up. The surroundings were suddenly filled with a coercive pressure, and conceptual frameworks manifested in the area. Instantly, this stretch of heaven and earth gained 'rules'.

'Rules' that uniquely belonged to Wang Boyan.

Rules bound the surrounding area. Within this space, Wang Boyan was the rule. The external Yin and Yang temporarily slowed down, and the four seasons were nearly lost. Shen Shuibi was also instantly enveloped within the framework.

Wang Boyan shouted sternly, "Just who exactly are you!? Why do you know of the _Luofu Moon-Coursing Visualization Art_?!"

His voice was filled with shock and fear.

Shen Shuibi frowned. "How do I know? I wrote it myself, why wouldn't I know?"

Hearing this, Wang Boyan was so furious he laughed, sneering, "What a joke! Do you know what kind of supreme expert authored this item? In the Luofu grotto-heaven, there is a Fairy Yuehua, titled the Divine Mother of the Supreme Yin and Upper Yellow within the Moon. The _Luofu Moon-Coursing Visualization Art_ was created by her venerable self! It is one of the absolute best Rank 5 visualization arts in All Under Heaven. You wrote it yourself? Don't tell me you wrote some trash method and just coincidentally gave it the same name?"

He fundamentally did not believe that the person before him could produce the _Luofu Moon-Coursing Visualization Art_.

One must know that the Fairy Yuehua who authored the _Luofu Moon-Coursing Visualization Art_ was the most mysterious figure on Mount Luofu.

This person possessed a revered and secretive title. She would often go hundreds or even thousands of years without showing her face. Not even Mount Luofu itself could find a trace of her, and her name hadn't even been passed down. Only Lady Luofu and another True Monarch—the Daytime Crimson Qi Sharp Smite True Monarch with the body of a Golden Crow—could locate her.

However, although she rarely met with people, her reputation was massive. The methods she created and the pills she concocted were illustrious throughout the entirety of Jiannan. A single pill was incredibly precious; it would be difficult to trade an entire Rank 7 world for one. Its efficacy was also superlative, almost peerless within Rank 5.

Even within Mount Luofu, unless one possessed a diligent heart that loved the truth and was destined with the skeletal records of a Soaring Profound Heavenly Immortal, they wouldn't even be able to hear of it.

Even if one had the qualifications, they still had to undergo three trials. Initially, it was passed down to one person every three hundred years, and later, one person every seven hundred years, which was enough to show its sheer preciousness.

Now that Mount Luofu had been destroyed, this item was even closer to being lost forever.

As an aristocratic clan of the Tang Empire stationed in Jiannan, the Wang Family boasted a long heritage. This was the only reason they knew of such secret information.

Even for the people of Shaman God Mountain, these were not things that Rank 9 or Rank 8 cultivators could come into contact with.

Shen Shuibi frowned upon hearing Wang Boyan's words.

So, she replied quite frankly, "If you don't want it, then forget it. I'll switch to another one. Let's make it the _Profound Gate's Scripture of the Treasure Sea_. This is a Rank 7 cultivation method. There shouldn't be any issues with this one, right?"

"Why would you have that?" Wang Boyan immediately asked.

"You're so annoying! Do you want it or not?" Shen Shuibi grew irritable and casually waved her hand.

With that, the surrounding 'rules' were shattered instantly.

The solidified heaven and earth crumbled in an instant, failing to produce even the slightest restriction.

The already thoroughly infuriated bunny stated bluntly, "It's a fair trade. If you don't want to trade, then I'll just rob you. After you're dead, I'll place the reward on your corpse. The Wang Family of Jiannan will find it a very worthwhile deal."

Her patience was running out.

Ever since Li Qi left, an indescribable irritability had been bottling up in her heart. Now, the bunny had gone red-eyed.

If he wasted any more of her time, she was going to use brute force.

[^1]: Konghou (箜篌) is an ancient Chinese stringed instrument that belongs to the harp family.
