# Chapter 186: Schemes

The Vast Yang Minister of War paused upon hearing these words.

It was the first time he had ever heard a subordinate soldier ask him 'why'.

Why?

There is no why!

In the military, the commands of a superior to a subordinate are absolute. How could there be a 'why'?!

Thus, the voice of the Vast Yang Minister of War rang out again. "Why? There is no 'why' in the military! Do you intend to disobey orders?"

Hearing this, the lieutenant general felt a surge of blood rush to his head. His face flushed red, and his hands even trembled.

He had heard the phrase 'disobey orders' far too many times today.

Disobey orders! Disobey orders! Disobey orders!

It was always disobeying orders!

Did these important figures know nothing else to say?!

However, the lieutenant general held back his temper. He dropped to one knee and said, "I dare not deceive the Minister of War. It is just that the Minister's military orders conflict with General Lie's, so this subordinate general neither dares nor wishes to comply."

"Thirty years ago, there was a great rebel named Boyue in Vast Yang. Relying on generational wealth and leading the masses of an entire prefecture, he raised an army to attack the imperial court. No one could suppress him. His intent to secede and submit to the Heaven-Devouring Demon Nest posed a tremendous threat."

"At that time, seeing the situation, General Lie urgently sent out a call to arms to crusade against the rebel. He sacrificed his family and risked his own life to stall the rebel Boyue for ten days. He held out until the Kingdom Lord brought reinforcements, finally preventing a major disaster. Although he was not the one with the highest merit, his exhaustion of effort and shedding of blood was enough to uphold great righteousness to All Under Heaven and repay the Kingdom's grace! The Kingdom Lord once said, 'Lie Kedi is a loyal subject of the Hundred Forests; he should be treated as an eminent figure of the nation.' As such, the White Rhino military banner was bestowed upon him."

"The white rhinoceros is an exceptionally divine creature among rhinos. Its horn possesses a radiant glow. By day, it looks like an ordinary horn, but when cast into the dark of night, its textures shine brilliantly. The light emanates from within, appearing like a torch. Thus, its horn is used as a guide for righteousness. By burning the rhino horn to illuminate the surroundings, it can reveal a hundred ghosts and verify the loyal and righteous. This was to praise General Lie's loyalty and bravery."

"Why would such a loyal subject and righteous man, an eminent figure of the nation personally appointed by the Kingdom Lord, be imprisoned by the Minister of War? And we, as his direct subordinates—our commander left behind military orders for us to protect the refugees. The white rhino is a benevolent beast that clearly distinguishes right from wrong and exposes a hundred ghosts. Since we use the white rhino as our military banner, our eyes naturally must also be bright and piercing. We cannot act like dumb oxen who close their eyes and follow whoever pulls them!"

The lieutenant general raised his head and shouted loudly. His voice was deafening, and the white rhino military banner flapped fiercely in the wind. Condensing into military qi, it circled into the high sky. The phantom of a gentle white rhinoceros manifested above the military formation; it was clear and reflected everything in minute detail, letting out a low growl.

Following this, all the soldiers of the entire White Rhino Army struck their weapons, producing a solemn sound.

Clearly, the lieutenant general's words had resonated deeply within the hearts of these White Rhino Army soldiers.

It could be seen that the true commander of the White Rhino Army held a very high position in their hearts.

Perhaps only such a general could build a legion like the White Rhino Army.

This turn of events also caused the commoners not far away to panic inexplicably.

They didn't understand what was going on.

Was the government abandoning them? Why would the previously amiable White Rhino Army, who loved the people as their own children, quarrel with their superior?

One must know that the reason these civilians were so peaceful was because the White Rhino Army kept telling them that the government wouldn't abandon them. Coupled with the White Rhino Army's consistent leading by example—never abandoning them when danger came—they were able to feel secure. Only then could a hundred thousand people successfully evacuate without any mishaps.

The White Rhino Army had constantly assured them that the government wouldn't abandon them; the government just temporarily hadn't freed up their hands to help.

Therefore, when Mr. Li arrived, they had constantly spoken highly of the government.

But now, what was happening?

However, no one had the time to pay attention to them right now.

They only saw...

In the sky, the voice of the Vast Yang Minister of War grew impatient. "Acting Commander, are you planning to lead the White Rhino Army in rebellion?"

The lieutenant general shouted loudly towards the sky in response, "It is not rebellion! It is—"

Before the lieutenant general could finish his sentence, a streak of blue lightning crossed the sky. A dragon colt with blue scales and black fur streaked over from a distance, chasing the wind and lightning, treading on auspicious clouds as it galloped forth.

With thunder and lightning mingling, and wind and rain surging together, the ringing sound of thunder galloping like iron-clad warhorses, and the roaring wind resembling the beating of war drums—the dragon colt's rapid advance created a massive commotion, instantly drawing the attention of countless people.

Almost everyone around turned their gaze over. Sitting atop it was a young man with a serious expression, two golden lines trailing him like shadows. Flying to this spot at this exact moment, his voice echoed all around, interrupting the lieutenant general's words, "It is not rebellion, but! Setting things right!"

It was Li Qi!

Li Qi had finally rushed over from the other side!

However, the phrase 'setting things right' completely baffled the White Rhino Army.

Setting things right?

What did Mr. Li mean by that? Wasn't the White Rhino Army the one disobeying orders and rebelling? But—

Just as everyone was stunned, Li Qi shouted loudly, "Generals and soldiers! Do not listen to this person's slanderous words! He is not the Vast Yang Minister of War at all!"

"You have been deceived!"

Hearing this, an uproar erupted below.

Not only the soldiers, but even the commoners not far away heard these words.

Those commoners who were already panicking because of the sudden change in events now became even more confused.

However, what responded to Li Qi was not words.

Right after Li Qi finished speaking, sharp light suddenly flared from the heavenly canopy in the sky!

Killing intent surged!

Killing intent enveloped the surroundings, thick as mercury yet calm as a mirror. From the Nine Heavens above, it swept out to cover the earth and sky, suddenly pouring down like an avalanche of raindrops!

Its momentum was like half the sky collapsing; its sound was like the raging waves of a bottomless river.

It was like the wrath of heaven.

Everyone around felt as if they had been bound by ropes.

The White Rhino Army below still had a moment to breathe under the protection of their military qi, but Li Qi's entire body went stiff, completely unable to resist.

How could he possibly resist?

Facing a Rank 8 was hard enough for him, and this person before his eyes claiming to be the Vast Yang Minister of War was a Rank 7.

What kind of immortal figure was a Rank 7?

A prefecture's Minister of War was an existence akin to a supreme ruler within a radius of ten thousand miles. Who knows how many elite soldiers and fierce generals were under his command. A single word from him could shake hundreds of millions of people and easily decide the life and death of countless individuals.

Under such an attack, it was fundamentally impossible for Li Qi to have the slightest chance to fight back.

As those 'raindrops' fell, the sky seemed to tilt.

Just in the instant Li Qi was about to be destroyed, a phenomenon suddenly erupted all around him.

Bright flames flew like purple fire, dazzling lightning flashed like golden snakes. Yin and Yang lines formed by various spell arts spiraled outward.

Like a long dragon twisting and turning, they flew around Li Qi. Surging purple flames even rose from the ground. Countless Yin and Yang lines darted through the water like shuttles, dense as hemp, resembling a fleet of sparrow-boats braving the wind and breaking the white waves. At first, they blew gently, but suddenly the wind grew fierce, carving out vortex after vortex.

The wrath of the collapsing sky collided with the vortexes of Yin and Yang lines.

The Yin and Yang lines shattered inch by inch, retreating steadily. However, their retreat was exceptionally organized and methodical. Each line deflected the force; connected one after another, strand by strand, they actually drew two Yin and Yang Taiji fishes in the air. Rotating seamlessly, they completely dissipated the power of the sky's wrath without the slightest overflow.

Protected by the Taiji fishes formed by the Yin and Yang lines, Li Qi remained entirely unscathed.

The momentum of the collapsing sky was clearly more than ten times stronger than the formation composed of the Yin and Yang lines, yet it was neutralized just like that.

This was a level that Li Qi's shaman spell arts could never touch. The subtleties of Daoist Sects' spell arts in this aspect far surpassed those of shamans.

The voice of the Vast Yang Minister of War came from the sky, "What an excellent spell art. Which senior expert is observing here and intends to ruin the national affairs of the Hundred Forests Kingdom? Do you dare reveal yourself for a meeting?"

However, there was still only a voice, with no actual person stepping forward.

As soon as these words were spoken, a rabbit on the ground suddenly transformed into human form.

With a slanted hair knot and a delicate, graceful posture, her temperament was aloof and cold, much like a solitary, high-hanging moon.

It was just that her expression was a bit blank.

After revealing herself, Shen Shuibi shook her head. Then, she spread her hands, her slender fingers moving rhythmically. Countless Yin and Yang lines converged once more, gathering and dispersing in forwards and backwards motions, faintly forming the atmospheric phenomena of the palace where the Supreme Yin anchored.

Unbeknownst to anyone, Shen Shuibi had actually set up a formation here, and it was precisely this formation that had protected Li Qi's life.

In fact, over a dozen hours ago, Li Qi had already used landscape transmission to have Shen Shuibi prepare in advance. He told her to transform into a rabbit and hide herself, preferably without letting anyone else discover her.

At the time, Li Qi felt that he would definitely be ambushed or even intercepted and killed, so he prepared a backup plan in advance.

But obviously, even Li Qi hadn't expected to encounter a Rank 7 powerhouse.

And this person was actually shameless enough to personally attack him.

As for Shen Shuibi, she quickly reacted. Raising her head to look at the sky, she said indifferently, "You cannot touch him."

Her tone had almost no fluctuation, just like how she normally spoke.

The voice of the Vast Yang Minister of State rang out again. "Oh? Why can't I touch him? Relying on a Rank 8 like you to stop me? Your spell arts are exquisite, presumably inherited from a high-tier sect, but your Daoist Sects' foundation is not here. I'm afraid your sect cannot help you. Insisting on opposing this Minister of War will not end well for you."

Shen Shuibi didn't speak.

Mainly because she didn't know what to say; she was never good at communicating to begin with.

So, she simply soared up, flew to Li Qi's side, then tilted her head and looked at him.

Having spent so much time with her, Li Qi understood her meaning.

The rest was left to Li Qi; he just needed to command.

Li Qi then asked in a low voice, "Can you beat him?"

Shen Shuibi shook her head, indicating she couldn't.

A Rank 9 fighting a Rank 8, she could still manipulate things a bit.

But a Rank 8 versus a Rank 7—the gap was too huge. Barely managing to stall him was already the absolute limit of what a Rank 8 could do.

One must know that in many lower-tier worlds, the difference between Rank 8 and Rank 7 was the difference between an immortal and a mortal! A Rank 8 was still just a cultivator, but a Rank 7 had already ascended as an immortal, possessing endless lifespan and enjoying unfettered freedom.

Even for Shen Shuibi, it was very difficult to operate under such a disparity.

Li Qi nodded, smiling bitterly to show he understood.

Things had blown up; they were really in a bind now.

However, this wasn't the most difficult situation Li Qi had ever encountered, so he hadn't given up just yet.

If he truly had to name the most despair-inducing situation, it would be when he hadn't even entered the ranks and had to face two Rank 6 demon kings.

Right now, he wasn't facing a Rank 6 demon king, merely a Rank 7. Moreover, Li Qi was also much stronger than he had been back then.

Therefore, Li Qi puffed out his chest, raised his head, and shouted loudly, "The eyes of men can be deceived, the laws of the nation can be bent, and all living beings can be fooled. But I wonder if the Great Master understands that the eyes of Heaven are supremely perceptive! Its majesty is incredibly stern and impossible to hide from!"

"Great Master Chanzhi, your act of impersonating the Vast Yang Minister of War has been exposed!"

Li Qi rebuked loudly. However, his words startled both the White Rhino Army below and Zhang Lan outside the battlefield.

Why had a Great Master Chanzhi suddenly popped up again?

Some soldiers had heard the rumors about Chanzhi, while others had not. As for Zhang Lan, he was completely unaware of anyone by that name, so Li Qi's sudden shout left everyone confused.

What did this have to do with Chanzhi?

In the sky, the voice temporarily fell silent.

But Li Qi did not stop.

Although he was the weakest person present, his voice was the loudest. He continued to yell, "That five-level Buddhist Pagoda—my eyes deceived me back then. That isn't a dharma treasure at all, but the manifestation of your dao rhythm! It is formed from the condensation of your cultivation and dao rhythm!"

(Recalling Chapter 171 to 172 once more.)

"The first level is the first step on your Dao path. A hundred Arhats and a hundred fiends symbolize your one hundred and eight subduals of devils, forging your Dao foundation."

"The second level represents how, after subduing a hundred devils, you painstakingly practiced Zen meditation and treated all things with a Bodhisattva's heart. The water in the glazed vase and the tassel-adorned cloud shrines are all reflections of this! They are the result of your Dao Heart suppressing the hostile, devil-subduing aura within you."

"The Sutra Altar on the third level represents your process of pious cultivation and slow accumulation. It is also your stepping stone from Rank 8 to Rank 7—accumulating scriptures, understanding classics, and integrating them as your own. Thus, your Buddha heart was formed, allowing you to step onto the fourth level: the Hall of the Three Buddhas."

"The Hall of the Three Buddhas on the fourth level represents your state of mind. Three Great Buddhas as one person—it is actually just you. It is the Buddha heart within you, representing the past, the present, and the future!"

"The Buddha of the Past is old, wearing patched cloud robes, leaning on a bamboo staff, and holding Buddhist scriptures on palm leaves. It represents your past methods of cultivating the mind and nurturing your nature."

"The Buddha of the Present is brand new, hugging its knees and shrugging its shoulders. It looks like a Ghost King, inexplicably terrifying. It represents what you are doing right now, because the hostile aura from subduing devils has already backfired on you! Your Dao path is on the verge of straying. One more wrong step, and you won't just look like a Ghost King—the true Buddha will become a true devil, your Dao path will be completely destroyed, and you will fall into the Devil Dao!"

"You want to turn over a new leaf, so you designed the Future Buddha in the posture of sitting withered among ten thousand mountains, with long eyebrows sweeping the ground. But Great Master Chanzhi, are your current actions truly leading in that direction?"

"Your fifth level, which is your current appearance—those canopies, pipas, precious banners, square flags, damaru drums, and Kapala bowls are all made of clay. It is clear that you have no Buddhist light, and your Buddha heart is almost completely lost. Only the form remains, completely devoid of true intent."

"Within the Buddhist shrine, a Buddha statue is enshrined. I presume that is your only remaining Buddha heart. Yet, you molded its golden body with precious materials and added colorful murals. In those murals, myriad living beings coexist harmoniously, all centered around the Buddha. It is obvious what your true scheme is!"

"Great Master Chanzhi, you want to use the Shaman's Dao of the balance of all things as a reference. You aim to use it to restore your Buddha heart and become the white-eyebrowed, pure-hearted Buddha of your future statue. That is why the murals inside your Buddhist Pagoda are like that."

"The Minister of War was likely the sacrifice for your actions. If my guess is correct, the true Minister of War is already dead. His Dao cultivation has already been crushed and absorbed by you. That Minister of War above your five-level Buddhist Pagoda is the proof."

"This blood sacrifice is probably not just to defend against the fiends, but your attempt to imitate the Shaman Dao! You are dabbling in sacrifices, and this blood sacrifice reinforced by the landscape grand array is the strongest shaman cultivation method you learned after devouring the Minister of War."

"Great Master Chanzhi, am I right or wrong?" Li Qi's tone was grave as he questioned the sky.

The sky remained silent for a while.

Then, the Vast Yang Minister of War's voice spoke.

"Utter nonsense."
