# Chapter 206: Slaying the Demonic Monk (III)

Shen Shuibi didn't actually know how to use a sword.

She had just learned it over the past month.

But that did not matter.

It was enough.

A Rank 5's divine will, even if only a minuscule trace was integrated into the sword techniques, was enough to be called the sword of an immortal.

Did he not understand the true weight of the Moon-Within Supreme Yin Essence Upper Yellow Spirit Mother Goddess?

Monk Chanzhi still had not realized the true nature of their layout.

From beginning to end, it was never Zhang Lan and Shen Shuibi who were making the moves.

Zhang Lan and Shen Shuibi were merely the pilots.

What truly confronted Monk Chanzhi was this very world itself!

Using the grand formation to regulate the mountains and rivers to mirror the human body, combined with the Wang family's resources, Shen Shuibi had transformed this formation into a controllable dharma avatar!

Silently and seamlessly, this formation—originally meant solely to regulate earth qi and water veins—had been refashioned by Shen Shuibi into a attack formation.

Regulating the earth qi and water veins only required a slight alteration...

To change the water veins into blood qi.

To replace the mountains as muscles and bones.

The Yin-Yang hexagram methods of the Daoist Sects supplemented the four directions and eight trigrams.

The shamanic methods of the internal deity nurtured the sun, moon, and the mountains and rivers.

The Human Dao's methods of establishing order wove the myriad threads of the world's framework.

With the four dimensions set, the eight trigrams facing each other, the sun and moon suspended together, the scenery rising in unison, the rules serving as a foundation, and balance acting as the guide.

In this way, it became a simulated personal cosmos.

This was what Li Qi had brought to the table this time.

By utilizing the grand formation originally constructed with the power of an entire prefecture, they had forcefully engineered a false Rank 7 cultivator.

It only required a single operator to control this entity.

Li Qi, Shen Shubi, and Wang Boyan had each contributed their greatest skills, solving countless technical problems to finally achieve this magnificent feat.

To put it another way, if the original formation was a massive farm tractor, then Shen Shubi, as chief engineer and designer, had taken its parts, disassembled it, and built a colossal war machine.

Now, she was the one piloting it.

And for that previous sword strike, Zhang Lan had been at the helm.

Monk Chanzhi still paying attention to some kind of formation enhancement was completely meaningless.

Because there was no formation enhancement at all.

What Monk Chanzhi was facing was the formation itself!

He was fighting a mechanical titan!

That dharma avatar of Shen Shuibi was this very titan.

This was the power of knowledge.

And that was not all.

Li Qi had previously asked Wang Boyan to provide two Rank 8 cultivators to help.

Those two Rank 8 cultivators were currently stationed at the other two grand formations.

It had been mentioned before that in Vast Yang Prefecture, there were three such arrays for regulating earth qi.

Three such arrays were enough to cover the entirety of Vast Yang.

The other two were currently under the control of those two Rank 8 cultivators, converging the earth qi and water qi of Vast Yang to supply power to this titan.

Meticulous arrangements, along with knowledge that transcended this realm, had jointly planted this bomb.

However, this simultaneously enraged Monk Chanzhi.

After being sent flying by that sword strike, he had managed to stabilize his posture with great difficulty.

Yet, the moment he steadied himself, all of the monk's refined elegance completely vanished.

His body began to mutate.

"Then, do not blame this poor monk for showing no mercy."

After leaving behind those words, Monk Chanzhi's physical body began to undergo a violent transformation!

His skin turned into something akin to a twisted piece of pitch-black leather, covered in disgusting calluses.

His neck extended outward gigantically like a snake, the skin upon it wrinkled like a lizard's. Although his entire body maintained a humanoid shape, as a whole, it resembled a super-sized reptilian torso.

Snake-like tentacles sprouted and spread from his lower body.

He grew two additional heads and six arms, all as pitch-black as ink, with skin as rough as hide.

This time, it was not a dharma avatar, but his true body.

The lineage of Mahakala.

The body of a Devil God.

Monk Chanzhi was finally revealing his true strength.

This was no longer the petty squabbling from before.

He was getting serious!

He chanted in a low voice, "A wicked heart invites vile spirits, and the heavenly devil enters the mind and belly. If one perceives this as divine enlightenment, they will succumb to the swarm of wickedness."

The flesh and blood on his body intersected, his tentacles intertwining demonically, and the three-inch Buddhist Kingdom instantly shattered! Then, it transformed into a devil kingdom!

The original heavenly beings tore off their skin, turning into rakshasa evil ghosts!

The originally incomparably radiant three-inch Buddhist Kingdom instantly opened up in all ten directions, spewing forth an endless gloom.

The concepts of name and form dissolved into shadow, giving way to a profound darkness completely saturated with the filth of the kalpa!

Monk Chanzhi had never been a buddha of great compassion. He was an absolute devil, the kind who would blood-sacrifice a hundred thousand people without blinking an eye.

Monk Chanzhi's devil body held four bowls in four hands. Inside the bowls was a thick, foul-smelling black soup, appearing to be the blood of some creature.

One hand held a bell, and the shaken bell was completely red, like the color of blood.

The devil body lifted a bowl of the foul-smelling liquid and said solemnly, "I drink the Five Nectars! Thus, a fraction of the great devil of arrogance enters my core. Disrespecting pagodas and temples, destroying sutras and statues, all living beings shall enter the endless Nether Prison. Losing true reception, they shall fall into ruin!"

As his voice fell, his empty hand held up the three-inch Buddhist Kingdom... No, it was a devil kingdom now!

Supporting the devil kingdom in his palm, a single hand covered the sky!

That hand was raised high, and the weather was obscured, the earth qi was thrown into chaos. Even the dharma avatar was affected by the disorder brought by the devil, cracks beginning to appear on its body.

The fiends of the devil kingdom swarmed out, then suddenly transformed into a sea of fire!

The sea of fire burned across the sky! It grew increasingly vigorous, even somewhat frantic.

Tongues of flame continuously billowed out like from a chimney. Wild devils one after another poked their heads out of the flames to observe the outside world, each with a green face and prominent fangs, inexplicably terrifying.

The sky was reflected in a bright red hue. Countless ghostly faces formed the visage of a devil god.

It was Monk Chanzhi.

He looked down from within the endless hellfire.

His piercing gaze descended from the sky, staring intently at the ground.

The faces of Shen Shuibi and Zhang Lan were both bathed in a fiery red glow; the entire sky blazed with infernal flames.

The fiends pounced down.

Their claws were long and sharp, full of overlapping anger, harboring thoughts of venomous harm.

When the fiends pounced, the gates in all ten directions of the devil kingdom opened wide, and a hot wind violently rose, blowing scorching black sand.

This hot wind blew over the animals and mountains below that had no time to escape. All living creatures turned entirely black, the hot sand burning their skin, stripping the flesh right to the bone!

Yet, it was not just the heat.

The countless grains of black sand ground against everything like sanders. Those struck by the hot wind and black sand had their bones and flesh pulverized, pus and blood flowing out. The bitter agony was immeasurable. Across a radius of a hundred miles, mournful wailing echoed, just like a Nether Prison!

"A Nether Prison aspect?" Below, Shen Shuibi immediately recognized the foundation of this method.

All these devils were the lives that Monk Chanzhi had previously slaughtered!

He had refined the ethereal souls and corporeal souls of these lives into such devils, then used the denizens of the Nether Prison as a blueprint to forge this devil kingdom!

This was a recreation of the suffering of reincarnation in the Nether Prison. An endless tide of resentment sought to drag every living thing into its torment.

That three-inch buddhist kingdom was this three-inch devil kingdom.

Buddhas and devils as one—this was exactly the fundamental core of Monk Chanzhi's cultivation!

The buddha is the devil, and the devil is the buddha.

With the Nether Prison descending upon this place, the earth qi and water qi below were thrown into complete chaos!

Boundless black fog surged in the surroundings, akin to dark clouds, dust storms, fierce winds, and torrential rain. Wicked karma, wicked words, wicked thoughts, and wicked fates continuously surfaced. Demonic mist billowed all around in waves of wicked atmosphere, as if the Heavenly Devil had descended.

Formations were rooted in the environment.

And the surrounding environment had been forcefully altered.

That constructed dharma avatar was on the verge of collapsing.

This was the reason why such arrays rarely succeeded. Unless they possessed another foundation, relying solely on the power of heaven and earth made them easily intercepted by others.

The power of heaven and earth could be used by you, but it could also be used by others. It all came down to who had the greater skill.

Rank 7 experts could change the heavens and alter the earth. The array you formed using the power of heaven and earth would be nothing but drawing water with a bamboo basket if you could not defend the surrounding heaven and earth.

However, everything was within their expectations.

Seeing this, Shen Shuibi formed incantation gestures with both hands. Then, Old Ma suddenly flew out from behind her.

Old Ma, who had been prepared long ago, swiftly flew over. Shen Shuibi vaulted onto the horse and galloped away at top speed.

It was time to evacuate.

And Zhang Lan had already made his escape. After delivering that sword strike and catching his breath, he had slipped away.

Without the colossus, a Rank 8 cultivation base was merely cannon fodder here. It was better to run away quickly.

Yet, Chanzhi did not pursue. On the contrary, he suddenly sat cross-legged in place, placed his palms together, and restrained his qi.

Because that tottering dharma avatar suddenly lit up.

Earth qi and water qi began to surge violently.

Was it not just self-detonation?

If the Vast Yang Minister of Works knew how to do it, how could Shen Shuibi not?

The furnace had long been stuffed with flammable wood shavings; it only needed a tiny spark to burn furiously.

Monk Chanzhi furrowed his brows. He had also sensed it; the array below was at the end of its rope. After unleashing two consecutive sword strikes, the array was tottering, on the verge of being completely destroyed by the Nether Prison aspect.

But, the array was about to explode.

If he pursued them, not only might he fail to catch them, but he could also be easily injured by the self-detonation.

Therefore, he steadied his mind. His devil god body began to shrink, going from a size that could cover the sky with one hand to the size of a child.

Retracting his power and stabilizing his defenses.

Although this made him more vulnerable to one-on-one damage like a brute's desperate sword strike, the impact he would suffer from a large-scale blast like self-detonation would be much lower.

The dragon colt and Shen Shuibi ran further and further away.

However, it did not matter.

They could not escape.

That dragon colt, even when bursting with its full strength, could only cover a mile in an instant, and could last for a few minutes at most.

Although the dragon colt's speed was fast, considered unparalleled among Rank 9 beings, it was nothing in the eyes of Monk Chanzhi.

This little bit of speed was like taking a stroll in front of him.

How fast was the speed of Rank 7?

One only needed to look at the Vast Yang Minister of Works from moments ago. In just three or four breaths of time, he had run over a hundred miles away, breaking out of his range of interference and escaping without a trace.

While that was a maximum speed akin to a desperate hundred-meter sprint, it still highlighted the swiftness of a Rank 7, and that was even considering that neither party specialized in sprinting.

If it were a Rank 7 who specialized in speed, increasing that speed by dozens of times would not be difficult.

_Wait, where is the Minister of Works?_

He—

The moment this doubt popped into Monk Chanzhi's mind, he saw countless dharma treasures sparkling in the sky, forming a starry expanse composed entirely of dharma treasures.

Unknown as to when, the Minister of Works had actually turned back!

Moreover, with a face full of grief and indignation, he was actually attacking him!?

Where did he get the courage?

---

A hundred and fifty miles away, Wang Boyan stood atop a mountain peak, lightly tapping his folding fan as he gazed out at the distant battlefield.

His mission was to wait on the outside and execute several logistical tasks.

Within a hundred miles, there was likely not a single living soul left; all the birds, beasts, fish, and fowl had already been swallowed by the Nether Prison.

A true living Nether Prison on earth.

The Nether Prison was manifested here on a small scale. Countless fiends and evil ghosts roamed, leaving only mournful cries echoing between heaven and earth.

Ghost-gods ran rampant, brambles grew from the earth, and mosquitoes, horseflies, bee stings, venomous snakes, poisonous insects, hot winds, and black sand rushed at their faces, devouring everything.

Inverted wicked views shrouded a hundred-mile radius, and all existences within were entangled by the ten evil karmas.

When the human body failed and life ended, all would fall into the Nether Prison.

A Rank 7 battle was far too terrifying.

However, what was even more terrifying was Li Qi.

Just moments ago, that Rank 7 Minister of Works had fled to this very spot.

Following Li Qi's instructions, Wang Boyan had handed him a sealed letter.

Wang Boyan did not know its contents; no one had opened it.

But that dignified Rank 7 Minister of Works, a man who cherished his life incomparably and scurried like a rat, actually gritted his teeth and turned right back around after seeing Li Qi's letter.

_I truly wonder what was written in that letter._

Thinking of this, Wang Boyan turned around. With a modest attitude, he cupped his hands and addressed a scrawny, blonde-haired man behind him, "Has the Rat King observed the situation clearly?"

The thin, blonde-haired man appeared to be in his forties or fifties. He had an ugly appearance, a hunched back, and a short stature, standing roughly five feet tall.

The hair on his body was entirely golden, including the hair on his head, his eyebrows, and even his chest hair; it was a pure, un-glowing gold.

The teeth in his mouth were sharp, with his front incisors being particularly prominent.

He looked like a mutant, but from his title, his identity was already known.

The Rat King of the Rat Soil Mound.

Without a doubt, he was absolutely a Rank 7 expert as well. He was someone who had traveled alongside Monk Chanzhi from the far west of All Under Heaven to its southwest.

"I've seen it clearly enough. However, based on my understanding of Chanzhi, with the strength you've displayed so far, your chances of winning don't exceed ten percent. Up until now, that bastard still hasn't revealed his true strength." The Rat King grinned, looking at the distant battlefield.

Over there, the terrifying pressure before the array's self-detonation and the brilliance of countless dharma treasures in the sky were shining.

The battle was still ongoing.

"Then, what if the Rat King were to intervene?" Wang Boyan asked again.

"If I intervene, the odds of winning become fifty percent," the Rat King said. "Now you know why I insisted on watching you guys make a move before deciding whether or not to step in, right?"

"Is that demonic monk truly that powerful? I saw him exhaust his divine abilities earlier, and all of them were top-tier, excellent abilities. Whether it was the Mahamayuri Mantra, the three-inch Buddhist Kingdom, or using buddhas and devils as one to transform the Buddhist Kingdom into a Nether Prison aspect, their might was immense. He still has trump cards?" Wang Boyan frowned.

"Buddhas and devils as one, buddhas and devils as one. Did you see a devil? Or did you see a buddha?" the Rat King retorted with a question.

Wang Boyan took a deep breath, instantly understanding.

The trump card of the demonic monk Chanzhi was buddhas and devils as one.

It was not the buddha of Mahamayuri, nor the devil of the Nether Prison aspect, but the combination of the two into one.

Then it was no wonder.

Yet, after hearing these words, Wang Boyan immediately cupped his hands. "Then what does the Rat King think of the young master's promise? This demonic monk is so powerful; if he were to be used as an offering for a blood sacrifice, the Rat Soil Mound would surely reap boundless benefits, would it not? The young master has already agreed that he will not take a single sliver of the power from the blood sacrifice."
