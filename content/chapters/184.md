# Chapter 184: Vajra Steel

Hearing this, Zhang Dailu was surprised, but he did not panic. Instead, he raised the gold token and shouted loudly, "How bold of you all! This is the Hundred Forests Kingdom. With the Kingdom Lord above and the Prefect in the center, we envoys uphold the majesty of the entire nation. How dare you act so insolently!?"

"Acting Commander, is this the 'righteous hero' you spoke of? Attempting to murder an official within the army, showing blatant disregard for the law, acting and speaking with such unbridled arrogance! Receive the order at once, form up the array, and quickly help me take down this scoundrel!"

As he spoke, the gold token flashed. That gold token was actually a dharma artifact! Its quality had reached Rank 8, enough to suppress any ordinary mountain or water god!

Everyone present below Rank 8 felt a heavy suppression, almost forcing them to their knees.

This was the gold token of a prefecture's Minister of State, carrying the might of the Hundred Forests Kingdom's laws and discipline. It was a Human Dao dharma treasure. As long as the Hundred Forests Kingdom still existed and the majesty of its national laws remained, it would remain effective and possess considerable power.

The lieutenant general of the White Rhino Army half-knelt on the spot, breaking out in a cold sweat. He was stuck in place for a moment, unsure of what to do.

As a man of the military, he should naturally obey military orders, but how could he raise weapons against Mr. Li and Mr. Zhang?

Zhang Dailu did not wait for the White Rhino Army's lieutenant general to think. Instead, he shouted again, "What are you staring blankly for!? Suppressing violence and punishing corruption to manifest national discipline—is this not the duty upon your shoulders? This scoundrel has openly violated the law and must be executed; all should unite to destroy him. Why haven't you quickly gathered your military forces to capture him!?"

But Li Qi placed a hand on the lieutenant general's shoulder and whispered a sentence, "General, let me tell you one thing. Do you know why the commander of the White Rhino Army never returned? It is because he, too, was thrown into prison on charges of disobeying orders. His life or death is currently unknown."

Then, without another word, Li Qi struck!

Two streams of metal qi drew out two golden lines, piercing forward silently.

This time, it was not just two streams, but two merging into one, transforming into a single strand.

The golden line thrust forward at an extreme speed that ordinary Rank 9s would find hard to react to.

Li Qi's sneak attacks had never failed before. When dealing with Rank 9 enemies, he had always dispatched them with a single strike, never needing a second blade.

Although these two innate divine weapons had not been forged by skilled craftsmen, they inherently divided into Yin and Yang paths. Combined into one, they were already considered top-tier among Rank 8!

Li Qi usually used them separately because, even separated, they were Rank 8, which was more than enough to crush Rank 9 cultivators who lacked counter-methods.

But right now, the opponent he faced was not some small fry like a rural mountain god.

Zhang Dailu, this burly old man, twitched the corner of his mouth and actually reacted! The muscles on his body tensed, a layer of Martial Dao internal qi coated his fist, and then, he punched out!

The two sides clashed head-on for the first time.

Two fists met the blade edge, erupting with mighty force.

Zhang Dailu was a proper expert from the government. His cultivation was stronger than Li Qi's, and his dharma treasure was better than Li Qi's.

Li Qi was very clear that against the gold token in the other party's hand, even with his metal qi combined into one, its might was only barely equal, unable to achieve a crushing advantage.

A heavy, irresistible punch instantly shattered the sword qi, carrying immense pressure as it hurtled straight toward Li Qi's face.

Li Qi's eyes widened in disbelief.

The metal qi had actually been knocked away by a fist!

But at the same time, Li Qi beckoned with both hands. While defending, the knocked-away metal qi flew back in an instant.

Rank 8 metal qi could travel five miles in a single breath.

Being knocked back a few dozen meters was fundamentally no different from being right in his face.

The metal qi erupted with all its power, reaching its absolute maximum speed with a momentum like solid metal. Traveling at a speed that literally sparked friction in the air, it was like a meteor piercing the sun. Without any fancy changes, it blasted directly into Zhang Dailu's shoulder at an absolutely unavoidable speed.

Actually, Li Qi had aimed for the head.

But Zhang Dailu was no amateur. At the very last split second, he tilted his head. Although he couldn't completely dodge it, he managed to move his head out of the way, taking the blow with his shoulder.

Zhang Dailu forcibly endured this strike, and his fist also arrived right in front of Li Qi.

However, at this moment, Zhang Lan had already arrived.

Zhang Lan also cultivated the Martial Dao, and he was at Martial Dao Rank 8!

He placed one hand on Zhang Dailu's fist and then gave it a fierce flip.

Zhang Dailu directly flew out, his figure tearing through the clouds! Carrying mountain-shattering momentum, he smashed through a cluster of clouds in the sky, flying off to who knows where.

Zhang Lan didn't say much. With a standing long jump, his body soared into the air, chasing straight after him.

But that punch, even though the fist hadn't made contact, the wind of the punch had already scraped Li Qi. It smashed directly into his face, making Li Qi see stars, dizzying him. His nose bled profusely, the bridge of his nose snapped, and his whole body flew directly out of the tent, sliding several meters away.

There was no helping it. When a shaman faced a martial artist at close range, the outcome was highly likely to be like this.

Regardless of the Dao path, as long as one was closed in on by a martial artist of the same rank, the result was entirely predictable.

Outside, Shen Shuibi seemed to realize what happened. Her droopy ears suddenly perked up, and she looked curiously at Li Qi, who had fallen to the ground.

However, Li Qi immediately covered his bleeding nose and waved his hand. "Don't make a sound, don't come out. I can handle this."

Thus, the rabbit lay back down.

She didn't look worried in the slightest, as if it wouldn't matter even if Li Qi died right here.

But Li Qi didn't care about this. He stood up and looked at the conflicted face of the White Rhino Army's lieutenant general.

"General, you do not need to be conflicted. You don't need to take part in this battle. But I guarantee with my life that what I said is absolutely not false. What you do now is up to you, General. The safety of the White Rhino Army's commander is likely in your hands."

After Li Qi finished speaking, he didn't wait for the lieutenant general to react. He whistled, and seeing Old Ma rushing over, he mounted the horse and hurried toward the battlefield.

At this very moment, the soldiers and civilians outside finally noticed the situation.

The figure that had just shot up into the sky had attracted a lot of attention.

The lieutenant general also made a decision in his heart. He immediately picked up the military Tiger Tally and activated the spell art within it.

The moment the Tiger Tally moved, a large drum formed by a white illusory phantom instantly manifested. A white rhinoceros was depicted on the drum surface, possessing a gentle yet strong and courageous aura.

The lieutenant general grabbed the drumsticks and smashed them down fiercely.

_Dong! Dong! Dong! Dong!_

The military drum sounded, and the entire army sensed the summons.

Almost all the soldiers of the White Rhino Army, regardless of what they were doing—eating, chatting, resting, or even using the latrine—quickly wrapped up their tasks and converged in this direction.

When the drum beats to rally the army, the summoned must arrive!

The commoners were still confused, not even realizing what had happened. Many of them gathered around to watch the commotion.

But the White Rhino Army couldn't care less about them now. Once the men were assembled, the lieutenant general immediately shouted to the surrounding officers, "Divide into ten platoons! Gather the commoners and do not let them wander around! Then rally the brothers, and let's go ask the High Envoy about the whereabouts of our commander!"

"Orders received!" the officers responded loudly in unison, then quickly split into ten platoons, each going off to execute their respective missions.

On the other side, the area where Zhang Lan and the envoy were battling was now completely devastated.

Battles between martial artists did not involve the various wondrous spell arts seen among shamans.

There were only flesh-on-flesh exchanges and terrifying frontal melee combat.

Both sides had brought out their dharma treasures.

Martial artists could also use dharma treasures, but none of them possessed any profound or miraculous abilities.

Generally speaking, the dharma treasures of martial artists only had two characteristics.

One was being hard enough, and the other was having high enough lethal power.

They couldn't use any other flashy, complicated functions even if given them.

Although Zhang Lan's Heavenly Cycle Sword was only one three-hundred-and-sixtieth of the complete set, even this fraction was already a Rank 8 dharma treasure. At this moment, he had drawn his blade. The longsword left its sheath with a long, ringing hum. Thousands of streams of sword qi transformed into rainbows, descending abundantly from the sky. In an instant, the earth cracked open, and everything was annihilated!

The Heavenly Cycle Sword was a representative work of such dharma treasures. Aside from automatically returning to its place after its wielder's death, all its other functions were enhanced in this exact aspect.

Hard enough—hard enough to withstand high-intensity combat.

Sharp enough—sharp enough to break through the defenses of most Rank 8s.

Thus, it was enough!

Zhang Dailu, meanwhile, had activated the gold token.

A profound qi surged from the gold token. This qi attached to his body, making him look like a man of gold. He was incredibly hard, clinking and clanging as he walked. Even when his physical body resisted the sword, the sound produced wasn't a dull thud, but the crisp ringing of metal striking metal.

It was just like a body of vajra steel.

The two martial artists traded blows. Zhang Lan was a swordsman, and actually followed a path of technique and sharpness. Yet for some reason, his blade edge actually could not break through the body of vajra steel!

That vajra steel body seemed to be blessed by some powerful expert. Even with Zhang Lan wielding a sharp blade coupled with his Rank 8 internal qi, it remained like a turtle shell—completely unscathed.

Clanging sounds constantly echoed across the battlefield, like the striking of a bell.

However, Zhang Dailu could do nothing to Zhang Lan either.

Zhang Lan moved his sword nimbly, leaving no traces and remaining elusive and unpredictable. Whether it was fists or kicks, Zhang Dailu couldn't touch the other party in the slightest. Sometimes, even if all his attacks landed together, the other party would dodge them with a bizarrely agile movement technique that the naked eye could hardly perceive... It was as if he could teleport.

The impression it gave was that he was just hitting air.

Exactly, like hitting air—unable to make any contact at all.

Zhang Lan completely suppressed Zhang Dailu. Zhang Dailu had no power to fight back whatsoever; relying on his vajra steel body, he could only stand in place and get viciously beaten like an iron turtle.

But unfortunately, his turtle shell was shockingly hard. Aside from the surrounding environment being completely destroyed, his body had suffered no damage at all.

By the time Li Qi arrived, he hadn't expected Zhang Dailu to actually be temporarily evenly matched with Zhang Lan.

Zhang Dailu was only Rank 9!

On what grounds could he fight Zhang Lan to a standstill?

Just then, Zhang Dailu suddenly spoke, "A swordmaster from the Righteous Sword Palace? Although the cultivation methods of your Righteous Sword Palace are swift and fierce, they are not suited to deal with my set of skills. You might as well stop. Even if you are Rank 8, you cannot do anything to me."

Hearing this, Zhang Lan also stopped. One could see his blade continuously trembling, and the hand holding the sword was somewhat bruised purple from the recoil forces.

Zhang Lan stared at his opponent. "What exactly is on your body? Even if you have reached the absolute limit of Rank 9, only half a step away from Rank 8, without Martial Dao divine will, there is a world of difference between us. Logically speaking, it should be impossible for you to block me."

This matter completely exceeded Zhang Lan's understanding.

Zhang Lan had never imagined that a Rank 9 could forcibly withstand his fierce assault head-on.

Previously, even that Rank 8 giant ape had been slashed by his sword until it couldn't stand, and at that time, he hadn't even struck with full force.

But now, he had clearly unleashed his full strength, yet he couldn't break through that vajra steel body.

It was truly bizarre.

But at this moment, Li Qi finally arrived.

Seeing this, Zhang Lan hurled his longsword.

The flying longsword directly knocked Zhang Dailu away, smashing him deep into a distant mountain. The sword bounced back and was caught by him again.

Then, he turned to look at Li Qi. "Brother Li, don't go over there. This person's strength is mediocre, but he's as tough as a turtle. I will trap him here, and you lead the White Rhino Army away. With me here, he won't be able to leave by even half a step."

However, Li Qi replied, "Brother Zhang, take me closer for a look."

"Eh?" Zhang Lan was stunned.

"Let me see what that turtle shell of his is. Maybe there's a way to break it," Li Qi said.

"Fair enough. Then follow me, and I'll beat him up again!" Zhang Lan replied.

Then, the swordsman lunged forward again!

Zhang Dailu had just crawled out of the ground when a sword smashed into his head.

Qi currents surged and cloud waves billowed. His offensive power could shatter mountains and split seas! Unstoppable force erupted with a boom. He was clearly using a sword, but in Zhang Lan's hands, it was as if he were wielding a spiked club. He hammered Zhang Dailu's head like driving a stake into the ground, smashing him back into the earth. The surrounding ground quaked, forming a crater-like terrain.

Li Qi's eyelids twitched at the sight.

_This is a Rank 8 martial artist?_

If he were that stake, he would have been pounded into minced meat from this single strike, ready to be made into meat patties with some onions and pepper.

Yet, Zhang Dailu was still completely unharmed.

He was merely driven into the ground again, temporarily trapped.

"Brother Zhang! Take it easy, you're making too much of a commotion, I can't see the details clearly!" Li Qi roared from the outside.

"So troublesome..." Zhang Lan grumbled, and then hoisted Zhang Dailu out of the ground again.

Zhang Dailu was pulled up like a radish. He swung a fist, trying to counterattack, but Zhang Lan caught it directly, grabbed his head, and smashed it into the ground.

He smashed it several times in a row.

As Zhang Lan smashed, he asked, "You piece of trash, you rely on this iron turtle shell to avoid getting beaten, but do you really think you are my match? I might not be able to hurt you, but with me here, you can't go anywhere else! Can't do anything to you? Does this count as a way?"

Setting aside the interaction between the martial artists here, Li Qi was observing from a distance.

That golden body: auspicious colors enveloped golden skin, red clouds cloaked the top, and a seven-foot halo surged around it. Although he was beaten until he was covered in dirt, he remained as steady as Mount Tai, completely unbreakable.

Li Qi felt like he had seen this somewhere before. He tried hard to recall while starting to flip through his books.

On the other side, Zhang Dailu burst into loud laughter, "A dignified Rank 8 like you can do absolutely nothing to me! How humiliating!"

Furious, Zhang Lan grabbed the golden body's arm and slammed him against the ground like he was swinging sacks of rice. As he slammed him, he demanded, "You are being pressed into the dirt by me to mop the floor, yet you call me humiliated?!"

Zhang Dailu simply said, "You are panting from exhaustion, yet I am completely unscathed, and I don't even feel pain. Who do you think is more humiliated? Besides, why do you think I am not panicking in the slightest? I am waiting for you to die. What are you waiting for?"

Zhang Lan laughed out of extreme anger. Grabbing the man by his hair, he hoisted him up. "Waiting for me to die? With just you?"
