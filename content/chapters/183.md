# Chapter 183: The Envoy

Below Tiger Roar Cliff.

Inside what used to be Lord Huangshan's lair.

Li Zhufang was shivering violently.

"Ben... Benefactor, you want me to be the mountain... mountain god?" Li Zhufang stammered, her entire body trembling so hard she couldn't even stand steady.

She never could have imagined that bringing her Benefactor to see Lord Huangshan would result in... Lord Huangshan dying.

This fierce mountain tiger who intimidated an area of a hundred miles had died just like that, without a sound.

To Li Zhufang, Lord Huangshan was an unshakable god, yet now the god's head was placed right in front of her.

"Who said I was making you the mountain god? Even if I let you be one, you don't have the ability. You need to be at least at Rank 9 of the Divine Dao," Li Qi chuckled. "Don't worry, I'm just telling you to take these golden body fragments. They will be convenient for your future cultivation."

Li Qi continued, "Take these things as your reward. If you walk the Divine Dao, you can use them directly. If you walk other paths, you can break them down and exchange them for other resources. However, you must promise me one thing."

Hearing this, the little girl hurriedly knelt down again. "Please speak, Benefactor. I don't need these rewards. Whatever your orders, Zhufang will definitely do her utmost! I wouldn't dare slack off in the slightest."

"To take as much reward as the work done—this is the righteous path. You need not say more, just accept it." Li Qi handed the golden body fragments over to the pure-hearted little girl, then pointed at Lord Huangshan's tiger head and said to her,

"This skull suppresses the remnant soul of Lord Huangshan. With this here, he is not considered dead. Leave it here, and guard it day and night to create the illusion that he is still alive. It won't take too long, just a month will do. After that, destroy this item and let him return to the cycle of reincarnation."

Suppressing a remnant soul was a method to prevent someone from ever being reincarnated. If it wasn't absolutely necessary, Li Qi would not use this method. But there was no other choice right now, so he found an overseer. After a month, when the dust settled, he could let him re-enter the cycle of reincarnation.

"Next, this is the core of the formation disk here. I have destroyed the part that extracts your essence qi, but the landscape grand array itself remains. I don't have the ability to sever it, nor is there any need to stop the landscape grand array. After all, this thing is used to defend against fiends. If it is destroyed, even more people will die."

"Therefore, you must guard it carefully and not let outsiders come into contact with it. Do you understand?"

After giving these instructions, Li Qi looked at Li Zhufang, waiting for her reply.

Li Zhufang quickly kowtowed. "Zhufang will definitely do her utmost and will absolutely not dare to slack off in the slightest!"

Li Qi sighed. "I have no trustworthy people here, so I can only entrust this to you. I still have important matters to attend to and must leave for now. You stay at Tiger Roar Cliff. I have already set up a restriction using the bone pile outside, imitating Lord Huangshan's spatial technique. As long as you don't make a fuss, a month should pass quickly. By the way, if a clear moon appears later, remember to immediately go and pay your respects. It won't lack benefits for you."

After clarifying a few more details, Li Qi turned and left, scaling the cliff and leaving only Li Zhufang behind.

This butterfly and him were merely passing acquaintances. After parting here, it was likely they would never meet again.

But it did not matter.

Along his journey, there had been too many people and things to bid farewell to. Li Qi was long accustomed to it.

The long road was endless. On the path of the Dao, there weren't many people or things that could accompany him forever.

Thinking this, Li Qi returned to Old Ma's side.

At this moment, the two hours were just about up, and Old Ma was mostly rested.

And so, the man and the horse set off once more.

---

By the time the sun was setting in the west, Li Qi had finally hurried back to the White Rhino Army.

This journey of over forty hours had finally come to an end.

The White Rhino Army had also led the commoners a certain distance, but they hadn't gone far. In just two days, they had traveled about a hundred miles, making it easy to find them with a casual search from the sky.

Not to mention that Old Ma possessed the ability to track, allowing him to locate Shen Shuibi.

After all, an old horse knowing the way was not just an idiom; horses inherently possessed the ability to discern paths.

Returning here smoothly, Li Qi had barely arrived when he sensed that the atmosphere was somewhat off.

Everyone in the White Rhino Army was tense, while the commoners were extremely joyful, with smiles plastered on everyone's faces.

He landed on the ground riding Old Ma, and someone from the White Rhino Army immediately came up to greet him.

"Mr. Li, you have returned? Was it you who brought the envoy?" a soldier approached and asked.

"Envoy? What envoy?" Li Qi frowned.

The officer who approached said, "Two hours ago, an envoy from Vast Yang City arrived holding the Minister of State's token, demanding to see our current commander and leaders. He has been in the tent for two hours now, but there's been no news. Mr. Li, would you like to go in and take a look?"

"Two hours? Alright, I will go take a look now. Oh, by the way, here is the food, take it... Wait, forget it, do not take it. I will handle it later." Li Qi had originally prepared to hand over the food, but after thinking about it, he decided against it.

People from Vast Yang City had come. Taking out the food at this moment would not be a good thing.

If the populace was led to believe that the food was brought by Vast Yang City, conflicts would likely arise later.

Pondering these matters, Li Qi let Old Ma rest and quickly walked toward the tent in the distance, which served as the temporary command post.

As he walked over, Li Qi could sense that the commoners seemed very happy. He heard quite a few voices rejoicing that someone from the government had finally arrived, figuring they wouldn't have to worry anymore.

The more he listened, the more amiss his heart felt.

The people from the government were not preparing to save lives, but rather preparing for a blood sacrifice.

Li Qi could tell with his toes that the envoy's intentions were not pure.

But the problem was, with the commoners trusting the government envoy so much, what did that signify?

It signified that the government usually had credibility and was trusted by the people.

So what exactly had happened in the middle of all this to make them actually choose to blood sacrifice these civilians?

However, Li Qi didn't completely believe this point either. Because regarding the blood sacrifice, although there was plenty of circumstantial evidence proving that the government didn't care about these soldiers and civilians...

The thing was, the claim of a blood sacrifice was something Li Qi had only heard from Wang Boyan. In Li Qi's own understanding, this was entirely Wang Boyan's one-sided story. There was no second piece of conclusive evidence, only some circumstantial proof.

Therefore, in reality, whether it was Wang Boyan or the Vast Yang government, Li Qi trusted neither.

He had his own plans.

But those were not to be shared with outsiders.

Reaching the vicinity of the tent, Li Qi hadn't seen the envoy yet, but he did spot a drowsy rabbit sunbathing.

Not Miss Shen, but a rabbit.

Furthermore, this rabbit was wearing clothes.

A perfectly fine rabbit wearing miniature clothing—the kind of four-legged outfit with a skirt meant for a dog. Someone had even embroidered flowers on it. He didn't know who was responsible for this.

This grayish-white rabbit had her ears drooping down, acting like a sleep mask covering her eyes. She lay sprawled out on a rock, sunning her rear. She was very conspicuous, yet also seemed perfectly normal; regardless, no one was paying attention to her.

As if everything had nothing to do with her, Shen Shuibi just lay there, taking a nap.

However, all this truly had nothing to do with her. Moreover, her current appearance was actually due to Li Qi's instructions.

Through his mountain-and-river transmission earlier, Li Qi had already sent a message back, and one of his requests was hoping Shen Shuibi would revert to her true form and conceal her aura.

What Li Qi hadn't expected was that after reverting to her true form, she would still wear clothes...

Rabbit fur, in her eyes, was probably just like skin—it counted as being naked.

It seemed that when he ruffled her fur last time, in her view, it was equivalent to groping her behind.

That was truly a severe consequence. He had managed to keep his life by a sheer stroke of luck...

If someone she wasn't familiar with had done that, they would probably be eaten by her like a carrot, wouldn't they?

Munched into pieces with a crunch-crunch-crunch.

Or perhaps she would just reach out and slap them to death.

He needed to be more careful in the future.

Deciding not to disturb the napping Shen Shuibi, Li Qi walked forward and lifted the tent flap.

Inside the tent, he saw that the lieutenant general currently in charge of the White Rhino Army, as well as Zhang Lan, were both present.

While these two were present, beside them stood an elderly man whom Li Qi did not recognize.

The elderly man was hale and hearty, covered in bulging muscles. Despite having white eyebrows, a white beard, and white hair, his knotted muscles made him look even more robust than the burly soldiers outside, striking an imposing and terrifying figure at first glance.

Even though he had the appearance of white hair and a youthful face, and wore the loose robes typical of an elder, such robes would normally drape loosely. On him, however, they bulged conspicuously, seemingly stretched to the limit by his bulk.

The moment Li Qi entered, the three people in the tent all looked over at him.

The muscular old man flew into a rage. Glaring at Li Qi, he rudely barked, "Who is this?! We superiors are discussing matters, yet you dare to barge in directly without first seeking an audience?! Go down and receive a hundred military canes yourself!"

Li Qi was still baffled, while the White Rhino Army's lieutenant general hurriedly stood up and cupped his hands. "High Envoy, please do not be offended! This man is not a subordinate of the White Rhino Army, but a righteous hero who came to our aid. He is that Li Qi we mentioned earlier."

Li Qi frowned. "I do not know why the envoy is so irritable. Could it be an excess of Yang qi?"

"You are Li Qi?" The burly old man stood up.

"Indeed. May I know the envoy's name? How did we manage to miss each other on the road? I just came from Vast Yang City as well," Li Qi said with a gentle smile.

However, no matter how one looked at it, his smile didn't reach his eyes.

Zhang Lan didn't speak. He just silently stood up, his hand resting on his sword box.

He could see what was happening.

There seemed to be some bad blood between this envoy and Li Qi.

But they clearly didn't recognize each other. It was obvious the envoy didn't know Li Qi by face, yet he knew the name 'Li Qi'. This indicated that Li Qi must have done something in Vast Yang City that reached the ears of this government envoy.

Whatever Li Qi had done must have displeased the government immensely; otherwise, the atmosphere wouldn't be this tense.

And in Zhang Lan's eyes, Li Qi was clearly not the type of bad person who stirred up trouble, nor would he do anything against nature and reason.

Then why would the government treat him with this attitude?

The underlying reasons were probably worth pondering over.

Thus, Zhang Lan remained silent, just quietly watching to see how the situation would develop.

"This old man is named Zhang Dailu, the ancestor of the Zhang family of Vast Yang. You must have heard my name," the burly old man lifted his chin and proudly stated to Li Qi.

Li Qi had never heard the name Zhang Dailu.

But he knew of the Zhang family of Vast Yang.

"I have long looked forward to meeting you. It is just that I do not know why Senior has come here?" Li Qi asked, cupping his hands.

The White Rhino Army's lieutenant general glanced between the two. He too could tell that there was a conflict between them.

However, stepping in to mediate without knowing the cause of the issue was taboo. So he also kept his mouth shut, choosing instead to stand aside and listen to their exchange, ready to interject at any moment to prevent the conflict from escalating verbally.

This was the safest approach.

Zhang Dailu flexed his pectoral muscles, raised his chin, and looked at Li Qi. "Do you truly not know why I have come? The one who openly disobeyed orders on behalf of the White Rhino Army is you, isn't it? As for what you did after arriving in Vast Yang City, are you not aware of it yourself? Disguising yourself as an intelligence envoy, defrauding military rations, and disobeying orders on behalf of the White Rhino Army. You accomplished all of this with just a single day in Vast Yang City. You are quite something."

After finishing, he turned to the lieutenant general and raised his voice. "Acting Commander of the White Rhino Army! Seeing this token is equivalent to seeing an edict from the Minister of State. Quickly help me capture this thief and execute him on the spot! Then, return with me to Vast Yang City!"

As soon as these words were spoken, let alone Zhang Lan and the lieutenant general beside them, even Li Qi was shocked.

Li Qi had wondered what the other party would say, but he hadn't expected him to immediately launch an attack right from the start!?

They hadn't even fully introduced themselves yet!

The lieutenant general was also stunned silly. He quickly rushed forward. "High Envoy, there must be some misunderstanding here! Mr. Li saved our entire army and even harbored—"

But before the lieutenant general could finish, Zhang Dailu raised his hand, displaying a golden token, and shouted sternly, "Acting Commander, do you intend to disobey orders?!"

"This general wouldn't dare! But there must be something fishy going on here. Mr. Li is absolutely not the kind of person who could do such things. High Envoy, please do not rush to pass judgment. Acting on impulse will only harm a loyal and righteous man!" The lieutenant general knelt on one knee, yet he refused to accept the order.

The entire White Rhino Army had seen with their own eyes Li Qi and Zhang Lan stepping forward to help at the most critical moment, forcibly resisting two Rank 8 experts and saving a hundred thousand soldiers and civilians.

Furthermore, Li Qi had specially set up an altar to expiate the souls of their deceased brothers, dispelled the evil spirits of those fiends, and cast spells to cure the sickly qi of a hundred thousand people. Moreover, he asked for no reward, having done every good deed imaginable.

How could such a righteous hero commit the acts mentioned above?

There had to be something amiss.

Seeing the White Rhino Army's lieutenant general actually disobeying orders, the corners of Zhang Dailu's eyes twitched, and he was just about to say something.

However, Li Qi chimed in at this moment.

"Is that so? What I heard seems to be quite different from the envoy's claims."

Li Qi took a step back before beginning to speak. "I made a trip to Vast Yang City, yet I only saw countless refugees with no one to care for them."

"I only saw that the granaries of wealthy households like the Zhang family of Vast Yang were fully stocked, while the military ration storehouses were completely empty."

"I only saw that the various armies had received no reinforcements, nearly causing the entire army to be slaughtered by fiends."

"I only saw a bunch of self-serving cowards! When the fiends attacked, you didn't think to resist. You only wanted to retreat to the rear and construct a landscape grand array, vainly attempting to perform a blood sacrifice with the commoners to ensure your own safety!"

"And now you want to use those same excuses to bluff real men of blood and courage, forcing them into acts of gross ingratitude."

"Is this the conduct of your Vast Yang government?! Where is your shame?!" Li Qi shouted loudly!

Zhang Dailu was consumed with furious rage. "Shut up!"

Li Qi ignored him entirely. Turning his head to Zhang Lan, he calmly asked, "Brother Zhang, do you dare to kill a government official?"

Zhang Lan threw his head back and laughed loudly. "Hahaha! What is there to fear?!"
