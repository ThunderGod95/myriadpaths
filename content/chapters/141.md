# Chapter 141: Young Master

_Clap, clap, clap._

Three slow claps rang out as a young nobleman in a white-and-gold long gown walked toward them.

The gown was purely white, embroidered with golden threads that formed intertwining patterns. It seemed to harbor some profound mystery within, giving him an overall appearance of effortless elegance and commanding presence.

Beside the young nobleman walked a maidservant with a cold, detached expression. She stayed close by his side, never leaving it by even a single step, and held a beautiful jade flute. The previous flute melody must have come from her.

"Impressive methods, and what quick reflexes." The young nobleman tapped a folding fan against his left hand, praising Li Qi's swift reaction.

Li Qi collected himself, looked at the man, and then glanced down at his own clothes.

_Wow, I'm dressed like such a country bumpkin. I don't look nearly as dashing as he does._

_Should I get myself a new outfit? But I don't have any money..._

Li Qi thought to himself.

As for feeling tense or fearing the sudden appearance of an enemy...

He felt none of that at all. There was simply nothing to be afraid of; these two were merely at Rank 9.

It wasn't that Li Qi was overly arrogant. It was just that as an orthodox successor of Shaman God Mountain, fighting ordinary Rank 9 cultivators would probably only require one hand.

"Hahaha! To think you're actually dwelling on such things at a time like this. You have quite the nerve. I like that! Just for that reaction, I shall reward you!" The young nobleman suddenly spoke, laughing heartily before tossing a precious orb to Li Qi.

"It contains ten wisps of pure fire qi. Take it and go buy yourself a new set of clothes!" he said condescendingly.

Li Qi reached out and caught the orb.

It wasn't that he intended to accept the gift, but rather that this was his first time seeing the 'qi' used as currency. He was extremely curious about what it was, so he caught it to take a closer look.

The moment the orb touched his hand, he felt it.

Purity. Aside from absolute purity, he felt nothing else.

It felt as if dozens of wisps of naturally occurring fire qi had been tempered and compressed into a single strand. It was virtually devoid of impurities or personal distinctiveness. It was genuinely unadulterated fire qi, an exceptional material for anyone who needed it.

It required no refinement at all and could be used immediately. It would even be highly effective if simply detonated as a bomb.

Furthermore, the production process had to be tedious. Li Qi pondered for a moment; if he were to condense this kind of pure fire qi himself, it would take at least three or four days of slow tempering just to produce a single wisp.

Just these ten wisps would require him to do nothing but condense and temper fire qi every day for an entire month.

It was portable, easily divisible, possessed universal value, had a certain barrier to production, and was consumed in massive quantities. No wonder it could serve as currency.

Only... this also meant that everyone possessed the ability to print money. He wondered how this market economy was regulated. Without official credit backing it, wouldn't the value of this qi fluctuate wildly?

Or... was the regulation of qi actually backed by someone?

In the Earth Oil World, the production of qi depended on the strength of the local Heavenly Dao. If he understood it that way... then perhaps this qi, while seemingly condensed by cultivators themselves, was actually fundamentally produced by the Heavenly Dao? Cultivators merely processed the raw materials further.

An absurd thought bloomed in Li Qi's mind, and he looked up at the firmament.

_Is the Heavenly Dao of All Under Heaven regulating the economy?_

It took a while to explain, but in reality, these thoughts merely flashed through his mind in less than half a second.

"I cannot accept a reward I haven't earned. You should take this back, young master." Li Qi cupped his hands, preparing to toss the orb back.

However, the young nobleman stopped him. "Ah, you entertained me, so you have earned it. Even prostitutes, actors, servants, and concubines receive tips. Why shouldn't you?"

Hearing this, Li Qi frowned but tossed the orb back anyway.

He was certainly short on money, but he absolutely refused to take _this_ money.

The Dao of Righteousness did not rejoice in ill-gotten wealth.

The Dao of Justice did not permit one to suffer humiliation and self-degradation.

To exchange his dignity for a windfall was something Li Qi would never do.

The other party didn't catch it either, simply letting the highly valuable orb fall to the ground.

By now, the crowd around them had dispersed. Those who wanted to watch the spectacle had retreated to a distance; otherwise, they risked getting caught in the crossfire and covered in dirt before seeing any action.

Anyone could tell that a conflict had brewed and a fight was likely about to break out.

The young nobleman chuckled softly. "You seem to look down on me? A mere savage, yet you take offense to being compared to prostitutes and servants? Hmm, but it seems you aren't an ordinary savage either. The first time you encounter qi, your mind actually conjures up delusions about the Heavenly Dao's economy. Your body may be that of a lowly ant, but your ambitions are certainly grand."

Hearing this, Li Qi furrowed his brow.

_Why does it seem like he can read my surface-level thoughts?_

_I thought he was just good at reading expressions, but that doesn't seem to be the case._

_He shouldn't be able to read my memories, but what method is he using to observe me?_

Li Qi wondered inwardly.

"There is no need to guess. How could a savage like you possibly comprehend my methods? Judging by your appearance, you must have just come from the countryside, right?" the young nobleman said with a smile.

Piaoxu, however, took stock of the situation, gritted her teeth, and stepped forward on her own accord. "Young Master..."

"Scram," the young nobleman said, waving his fan.

The disdain in his eyes practically spilled over. Even Li Qi could read the obvious message in his gaze: _A lowly creature like you dares to speak to me?_

Yet, with just that single word, Piaoxu actually trembled all over and uncontrollably fell to her knees, not daring to utter another sound. Even her ears and tail popped out, her entire being beginning to revert back toward her beastly form.

With merely a glance, she was forced back into her original shape!

Right at that moment, Li Qi took a few steps forward and shielded Piaoxu behind him.

Immediately after, he adjusted his vital energy and blood, stimulating his heart.

Then, Li Qi said, "Combined with the flute melody from earlier, this is a dao rhythm related to the five tones, isn't it?"

Upon hearing this, the young nobleman's gaze instantly sharpened. He snapped his folding fan open and held it across his face. "You know quite a bit for a savage. To think you even recognize the existence of a dao rhythm. What is your background?"

"Music is the voice of the heart. From the looks of it, you must cultivate a cultivation method based on the five tones. Coupled with a wisp of dao rhythm, you can eavesdrop on people's inner voices and even attack them invisibly with sound, correct?" Li Qi stated while shielding Piaoxu.

He then continued, "I have no grand background; I'm merely passing through. By the way, how about now? I am using my heart as the drum skin and my blood and qi as the drumsticks. The percussive beats scramble the qi, roaring to set it in motion. Can you still hear my thoughts?"

Having said that, Li Qi watched the man, his hands ready to strike.

Yes, just like Li Qi and Bo Yue, this person also possessed a wisp of dao rhythm.

Bo Yue's dao rhythm was the devil seed, while Li Qi's was his familiar progress bar. This man's dao rhythm was likely the ability to hear inner voices.

Except... Li Qi subconsciously felt that the other party's dao rhythm was vastly inferior to his own and Bo Yue's.

It seemed to be an innate intuition.

_Is this how possessors of an innate dao rhythm feel? Does he feel the same way?_

_No, he probably doesn't. Otherwise, he wouldn't be acting so arrogantly._

Upon hearing Li Qi's words, the young nobleman's ears twitched.

Then, dropping his previous frivolity, he cupped his hands. "You savage... no, you must have a master. You do have some genuine skill. I underestimated you earlier. As expected of someone who could resist Elder Si's curse. Not bad, not bad at all. I just wonder, brother, why did you intervene and stop me?"

"It's nothing, just righting an injustice I came across. I should be asking you, young master, why did you try to take this person's life? What enormous grudge do you bear?" Li Qi asked.

Just as they were conversing.

Three enforcers wearing matching black long gowns and armbands descended from the sky. "You two, this is a market, a crowded area. Do not clash here. If there is a dispute—"

However, the young nobleman impatiently waved his fan and called out a single name: "Lord Yan."

Instantly, a flash of fire erupted!

A massive humanoid creature formed entirely of multicolored flames suddenly stood up, resembling a featureless flame giant!

The absolute pinnacle of flames! Heatwaves swept across the surrounding area, forming miniature sand twisters. Accompanied by a provocative, thunderous roar, the billowing heatwaves surged forward. The searing temperatures pressed closer step by step, descending upon the three enforcers like the karmic fire of the Nether Prison!

Within the blazing inferno, all matter was burned to ash and red molten sludge. The intense heat caused everything to outright collapse and melt together in violent collisions and entanglements.

In the path of the faceless flame giant's advance, everything met with total destruction.

An utterly unreasonable, absolute destruction!

The three enforcers, along with a quarter of the market, were completely incinerated, reduced to nothing but a wisp of smoke.

A quarter of the market—that was nearly ten square li of land!

The scorching liquid churned like mud across the ground, forming a massive magma lake. Sweltering air swirled around it, and the surroundings reeked heavily of sulfur and ash.

Li Qi's pupils shrank. He immediately circulated his qi, readying himself to retreat.

Rank 8! Such destructive power could only belong to a Rank 8!

The surrounding market was filled with the wails of countless people. The entity known as Lord Yan had controlled his power to avoid the civilians. Although some were injured, aside from the three enforcers, no one had died.

"The injured, and those whose goods were damaged, will be compensated tomorrow! The business delayed today will also be reimbursed! Now, all of you, scram! I don't want to see a single soul in this market other than us!" the young nobleman shouted, raising his voice.

The sheer presence of a Rank 8 expert had already terrified the onlookers out of their wits. Countless people deployed whatever methods they had to flee for their lives. For a moment, various escape lights flashed wildly, and soon, not a single person was left in the entire market.

"There, now it's just us. Shall we find a place to sit? To know of and recognize the existence of dao rhythms... Brother, may I ask for your esteemed name?" The young nobleman's demeanor shifted as he spoke to Li Qi with a beaming smile.

Li Qi frowned. He naturally knew that the man had burned down the market, promised compensation, shouted, and driven everyone away purely to put on a show for him.

This person... was truly arrogant, childish, and haughty.

But he was also undeniably formidable.

At that moment, Shen Shuibi suddenly stepped up beside Li Qi and said to him, "The one in the sky, I can handle him."

As she spoke, she pointed a finger at the flame giant known as Lord Yan.

Because she hadn't lowered her voice, everyone present heard her clearly.

"A mere Rank 9, ignorant of the immensity of heaven and earth." The flame giant, Lord Yan, snorted coldly. He reached out a hand, preparing to teach her a lesson.

However, Shen Shuibi likewise extended a hand.

With a pale, slender finger, she tapped lightly.

Supreme Yin qi surged forth, and the fire qi in the air was instantly reflected right back!

As it was reflected, a bright moon unfurled.

"The moon is formed by reflecting the sun; the bright moon serves as a Mirror." Shen Shuibi plucked the air with her ten fingers, manifesting a cold, clear moon.

If the flame giant was like a furiously burning sun, then Shen Shuibi had used her own pure qi of the Supreme Yin to craft a moon.

Both the light and the heat were deflected away.

Nevertheless, even so, the moon still had to endure a portion of the light and heat.

Just a second later, that illusory moon began to tremble.

"Decent methods, but your power is too weak." Lord Yan's deep voice boomed, and he ramped up his output.

The greatest of skills still required power to support them. If the power backing this bright moon were equal to his own, Lord Yan knew he wouldn't be able to break through it; her technique was indeed uniquely brilliant.

But the gap between Rank 9 and Rank 8 was well over a hundredfold!

Deflecting a thousand pounds with four ounces still had its limits. With such feeble power and a body that would shatter at a touch, what could she possibly use to stop him? Love?

As Lord Yan increased his power, the surrounding flames grew even grander, seemingly threatening to shake the heavens and blot out the sun.

Li Qi grabbed Piaoxu, who had reverted to her true form, with one hand and rapidly retreated.

Even the young nobleman and his maidservant began to back away.

When a Rank 8 expert took action, the aftershocks were simply unbearable for anyone at Rank 9.

Although Shen Shuibi was currently also at Rank 9, Li Qi knew her true background, so he wasn't worried in the slightest.

After the young nobleman had withdrawn, fire qi steamed from Lord Yan's body, his calamitous clouds shrouding the sky! Dense, unparalleled flames surrounded them endlessly, wave after wave mercilessly battering against the moon Shen Shuibi held aloft.

That luminous, bright moon was rapidly shattered.

Lord Yan's hand instantly slammed down. Compared to the flame giant's massive frame, it looked as though he were swatting a mosquito.

However, at this exact moment.

Hidden within the bright moon was the small cauldron Shen Shuibi had previously used to refine Outer Alchemy and snacks.

Lord Yan's strike landed squarely upon the cauldron, without the slightest deviation.

Shen Shuibi formed a hand seal, and a terrifying suction force abruptly erupted from the cauldron!

"Hmm?!" Lord Yan cried out in bewildered alarm, attempting to pull his hand away!

But the cauldron's suction was overwhelmingly massive. The flame giant's body was composed entirely of the qi of blazing fire, making it completely impossible to break free!

Lord Yan immediately executed a spell art, and the worldly fires of men reappeared.

The worldly fires of men were ethereal and intangible, capable of turning the body into a state of nothingness—an absolute perfect method for escaping restraints.

Yet, Shen Shuibi formed another seal, and a second bright moon hung high in the air.

The moon shed its pure radiance, illuminating the mortal realm for millions of years.

The worldly fires of men were ethereal and intangible, but beneath the bright moon, they were exposed like a fire in the night, incomparably clear and distinct.

Having lost two exchanges in a row, Lord Yan had no more tricks up his sleeve. His entire body was dragged toward the cauldron, and his entire left arm was sucked straight inside!
