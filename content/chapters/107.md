# Chapter 107: Enduring

Black clouds blanketed the sky.

Li Qi merely glanced at it, let out a muffled groan, and immediately dropped to his knees.

He did not kneel by choice; he had instantly suffered a massive mental impact. Just a single look, and it felt as if he had been struck by a warhammer. He collapsed straight down, a stream of blood pouring from his nose.

He felt as if he had plummeted into a pit teeming with venomous snakes, scorpions, and centipedes, screaming and struggling madly within.

Then, it was as if he were thrown into a scorching brazier. His body sizzled over the charcoal, emitting the horrific sound of searing flesh, the agony unbearable.

A lake of wine burning with karmic fire.

Forests of human flesh were hung with dripping, oily corpses.

Pyramids built from countless severed heads, shrouded in black mist.

Demons with fetid breath were subjecting countless humans to horrific tortures—gouging out eyes, branding hands, mincing corpses, feeding them to venomous insect pits, burning them on copper pillars, and carving out their hearts.

Some demons did not even bother with torture, directly tearing into living victims amidst the dreadful, wailing winds and the gloom of eternal night.

A landscape of pure hell.

Demon qi dissipated and danced in madness.

Sadistic abuse indulged to the absolute limit.

All of this was exactly what that wicked shadow looming over the horizon represented.

Just looking at it caused Li Qi so much pain and despair that his mind was on the verge of collapsing. It was a great demon.

Grotesque and terrifying, steeped in sin and karma.

Endless greed, bottomless lust, the intertwining consequences of good and evil karma, every perverted and inverted ideology, every trace of avarice, ignorance, and wrath—all were displayed right here!

However gorgeous Lady Luofu's precious canopy tower was, that was exactly how terrifying this wicked shadow blanketing the sky proved to be!

"Li Qi! Don't look!" Shen Shuibi immediately covered Li Qi's eyes while using her own body to block the black qi.

Her Unsullied Body exerted its power at this very moment. An invisible barrier seemed to surround her, holding the encroaching wicked qi at bay and forming a pristine domain.

After his eyes were covered and he briefly regained consciousness, Li Qi drew his blade. Without a shred of hesitation, he plunged the knife straight through his own palm.

The intense pain of piercing his palm forcibly dragged him out of the hallucinations.

Cold sweat poured down his skin, soaking his body. His clothes were completely drenched, as if he had just been fished out of a river. The metallic stench of rust constantly wafted from his mouth; his nose bled without end, and his eye sockets were burning red.

And all of this was simply because he had taken a single glance at that black cloud.

That wicked shadow overwhelming the sky.

"What... what is that thing?" Li Qi asked through gritted teeth.

He bit down so hard that his gums bled. The masseter muscles on his face twitched ceaselessly, revealing just how much force he was exerting.

"It's a Rank 6 demon. Don't look," Shen Shuibi said, moving to stand directly in front of him to prevent him from staring at it again.

Li Qi glanced to the side, only to realize that Old Ma had already collapsed. The horse's entire body was violently convulsing, foaming at the mouth, completely unable to move.

Rank 6.

Was this what a true, complete Rank 6 was supposed to look like?

It made sense. When he had spoken with Jing Yan about the Song Kingdom previously, she had mentioned that the mountain gods of the Five Great Mountains in the Song Kingdom were also Rank 6, and their bodies spanned five thousand li.

In that case, what kind of terrifying form did Shen Shuibi possess at her peak?

"I can endure it," Li Qi said, prying Shen Shuibi's hands away from his eyes.

"Don't push yourself! Be careful, or your Dao Heart will be tarnished and demonic thoughts will be planted within you," Shen Shuibi warned as she tried to pull him back. However, she was far too weak right now to hold him, and Li Qi easily broke free.

Li Qi took a deep breath, ran over, and dragged Old Ma into the safety of Shen Shuibi's barrier.

"You... you're alright?" Shen Shuibi asked in astonishment. How was Li Qi no longer affected?

But upon a closer look, she realized Li Qi's face was fluctuating between shades of blue and purple. His features were contorted, and he looked as though he was about to shatter his own teeth. He didn't look unaffected in the slightest.

"Li Qi..." Shen Shuibi reached out, her tone laced with worry.

"I can endure it," Li Qi repeated. He then continued, "Now is not the time to cower. You have sharp senses. Go listen to what's happening ahead, then come back and tell me so we can figure out what to do."

"But if I leave, you guys..." Shen Shuibi hesitated.

"I can endure it!" Li Qi repeated through gritted teeth. "Don't you want to save Lady Luofu?"

Indeed, it wasn't that he was unaffected.

He was simply using every ounce of his strength to suppress the terror. Even though his legs had turned to jelly and his internal organs were boiling so violently he felt like throwing up, he still persisted in doing what needed to be done at this moment.

Hearing the words _Don't you want to save Lady Luofu?_, Shen Shuibi bit her lower lip, her face filled with struggle and hesitation.

But very quickly, she said, "Hold on. I'll be right back."

Shen Shuibi hurried off, rushing forward to scout for information.

The moment she left, the wicked qi that her Unsullied Body had been blocking immediately seeped in.

They were at least a hundred li away from that black cloud, yet just this tiny bit of permeating wicked qi was almost too much for Li Qi to bear, leaving him completely unable to move freely.

Therefore, Li Qi bit the tip of his tongue, using the agonizing stimulus to force himself to stay awake. He roused his inner qi to resist the encroaching corruption.

However, it was to no avail. The wicked qi simply could not be fended off with willpower alone.

_What do I do... what other way is there?_ Li Qi racked his brain, trying to think of a method that would allow him to hold on just a little longer.

This was wicked qi attempting to invade his body. His inner qi could offer a brief resistance, and his vitality could expel some of it, but it still wasn't enough. He needed an even more intense surge of vitality to stave off the invasion.

An even more intense surge of vitality... _Got it._

Fortunately, he had a good memory.

Circulating his inner qi, he began to sing at the top of his lungs!

"Oh, brilliant Seven Luminaries, shining brightly above! Oh, valiant warrior, how heroic, how heroic!"

The song Ah Du had taught him in the past finally took effect.

At the same time, he activated the Ox Strength Art.

His body expanded, his vitality surging. Combined with the song igniting his blood and erupting with exuberant lifeforce, his vitality forcefully drove away the naturally radiating wicked qi!

He even leaned against Old Ma, using his own radiating lifeforce to help the horse fend off the wicked qi.

However, Li Qi knew this wouldn't last long.

Even with ample inner qi, the Ox Strength Art could only be maintained for half an hour. He had to make a decision before then on what their next move should be.

Should they just run for their lives, charge in to find Lady Luofu, or just skirt the edges and observe?

All of this depended on exactly what kind of intelligence Shen Shuibi brought back.

He hoped the rabbit would pull through.

Li Qi waited anxiously, continuously belting out his song.

But suddenly, in the distance, the great demon's wicked shadow and Lady Luofu's precious canopy violently clashed!

A brilliant flash of blue and white light erupted, establishing a domain of supreme clarity! The pure qi ascended while the turbid qi condensed below. The Three Realms and Four Symbols, the merging forms of Yin and Yang, along with the sun, moon, five planets, and the seven stars of the Big Dipper all manifested!

Li Qi saw the beautiful and solemn phantom of a woman manifest above the canopy. She reached out and pointed a finger.

In that instant, the woman's figure commanded the Great Obscurity, projecting her terrifying might across the Three Realms! The resounding chime of an imperial bell echoed forth, terrifying every ghost and demon. Mountains toppled, trees sank into the earth, and a hundred valleys violently boiled over!
