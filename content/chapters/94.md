# Chapter 94: Conflict

All in all, Shen Shuibi knew that serious matters took precedence. She didn't put up a tearful fuss about leaving.

It was just that the rabbit stayed angry for an exceptionally long time this instance.

Li Qi was helpless. He had apologized, but the problem was... how was he supposed to guard against this?

Would any normal person look at a fluffy rabbit and think of _that_?

But Shen Shuibi was not human.

_Sigh_, she acted so normal most of the time that he had forgotten the difference in their species.

Afterwards, Li Qi spoke with the innkeeper. He arranged for Old Ma to rest there, using the prepaid room fee for the next few days to cover Old Ma's food. The innkeeper naturally had no reason to refuse and agreed.

After settling Old Ma, he set off with a sulking, quietly fuming Shen Shuibi.

But she was a rabbit, after all. Though she remained angry during the journey, her timid nature flared up the moment they arrived at the caravan and a crowd of people came forward to greet them. She hurriedly hid behind Li Qi, clutching his clothes tightly and using his body as a shield against their gazes. The remnants of her anger vanished into thin air.

This was the impression Shen Shuibi had always given Li Qi: exceedingly gentle, only ever staying angry for a brief moment.

Although this time, it truly was Li Qi's own fault...

But he couldn't really be blamed...

Who could have anticipated that petting a rabbit would result in an accusation of harassment?

_Forget it. I'll just have to remember this for the future._

Taking Shen Shuibi and a bit of luggage, Li Qi joined the caravan.

After settling Shen Shuibi, Li Qi immediately went to visit the caravan leader—the merchant who had invited him earlier. "We were in a rush when we spoke before, and I forgot to introduce myself. I am Li Qi. May I know how to address you, sir?"

"My surname is Jing, and my given name is Biewen. I am a manager of the Jing family's chamber of commerce. You may simply call me by my name, Warrior Li," Jing Biewen said.

"How could I? You are an elder; how could I address you directly by your name? From now on, I will call you Manager Jing," Li Qi replied.

After exchanging a few more pleasantries, Li Qi eagerly got to the main point.

"Manager Jing, do you know where the caravan's other Rank 9 is? We will be working together for the next few days, so I would like to pay a visit. Can you point me in the right direction?" Li Qi asked politely.

"So that is what Warrior Li is inquiring about. However, that Rank 9 is not an external hire. She is a disciple from the Martial Affairs Hall of the Jing main family. Still, if you are to fight side by side, making contact beforehand is a good idea." Manager Jing nodded as he spoke.

He then began to introduce her to Li Qi. "She is also a Rank 9 of the Martial Dao, a deacon of the main family's Martial Affairs Hall. She is different from those of us in the branch families..."

As Manager Jing explained, Li Qi listened intently.

As it turned out, this chamber of commerce was affiliated with an aristocratic family surnamed Jing.

When speaking of his family, Manager Jing appeared quite proud. The family was overseen by a Rank 7 patriarch, possessed multiple Rank 8 hall masters, and boasted dozens of Rank 9 deacons. The family members had clear divisions of labor, practicing different cultivation methods depending on their specific business. They formed a sizable faction within the Song Kingdom and were considered a major clan there, holding the hereditary title of Viscount, known as 'Viscount Jing of the South'.

Li Qi marveled internally. Aside from the ethereal Shaman God Mountain and the Tang Empire, this was his first time dealing with such a massive faction.

It truly was a major power. A merchant house from the Song Kingdom had managed to extend its business all the way to White Snake Prefecture in the Great Deer Kingdom.

Still, even a mighty dragon struggled to suppress a local snake. Having offended the Assistant Magistrate of Snake Tail County, they still had to flee in a hurry.

After outlining the family background, he began introducing the 'Martial Affairs Hall Deacon'.

The Martial Affairs Hall, as the name implied, was responsible for the Jing family's martial matters and practiced Martial Dao cultivation methods. This particular deacon was a woman named Jing Yan. Her mother had died early, and her father was exceedingly strict, resulting in a withdrawn personality. Aside from practicing martial arts, she had almost no other hobbies.

Therefore, Manager Jing suggested that if Li Qi wanted to build a good relationship to facilitate their future cooperation, he should approach her under the guise of sparring. He explicitly warned against bringing gifts, ensuring he wouldn't be thrown out.

Li Qi listened carefully to all of this, then took his leave, promising to dedicate himself fully to his duties.

Manager Jing cupped his hands, seeing Li Qi off.

Li Qi returned the gesture and walked toward the location Manager Jing had specified.

However, halfway there, he heard Shen Shuibi's voice.

"What are you trying to do!?" Shen Shuibi's panicked cry rang out from nearby.

Li Qi instantly abandoned all his previous plans. Without hesitation, he tore the coarse cloth from his long blade and took a step forward, his internal qi surging! Within two blinks of an eye, moving with the explosive power of a leaping tiger and the agility of a diving falcon, he bounded past the caravan members and vaulted over a wall. In an instant, he crossed dozens of meters, arriving at Shen Shuibi's location.

Though his reaction was fast, the other party had already anticipated it.

_Clang!_

The ring of clashing metal echoed out as a longsword blocked Li Qi's blade directly. Countless sparks flew from the point of impact.

Li Qi distinctly felt a clever, redirecting force transmit through his long blade, nearly causing him to lose his grip on the hilt.

His heart tightened. He knew he had encountered an expert.

This was an unexpected encounter. There was no time for Li Qi to plot a sneak attack; he could only face her head-on.

However, Li Qi knew he wasn't skilled at head-on combat. He wasn't a true martial artist; his Rank 9 cultivation was purely the result of Shen Shuibi's help. Although he had trained diligently, he had only crossed the threshold a little over a month ago. Compared to martial artists who had tempered their skills since childhood, his technique was utterly atrocious.

Yet, precisely because Li Qi wasn't a true martial artist, he possessed other methods.

Just as his opponent's clever force nearly jarred the long blade from his grasp, Li Qi gritted his teeth and activated the military qi within the blade!

A cold gleam instantly flashed across the long blade. White qi, like a flock of birds, resembling fine powder and billowing smoke, coated the edge. Li Qi felt the strength in his previously numb wrist multiply!

It didn't truly multiply, but it was enough to stabilize his grip.

This was still only their first clash; not even the time it takes to blink had passed. But Li Qi had already formulated a tactic. After triggering the military qi, he gripped the blade with both hands and executed a forceful downward cleave, like splitting Mount Hua.

Carp Stream Intent erupted. Like a carp leaping over the dragon gate, an explosive double portion of power surged forth!

The sharp edge of the blade, combined with the sudden, overwhelming momentum and the advantage of his weapon, allowed Li Qi to cleave straight through his opponent's sword, snapping it in two!

With a sharp clatter, the broken sword fell to the ground.

Li Qi used the momentum to sweep Shen Shuibi into his arms, shielding her behind him. Only then did he have the time to survey his surroundings.

Given Shen Shuibi's personality, it was absolutely impossible for her to provoke trouble on her own initiative, nor would she cry out like that for no reason.

Therefore, even if it meant forfeiting this job, he couldn't allow the rabbit to be wronged. He had discarded his polite demeanor and attacked without a second thought.

All around them, many caravan members stared at Li Qi in dumbfounded shock.

_Good heavens, a longsword forged from hundred-refined steel, snapped in half with a single strike?!_
