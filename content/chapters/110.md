# Chapter 110: Method to Break the Deadlock

"What's the situation? What's going on?" Li Qi hurriedly rushed out and brought her back.

In the distance, the three mighty figures were still engaged in their clash of arts. The scene was magnificent, the battlefield stretching over a hundred li. Fortunately, it hadn't reached their gorge yet, so Li Qi remained safe for the time being.

After returning, Shen Shuibi gasped for breath before saying to Li Qi, "Li Qi... Lady Luofu is truly in danger. That isn't Lady Luofu over there, it's Yang Ning!"

"Yang Ning, like me... is Lady Luofu's—" Shen Shuibi started to explain.

Li Qi immediately cut her off. "I know. The sun essence Yang Ning. That demon already introduced her earlier. Get to the point."

"Right. Lady Luofu isn't dead, but she is incredibly weak. I don't know how many years of recuperation she'll need to recover. Right now, that's Yang Ning over there bluffing. It's just that Yang Ning is also severely injured, and she likely isn't a match for those two demons."

Li Qi was about to say something, but Shen Shuibi clamped a hand over his mouth. She said very seriously, "Don't speak yet. Let me talk. This kind of power isn't something you can resist. Don't try to think of a solution using your own methods. Divine abilities at Rank 6 and above are completely inconceivable and incomprehensible to you."

"I've already thought of a way. I will give you Lady Luofu's revered name, which can directly point to her remaining true spirit. When the time comes, you will set up a dharma altar and use me as a sacrifice. Although my cultivation foundation has scattered, my rank remains. By offering me up as a 'sacrifice' in your ritual, you can help Lady Luofu's true spirit briefly awaken. As long as Lady Luofu recovers even a tiny bit of power, this crisis can be resolved," Shen Shuibi stated with utmost gravity.

"And what about you!? No, there has to be another way." Li Qi immediately grew anxious upon hearing this.

Using Shen Shuibi as a 'sacrifice'... didn't that just mean sending her to her death?

Sacrificing Shen Shuibi through a ritual to buy a brief moment of recovery for Lady Luofu—there was no way he could accept such a thing.

"There are no other ways!" Shen Shuibi was frantic. "Look over there. Yang Ning's injuries are no lighter than mine. She won't be able to hold on for much longer."

Shen Shuibi pointed at the scenery in the near distance.

Over there, grand armies were locked in combat. The weakest among them were genuine demons capable of easily slaughtering dozens of humans, creatures that could become the nightmare of an entire region.

But those were merely the weakest. There were other Rank 9, Rank 8, and even Rank 7 demons recklessly unleashing their divine abilities, pouring their firepower onto the heavenly soldiers and generals.

The ground had long since entirely fractured, the rock layers groaning and cracking! The shockwaves of their impacts created endless ripples of solid earth that propagated ever forward!

Under that colossal power, the soil and bedrock rippled like the surface of water, generating massive, wave-like shockwaves that spread for kilometers, forging an unbelievable crater!

The earth collapsed outright. Winds and clouds roiled, stripping the sun and moon of their light. The mountains seemed to tremble incessantly, the very air weeping in agony. Flames born from the aftershocks scorched across the entire sky, and thick, pungent black smoke spiraled upwards like beacon fires.

The crimson military qi stretched to the horizons like fiery clouds, as if the entire firmament had been neatly painted over with a coat of red lacquer.

More than half the sky had turned a bloody red. The sun looked like a hole in the heavens, a gaping wound from which an endless torrent of fresh blood spewed forth, dyeing the horizon crimson.

Across a hundred li, divine abilities flew wildly. A heavenly general shattered a massive boulder hundreds of zhang wide with a single sweep of his halberd, yet it barely managed to scratch the skin of a giant bear demon general. The giant bear leaped into the air, and the seismic shockwave it unleashed upon landing carried hidden demonic power, instantly scattering dozens of heavenly soldiers into white vapor.

The golden light shielding the heavenly soldiers had turned purple-gold. It wasn't to look imposing, but because the 'Curse Demon King' in the sky was casting hexes, using his curses to turn them into stiff, puppet-like husks with severely diminished combat power.

Purple insect venom gnawed away at the heavenly forces' protective golden light. Given a bit more time, the heavenly soldiers and generals would likely suffer a total defeat.

However, judging by this pace, it would likely take more than half a day.

Li Qi understood. That meant he only had a little over half a day left.

Perhaps Yang Ning had other ways to hold out a bit longer, but it definitely wouldn't be for much longer.

"There has to be another way. Give me fifteen minutes. Just fifteen minutes. It won't delay anything, and I can definitely think of a solution," Li Qi said, holding Shen Shuibi down.

In his heart, however, he was thinking that if there truly was no way out, he would forcibly grab Shen Shuibi and make a run for it.

Shen Shuibi would inevitably resent him for it, but there was no helping it.

To personally kill Shen Shuibi and use her as a 'sacrifice'—he couldn't do it. Even if it meant becoming sworn enemies with her afterward, it was absolutely impossible for him to go through with it.

Shen Shuibi also knew that fifteen minutes wouldn't affect the grand scheme of things, so she waited for Li Qi.

It was just that she was usually very patient. She could often space out alone for several hours without feeling bored.

But now, during these fifteen minutes, she repeatedly glanced between the battlefield and Li Qi. Fraught with anxiety, she wished the time would burn away instantly and fast-forward several times over.

Li Qi, on the other hand, was praying for time to move as slowly as possible to give himself more time to think and strategize.

What cards did he have left to play?

Right now, the only thing he could rely on was Shaman God Mountain. But Shaman God Mountain was too far away; he was afraid he couldn't count on them.

But something like this shouldn't require Shaman God Mountain to act personally. If he had a way to communicate with Shaman God Mountain... given Lady Luofu's importance, some true bigshot would definitely strike from afar and swat these two little bastards to death.

But how was he supposed to communicate with Shaman God Mountain?

The Grand Diviners had trouble deducing Lady Luofu's location because her rank was too high.

They couldn't divine his location either, as he was a person of the Outer Dao and thus difficult to read. This was exactly why Young Master Zhu had sent him. If a person of the Outer Dao approached, Lady Luofu wouldn't misunderstand and think Shaman God Mountain was trying to take advantage of her vulnerability to loot a burning house.

As for divining Shen Shuibi, that was utterly pointless. Young Master Zhu had already laid down a chess piece on her ahead of time, which meant they knew she wasn't by Lady Luofu's side. The entire point of that layout was for Shen Shuibi to seek out Lady Luofu. Now she had been found, but Young Master Zhu probably never anticipated that Lady Luofu would be facing a crisis of survival.

Therefore, summing it all up, given the current circumstances, there was absolutely no way to communicate directly with Shaman God Mountain.

Then what should he do?

Were there any other sources of help he could factor in? Anything that could draw Shaman God Mountain's attention?

Wait a minute. Just now, while those two demons were boasting, didn't they say, "For ten thousand miles around White Snake Mountain, we two brothers have the final say. Even that Prefect Invocator is forced to lock down the city and hide."?

Then that meant right now, there were at least two entities that could likely help him, and who should also be willing to help him!

The Prefect Invocator of White Snake Prefecture, and the mountain god of White Snake Mountain!

These two were probably being utterly suppressed and beaten by the Curse Demon King and the Bear Slaughter King. But what if they showed up right now and helped him contact Shaman God Mountain?!
