# Chapter 114: Grand Diviner

In the exact moment Li Qi blinked.

The Curse Devil King and the Bear Slaughter King clearly saw a finger extend from the sky.

The finger descended from the heavens without a sound, as if a speck of dust were falling.

However, both the Curse Devil King and the Bear Slaughter King revealed expressions of utter despair.

But this finger was unstoppable.

Everything below, including the precious canopy, was flattened.

The ground caved in violently.

The Bear Slaughter King and the Curse Devil King, who had been incredibly majestic earlier and hadn't suffered a single injury while fiercely battling the Golden Crow, plummeted directly to the ground.

On the ground, all depressions and massive craters were filled.

The two demon kings didn't even have time to make a sound before they were instantly reduced to powder.

The finger's might remained undiminished as it continued down to the earth.

There was no sound, yet the terrain was completely altered.

At the very bottom, the hundred-li radius that was originally the battlefield, previously rugged and uneven, had been thoroughly compacted. Various materials, corpses, demons, including the two demon kings and the precious canopy, were all mashed together into a single entity, impossible to tell apart.

At first glance, it was incredibly smooth, as if a circular mirror had been placed on the ground. However, looking closely, one could see lifelike, intricately complex fingerprints embossed upon it.

With just a single finger, every entity within a hundred li was killed on the spot! Dying without a burial place! All of them were assimilated into this incredibly smooth circular mirror.

There was absolutely no room to resist.

Thus, when Li Qi opened his eyes, he saw a hundred-li mirror.

If one were to fly high into the sky right now and look down, they would see an extra fingerprint stamped upon the earth.

"This is..." Li Qi stammered. He then discovered that all the pain and wounds on his body had vanished. He didn't feel a thing, and even the injuries to his divine soul were completely healed.

Then, he noticed an elegant scholar in white robes had appeared beside him.

Wasn't this the mountain god of White Snake Mountain? Why did he suddenly come out?

Yet, he saw the mountain god of White Snake Mountain drop straight to his knees, prostrating himself completely as he shouted, "This minor god pays his respects to the Grand Diviner!"

Grand Diviner!

Hearing this, Li Qi shuddered.

However, he didn't kneel.

Shen Shuibi, who was holding him from behind, hadn't kneeled either, and it would be awkward to break free just to kneel, so he simply gazed up at the sky.

He saw a river of stars in the sky.

Even though it was broad daylight, a galaxy stretched across the heavens, splitting the sky in two.

On the left was the normal sky.

On the right, the side closer to the Tang Empire, the sky was filled with golden lotuses.

This scene lasted for a full five seconds. Then, the golden lotuses receded, but the river of stars still spanned across the sky, ever-present. Despite it being daytime, not even the sunlight could obscure this starry river.

Subsequently, a star from within the river shot down to the ground, landing perfectly beside the half-dead Golden Crow, Yang Ning.

The star spoke, "With injuries this severe, you will likely drop in realm. There's nothing I can do either; half your divine soul has been burned away. You'll just have to slowly recuperate from now on. By the way, where is Luofu?"

"Paying... paying respects to the Grand Diviner. Lady Luofu's true spirit resides within the Sun and Moon True Lead Cauldron. I implore the Grand Diviner to protect it." Yang Ning struggled to get up, enduring her heavy injuries to transform into her human form and bow. She then pulled out the deeply hidden small cauldron from her robes.

"So this is the Sun and Moon True Lead Cauldron? A treasure rumored to be capable of nurturing the essences of the sun and moon. Truly, seeing it once is better than hearing about it a hundred times; it indeed possesses immense might. It's a pity you couldn't activate it, otherwise how could you have been driven to such straits by two minor demons?" The star clicked its tongue in wonder. Following that, the small cauldron flew up into the river of stars.

"Leave Luofu to me. Shaman God Mountain will naturally reconstruct her true spirit for her. I just didn't expect her to be injured this severely. After we made the public proclamation earlier and didn't see her come to our doors, we assumed she was unwilling. To think she couldn't even maintain her consciousness. How lamentable."

After safely storing the Sun and Moon True Lead Cauldron, the star asked Yang Ning again, "Are you willing to go up the mountain with me? Your injuries also need to be nurtured. Shaman God Mountain has extraterrestrial grotto-heavens with two small suns, perfect for you to reside in."

"I dare not refuse the Grand Diviner's invitation. I only hope the Grand Diviner will take my sister along as well," Yang Ning said with a bow.

"Sister? Oh, the Moon Essence, right?" The star wobbled slightly, then suddenly appeared beside Li Qi and Shen Shuibi.

Li Qi was in a daze.

This was... a Grand Diviner. Unassuming and revealing nothing of their true depth, easily swatting the two previously formidable demon kings into two-dimensional smears.

And his true body was that river of stars in the sky?

"Moon Essence Shen Shuibi, what about you?" the star asked Shen Shuibi.

Li Qi felt a bit awkward. Had he and the White Snake Mountain god been ignored?

Then again, he was just a total scrub, not even ranked. Compared to these immortal-like figures, the gap was simply too massive.

At least he hadn't kneeled.

The guy kneeling next to him had been completely ignored too; that was the real embarrassment.

"Ah? Me? I... what about me?" Shen Shuibi seemed unable to process the situation for a moment.

Seeing her state, the Grand Diviner said, "Never mind, I'll just help you reconstruct your Unsullied Body. Mountain god of White Snake Mountain, are you willing to return her body part?" The Grand Diviner looked at the still-kneeling White Snake Mountain god.

"This minor god naturally has no objections!" The White Snake Mountain god immediately offered up Shen Shuibi's thumb with both hands.

The star flashed slightly. Before their eyes, Shen Shuibi's thumb reattached itself, the flesh and blood regenerating, and her broken Unsullied Body naturally recovered as well.

Meanwhile, Shen Shuibi kept looking back and forth between Li Qi and Yang Ning, her gaze constantly shifting between the two.

"You rendered a meritorious service by delivering the message this time, so I will bestow upon you an official title. From now on, you shall govern the White Snake Prefecture," the Grand Diviner said casually.

To the Grand Diviner, it was merely a casual remark, but the White Snake Mountain god kowtowed repeatedly, shouting praises of the Grand Diviner's benevolence.

After this was done, Li Qi suddenly felt his body float up involuntarily, slipping out of Shen Shuibi's embrace and hovering before the star.

"The outsiders' affairs are settled. Now it's your turn, little guy," the Grand Diviner said. "It's just a pity that I am merely an avatar and cannot bestow rewards upon you in place of my true self."

"This disciple pays respects to the Grand Diviner! This disciple's actions were not for the sake of rewards. The Grand Diviner need not trouble yourself over this," Li Qi immediately bowed and said.

So the other party just had a priority order and hadn't ignored him. This made Li Qi instantly realize that this was the perfect time to score some impression points.

So he immediately declined.

Not only because he wanted to leave a good impression, but also because his actual boss was Young Master Zhu. If this Grand Diviner rewarded him now for this merit, what would Young Master Zhu think?

Therefore, declining now was the most appropriate move.

At the same time, he was secretly shocked.

Did the other party mean that the entire river of stars spanning the sky was merely an avatar? Or that just this single star was an avatar?

If it was the former, that would be terrifying. How strong must the true body be?

If it was the latter, it meant every single star in that river could be an avatar.

Holy crap, both possibilities were scary as hell.
