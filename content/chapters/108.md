# Chapter 108: Clash of Arts

A resounding _dong_ echoed out, crisp and clear.

The halo of the precious canopy instantly swelled into a magnificent expanse.

Illusions surged forth—jade tiles overlapping like scales, alabaster steps carved from pristine fat, pavilions shrouded in auspicious mist, and towers veiled in propitious rainbows. Around the precious canopy, a hundred gods stood guard, various perfected beings lined up in ranks, immortal elders stood tall like cranes, and Daoist masters appeared pure as ice.

These gushing illusions suddenly coalesced into a single talisman.

The talisman bore the words: _'By the Edict of the Sage.'_ The core of the talisman naturally formed a phrase, which read: _'Nine Phoenixes Shatter Filth and Transform into Pure Dipper's Edge.'_

As the talisman was cast, the hundred-zhang-tall female phantom spat out a true mantra. Her voice was like a gentle breeze, yet simultaneously like an exploding thunderclap. Though not overly loud, it made one feel as if it were the only sound echoing in their head.

The voice chanted: "Supreme Terrace Stars, responding to change without cease.  
Exorcise the wicked and bind the malicious, preserve life and protect the body.  
May wisdom be bright and pure, the mind and spirit at peace.  
May the three ethereal souls endure forever, and the corporeal souls suffer no ruin.  
By the Law, move with utmost haste! By Divine Command!"

The moment the command was uttered, the sky-filling wicked shadow was forced to retreat.

The talisman reflected across the void, and bizarre phenomena erupted around the precious canopy. Azure Dragon and the White Tiger appeared in intricate opposition, while the Vermillion Bird and Black Turtle stood guard over its truth. The mighty gods of the eight directions proclaimed to the nine heavens, harmonizing with the natural heaven and earth to disperse the filthy qi.

Then, the perfected beings, hundred gods, immortal elders, and Daoist masters who formed the talisman bowed in unison and chanted:

"The Nine-Phoenixes soar, scouring filth across the ten directions.  
Golden youths guide the way; Jade maidens attend at the side.  
We humbly invoke the Nine-Phoenix Filth-Shattering General.

Swiftly descend on the Dipper's Edge to strike down all wicked spirits  
Heavenly generals and divine envoys, descend through the celestial heights.    
As stars shift and the Dipper turns, the three lights ripple in resplendent waves.

Honor this talismanic decree and purify the ten directions! 
Act with haste, by the command of the Heavenly Queen Mother of the West!"

Subsequently, a nine-headed divine bird abruptly materialized in the sky!

Amidst a sea of golden light, the nine-headed divine bird spiraled along with the talismanic disk, purifying the dharma realm, shattering filth, and exorcising the wicked. The wicked shadow that had clearly been suppressing the sky were seemingly touched by a single speck of spiritual light from the void. In an instant, a hundred evils were severed, completely eradicating the inauspicious.

With the thorough completion of this talisman, the heavens cleared and the earth found peace, purifying wickedness and destroying its form.

The nine-headed divine bird completely dispersed the wicked fog. Even Li Qi felt his entire body lighten, his spirits thoroughly invigorated.

A surge of pure qi washed over him. He and Old Ma couldn't help but take a deep breath. Instantly, Qi was generated and salivation stimulated, dissipating all malicious filth. He felt completely revitalized, his entire being emptied of wicked qi and radiating with vibrant energy.

Old Ma, who had lost a significant amount of weight and looked utterly listless, was unexpectedly reinvigorated. It was as if the exhaustion from fifteen days of continuous travel had completely vanished. He miraculously stood up and even let out a resounding neigh.

As for Li Qi, he could feel his inner qi rising!

A single breath of this pure qi was equal to two hours of his arduous training!

It was a pity that both the wicked qi and pure qi dissipated rapidly. He only had time to take four or five breaths. But before he could even feel frustrated, he heard voices and a conversation echoing from afar.

That hundred-zhang-tall female phantom... it had to be Lady Luofu's dharma body or something similar, right?

The softly glowing, translucent phantom possessed a tranquil and serene face, but her voice was cold. "A mere Rank 6 minor demon from some backwater dares to act so impudently before me?"

However, the dispersed sky-filling wicked shadow swarmed once more like ants. It coalesced into a terrifying demonic dharma body with a blackened face and jutting fangs. It was also a hundred zhang tall, standing shoulder to shoulder with Lady Luofu.

"Hehehe, as expected of Lady Luofu. A dignified Rank 3 mighty figure. Even with only a wisp of a remnant soul remaining, you effortlessly shattered the curse demon I condensed with a hundred and twenty percent of my power, reducing my thousand years of bitter cultivation to nothing. If your injuries were even slightly lighter, I reckon I would be dead as a doornail right now."

"Unfortunately, I've already seen through your true state." The demon spoke with an eerie, sinister tone. "To think you had to use the 'Nine Phoenixes Filth-Shattering Talisman' just to kill a single one of my curse demons. It shows you are already a lamp drained of its oil. If you ask me, you should just stop. Otherwise, while you might be able to kill me, you will expose your location and completely exhaust your true spirit. When that happens, you won't even have the luxury of becoming a ghost."

"Tch. You are a minor demon who has never seen the world. I don't blame you, though. The Nine Phoenixes Filth-Shattering Talisman is merely an environmental purification talisman used to clean the Daoist ashram. How did you make it sound like some ultimate killing weapon?" Lady Luofu's phantom laughed.

"Haha, naturally! Of course I know what the Nine Phoenixes Filth-Shattering Talisman is." The demon burst into raucous laughter, rocking back and forth.

The method of the Nine Phoenixes Shattering Filth was used with extreme frequency among the Daoist Sects. Whenever an altar was set up to execute dharma arts, a filth-shattering ritual was generally required. It swept away the qi of various evils, purified the environment, and warded off the inauspicious. Creating such a pristine environment before casting spells and circulating talismans could significantly reduce interference.

To put it bluntly, it was a method for doing the chores and cleaning up before performing magic.

The demon naturally knew this, which was exactly why he was laughing.

Once his laughter subsided, he said, "The real Lady Luofu wouldn't need these things at all. Even if she only had a wisp of a remnant soul left, the talisman she slaps down could still wipe me out cleanly! Even if it was just a cleaning talisman!"

"Your current level, while strong, simply can't pass for Lady Luofu. You must be her handmaiden!"

"I heard that Lady Luofu is attended by the essence of the sun and moon. The Sun Essence Golden Crow, Yang Ning, and the Moon Essence Jade Rabbit, Shen Shuibi. Speak, are you Yang Ning or Shen Shuibi!? Where exactly is Lady Luofu!?" The demon laid it all out with absolute certainty, pressing forward aggressively.

As he spoke, black fog surged out once more. This time, it rushed directly toward the vicinity of the precious canopy. It didn't dare advance too close, but instead tightly encircled the precious canopy, rendering it impenetrable.

The female phantom shook her head. "Complete nonsense. If you are truly so confident, why don't you step forward and try?"

"If it weren't for the Human Emperor Sword's sword intent 'drawing the ground as a prison' here, did you really think a minor demon like yourself could offend this venerable one and live?"[^1]

The demon refused to believe her. He continued, "How long do you think you can borrow a tiger's prestige? A Great Sage of my demon race has already passed down the word. They said Lady Luofu narrowly escaped the Human Emperor Sword. Her three ethereal souls and seven corporeal souls are completely shattered, leaving only a sliver of a remnant soul clinging to life. They ordered us to search the five lakes and four seas, the thousand rivers and hundred mountains. Anyone who captures Lady Luofu will be rewarded with a Rank 4 dharma treasure."

"You are nothing but a handmaiden. You'd better spit out Lady Luofu's whereabouts right now. Do that, and I might just spare your life. Once my patience runs out and I resort to extorting a confession through torture, you'll come to know my methods!" the demon threatened viciously.

As for Li Qi, it seemed no one had discovered him at all.

After all, there was virtually no difference between him and a bug on the side of the road.

However, listening to this conversation filled him with absolute horror.

Shen Shuibi had said there were traces of Lady Luofu's aura here. That meant... Lady Luofu's aura was definitely present. Lady Luofu had to be right here.

The demon claimed this was Shen Shuibi's colleague, the 'Sun Essence' Yang Ning. That was likely true as well. After all, if the real Lady Luofu was alive—especially given that she was described as a 'Rank 3' entity—she could probably blow a breath and kill this demon instantly.

Therefore, if both of those conditions were met, it was probably the absolute worst-case scenario.

Lady Luofu was here.

But she couldn't even manage to reveal herself! She could only rely on an equally heavily injured Yang Ning to bluff and protect her!

[^1]: 'drawing a prison on the ground' is a chinese idiom which basically means restricting activities, thoughts, or progress to a very narrow scope.

    This is essentially a very high-level way of saying that lingering power of the Human Emperor Sword is what's keeping them trapped here.
