# Chapter 112: Threat

At this very moment, Li Qi was strictly adhering to the proper etiquette, beginning the sacrificial ritual.

Evidently, for the Rank 6 mountain god of White Snake Mountain, the flesh of a Rank 5 Moon Essence was far too shocking. It was so much so that he personally sent down divine light, merging it with the ritual to extend an invitation to the Invocator.

Li Qi accepted the invitation without a moment's hesitation.

The ritual, the Invocator, and the ghost-god—the three became one.

The sacrifice was complete.

Li Qi's divine soul left his body, entering a chaotic expanse of space!

However, unlike Old Man Li's ghost-god domain, upon entering this place, Li Qi watched the surrounding scenery rapidly evolve.

The chaos receded and pavilions emerged. The empty world was like a blank sheet of paper upon which a painted scroll was revealing itself.

Soon, a landscaped garden appeared before Li Qi's eyes.

At a glance, he saw stone bridges akin to jade reflecting distant, bright halls. Azure waters encircled the area, and the willows and pines were a vibrant, verdant green. It was adorned with stone statues of men and horses. To the east lay a dragon vein aligning with the feng shui, while the south wind carried the fragrance of wildflowers.

There were also waterfalls, artificial rockeries, and clear springs.

The waterfalls echoed, the rockeries were elegant, and the clear springs rippled with purity. Fragrant grass blanketed the ground in green, while emerald peaks towered above. The shadows of clouds lingered, distant dark hills were shrouded in mist, and the dense trees looked as if they had naturally stepped out of a painting. It was an extraordinary sight.

Yet, within this, stood an elegant scholar in white robes. He was as handsome as a jade tree facing the wind, exuding an air of nobility.

However, standing before Li Qi, he was trembling with fear. He actually bowed first and asked, "I hope the Invocator will forgive me, but may I dare ask... where did this sacrifice come from?"

It seemed a Rank 5 physical body had truly frightened him quite badly.

Looking at the mountain god's demeanor, Li Qi frowned.

No wonder this guy was getting pinned down and beaten by the Curse Devil King and the Bear Slaughter King. A dignified Rank 6 mountain god was actually this cowardly, even bowing to an unranked Invocator like himself.

Doesn't acting like this just make others look down on him?

Nevertheless, Li Qi had no other options.

"Time is of the essence; there's no time to explain. Does the mountain god have a way to cause a massive commotion? I am an Invocator of Shaman God Mountain, entrusted by a Grand Invocator with a matter of grave importance. We can discuss the sacrifice later. I ask the mountain god to shine your divine light and draw the attention of the Grand Diviners of Shaman God Mountain!" Li Qi immediately cupped his hands and requested.

This demand terrified the White Snake Mountain god.

"This... this minor god's cultivation is still shallow, not yet enough to enter the sights of a Grand Diviner. If I rashly cause trouble and the matter turns out to be real, I'm afraid I would be struck down from my divine throne. I truly dare not—" The White Snake Mountain god hurriedly declined, even preparing to cover his face and quietly slip away.

Li Qi flew into a rage. Seeing the other party trying to leave had already pissed him off.

Shen Shuibi had to sever one of her own fingers and break her Unsullied Body just to summon this guy here! This even concerned their very lives! How could he let him just walk away like this!

Therefore, Li Qi whipped out his willow branch and lashed it at the void.

With a thunderous boom, even though the White Snake Mountain god had constructed various things within this space, the willow branch still produced an incredibly dazzling effect.

"Open your eyes and look closely, you coward! This is a token bestowed by a Grand Invocator! The divine qi within it cannot be faked! If you delay this important matter, forget about your divine throne, you won't even be able to keep your head!"

Li Qi pointed outside. "Cast your gaze outward right now! Look at the Curse Devil King and the Bear Slaughter King out there!"

Hearing this, the White Snake Mountain god's external body shifted its gaze. He genuinely saw the two demon kings he feared so intensely currently besieging a Daoist Sect cultivator.

That Daoist Sect cultivator was relying on a dharma treasure to bitterly hold on, seemingly at the absolute end of her rope.

While the mountain god was still observing, Li Qi continued to shout, "This matter involves Lady Luofu! If you dare delay any longer, not only will the Grand Invocator lay blame upon you afterward, but you will undoubtedly die!"

"If you act now, you will absolutely not be left out when rewards are doled out based on merit later! Furthermore, I offered the flesh of a Rank 5 Moon Essence as a sacrifice, which should be proof enough of my sincerity. Now, two paths are laid out before you. Let's see how you choose!"

Li Qi's tone was rapid and urgent, as if he were rushing him.

In reality, he _was_ rushing him. He wanted to ensure the White Snake Mountain god didn't have enough time to think up a third path.

What was the third path?

Naturally, it would be to silence Li Qi and the others, then defect to the demon kings' side.

It was just highly unlikely.

Judging from the Curse Devil King's tone, the White Snake Mountain god had been constantly oppressed by them. Now that he had a chance to turn things around, he surely wouldn't let it slip by, right?

Fearing this exact possibility, Li Qi spoke up once more. "The mountain god resides here, suppressed under the heels of the two demon kings. Although you don't have to manage affairs, it must be difficult to find an opportunity to realize your grand ambitions. Once this matter is settled, you'll be able to lean on the giant tree that is Shaman God Mountain. From then on, you can hold your head high and live a free and unfettered life. Wouldn't that be a joy?"

After Li Qi finished speaking, he looked back at the White Snake Mountain god, only to find him still looking completely hesitant and indecisive.

He was so angry he was dumbfounded.

"Mountain god! If you shirk your duty now, I will absolutely ask the Grand Diviners to kill you! From this day forth, you will be my mortal enemy, and I will thirst for your blood!" Li Qi roared in fury. "Even if the Grand Diviners refuse to kill you, once my cultivation is successful in the future, I will definitely shatter your mountain root and refine your body into a Five Peaks Seal! Even eating your flesh and sleeping on your skin would not be enough to exhaust my hatred!"

These words caused the White Snake Mountain god's expression to turn cold as he looked at Li Qi.

Even if his background was massive, right now he was nothing but an unranked Invocator. How dare he speak to him like that?

However, as he looked over, his expression tightened once again.

Ordinary people wouldn't be able to tell, but he could clearly see that the willow branch truly had the divine qi of a Grand Invocator infused within it.

In terms of rank, because it hadn't been refined and was just an ordinary willow branch infused with divine qi, it could be considered Rank 8 at best. But... the divine qi was genuine.

That meant this brat was more than likely telling the truth.

_I'll risk it!_

The White Snake Mountain god, who had always been indecisive and lacking his own opinions, steeled his heart and moved his body.

The mountain god's true body had moved!

---

To the outside world, it looked as if White Snake Mountain had suddenly begun to tremble!

The Curse Devil King looked panicked. "What's going on? Didn't we block his senses? That bastard is still sleeping, how could he wake up?"

They naturally knew they had to eliminate any variables. Because of this, they had made their move early on. First, they used fine wine to get the White Snake Mountain god drunk, plunging him into a deep slumber. Then, they used curses to seal off the avenues of the White Snake Prefecture's Governor.

This guaranteed that within the entire prefecture, they would be the only two Rank 6 demon kings.

The Bear Slaughter King was also suddenly startled. Looking in that direction, he discovered a sacrificial dharma altar.

"There's an Invocator over there offering sacrifices to White Snake Mountain! I'll go sever the connection!"

He roared, then ruthlessly launched an attack toward Li Qi at the altar.

Surging killing intent and wicked intent flooded into the space around Li Qi in an instant.

Li Qi's vision suddenly went black.

His body was pierced by indescribable agony, driven and wailing as he marched toward death!

_I can't stop!_

Li Qi stubbornly held on. He knew that while this killing intent was terrifying, it couldn't actually hurt him!

Someone would definitely help him block it.

If no one blocked it, then struggling would be useless anyway. He might as well persist with the ritual!

The ritual continued, and White Snake Mountain trembled. The ley lines were surging up, and the entire White Snake Prefecture, ten thousand li across, was experiencing an earthquake!

If this level of commotion continued for a while, it would be enough to attract the gaze of the Grand Diviners on Shaman God Mountain.

The two demon kings knew this as well, which was why they desperately tried to interrupt Li Qi.

However, the Bear Slaughter King's strike was blocked.

A dazzling Golden Crow had directly blocked the blow with her own body!
