# Chapter 166: Army Battle

On the battlefield, three Rank 8 experts faced off, their battle unceasing.

However, even as they fought, the two Rank 8 fiends did not keep their mouths shut. 

A fiend with an eagle's head and a human's body suppressed the other Rank 8 expert while speaking, "Zhang Lan, even the Hundred Forests Kingdom doesn't care about these people. Why must you meddle in others' affairs? You might as well retreat now, and you can still keep your life."

The other fiend lacked a human form. Instead, it was a terrifying giant ape. Wielding a stone club, it roared furiously, "Why waste words! This scum from the Righteous Sword Palace has already killed two of our brothers! Today, he must die here!"

"Shut up!" The eagle-headed fiend was secretly annoyed and sent a low-voiced transmission. "This person's cultivation is profound. Don't force him to fight us to the bitter end. We just need to abduct the population this time and—"

But before the eagle-headed fiend could finish, the giant ape fiend completely ignored him. It directly unleashed a divine ability, grabbed a small mountain near the battlefield, and forcefully pulled!

That small mountain was over a hundred meters tall, looming over the surroundings. Standing at its peak, one could look out and see a beautiful landscape.

However, this mountain suddenly began to shake.

The wild beasts within the mountain howled continuously! Their sorrowful cries could be heard all the way over to Li Qi's position. The earth trembled. A streak of silver light several hundred meters long flashed from within the mountain range and struck the giant ape. But the ape directly caught it with its other hand, squeezed lightly, and silenced it.

"Haha, a Rank 9 mountain god. Not only did you not run, you even dared to block your granddaddy?" The giant ape laughed uproariously. It tossed its hand back, casually throwing away the crushed silver-glowing lizard.

The silver light dissipated. Within the mountain ravines, only the rumbling sounds of rocks could be heard. The wild beasts that had been wailing earlier all lay low, not daring to make a single sound.

Immediately after, the entire mountain peak was uprooted! The ground split open, and the mountain range collapsed! Billowing earth qi surged upwards. The earth vein qi, previously suppressed within the soil, suddenly erupted. Yet, it was completely gathered by that divine ability, transforming into a long snake that coiled around the mountain peak.

Then, the giant ape swung the mountain peak, smashing it fiercely toward the Rank 8 cultivator.

An entire mountain, carrying its earth qi, crashed down in a single blow!

The Rank 8 cultivator, while resisting the interference of the eagle-headed fiend, formed a hand seal with his other hand. Facing the mountain, he softly said, "Go!"

Following his words, his lung qi surged out.

The lungs belong to metal. The qi of Geng-Metal condensed within his lungs, transforming into Geng-Metal sword qi. Along with his exhalation, it was spat directly out.

All the energy of the exhaled airstream converged. When it first emerged, it was merely a small golden sword. But after flying a mere sixty metres, it gradually expanded. A majestic sword intent poured out like a rushing river.

The sword light was inescapable. The sword qi pierced straight into the clouds. The small golden sword gradually swelled until it was level with the mountain peak. Then, with two strokes—one horizontal, one vertical—it carved out a cross.

Those two swords directly cleaved the mountain peak into four segments!

Even the earth was left with a massive cross-shaped indentation, as deep as a canyon! The tide of fiends below fled in panic. Those who ran too slowly were instantly sliced into four pieces by the overflowing sword intent. Their wounds were identical to the cross-shaped canyon, only differing in size.

Even the giant ape gained a cross-shaped wound on its body. The cut was extremely deep. Blood poured out, dyeing the ground red and even forming a small river that washed away parts of the army's formation.

For a moment, the giant ape slumped to the ground, unable to catch its breath.

"I told you not to push him to the brink!" The eagle-headed fiend was furious, yet its hands did not stop as it unleashed a spell art.

With a wave of its hand, the tide-like fiends below seemed to receive an order. They formed formations and ceased their charge. Instead, they manifested military qi, condensing it into a form like piled earth solidifying a defense. Its color was black, the qi dense and perfectly round, with crimson qi swirling within, resembling a rainbow that drifted without wind.

The military qi converged, but it wasn't used on the battle formation. Instead, it gathered in the eagle-headed fiend's hand and transformed into miscellaneous qi. Like a flock of birds flying chaotically, it descended from the sky, obscuring the daylight in a murky gloom, and heavily blanketed the Geng-Metal sword qi.

The sword qi was originally bright and polished, but once tainted by this miscellaneous qi, it immediately grew dim.

The sword blade wailed! Several small notches even cracked open on its edge. The resulting explosion of energy swept outward, instantly clearing a broad, pristine patch of sky over the center of the battlefield.

The dissipating energy even surged downward, but the condensed military qi of the fiends blocked the vast majority of it, causing only a minor impact on the fiends' formation.

Seeing this, those human military officers immediately shouted at the top of their lungs, "Assault battalion, follow me! We must carve out a path of survival for Mister Zhang!"

About a thousand-odd soldiers abandoned their defensive positions. Disregarding their own safety and unafraid of death, they charged forward to engage the tide of fiends head-on.

The moment they clashed, the military qi of both sides entangled, unable to separate. Only then did the Geng-Metal sword qi manage to break free from the miscellaneous qi.

The human soldiers charged into the fiends' position, temporarily tying up the enemy's military qi so it could no longer jointly suppress the Righteous Sword Palace cultivator whom the fiends called Zhang Lan.

However, the consequence of this forced assault was the rapid loss of numerous soldiers. Casualties skyrocketed, and screams of agony rang out continuously.

A dozen or so Rank 9 military officers struggled to hold the line on the battlefield, but they were heavily outnumbered. They faced almost double their numbers; a single Rank 9 officer had to simultaneously fight two or even three Rank 9 fiends.

It was difficult to sustain.

Battle losses continued to expand.

They wouldn't last much longer.

Although the officers had not given up, they began to fall one by one. Officers continuously died in battle, their bodies torn to pieces, the corpses eagerly devoured by the swarming fiends.

The physical flesh of a Rank 9 cultivator was a great tonic!

Up in the sky, the Rank 8 cultivator named Zhang Lan remained silent, yet he continued to drive his sword qi and even activated other divine abilities, doing his utmost to block his two peers.

The state of the battle was already clear.

Defeat was certain, and the hundred thousand commoners behind them would likely be lost as well.

These commoners shivered in fear, with nowhere to run and nowhere to hide. Some wept, some trembled, and some attempted to flee for their lives. But how could they possibly outrun a heaven-blotting horde of fiends?

However, right at this moment—

Within the battlefield, two golden threads suddenly appeared, one bright and one dark!

The two golden threads stretched out for several miles in an instant. Continuous and glowing with a lustrous light, they looked entirely tangible.

The golden threads rapidly crossed a distance of three or four miles. Before any of the soldiers or fiends below could react, they doubled back.

This entire round trip took no more than two breaths of time.

The golden threads slowly faded.

In their wake, a massive number of fiends—numbering in the hundreds—discovered two thin lines of blood appearing on their bodies.

Then, as they moved, the bloody lines gradually split open. Without any prior warning, their bodies fell apart into several pieces. The muscles of these fiends were even still twitching, and their eyeballs were still darting around.

"A supreme expert is helping us! I, Lin Ye, a thousand-man commander of the Hundred Forests Kingdom, offer my deepest thanks! Brothers, charge with me!" A military officer, seeing the fiends before him instantly cleared out, was immediately overjoyed and shouted toward the direction the golden threads had come from.

Following that, he immediately leveled his long spear and charged forward once again without hesitation!

The fiends were extremely panicked. Even some of the Rank 9 fiend leaders began to retreat.

However, the two golden threads struck out once more.

But up in the sky, the Rank 8 eagle-headed fiend slightly narrowed its eyes and then lightly pointed downward.

The military qi it had extracted earlier fell back down, having even grown stronger. The power of the fiends instantly surged, overwhelming the army.

The excess military qi formed a canopy like an umbrella, swirling like tumbleweeds. Its momentum was cohesive and unyielding, forming an impregnable fortress wall.

The two golden threads slammed into it with a *bang*. Their momentum greatly diminished, they returned in defeat, finally revealing their true forms.

If anyone possessed keen enough eyesight, they would be able to see that these two golden threads were actually two small knives. Flying through the air at an extreme speed, they left a solid, lingering trail in their wake.

And now, the military qi of the fiend army forcefully blocked these two small knives, rendering them unable to attack.

However, the price the eagle-headed fiend paid for doing this was that the cultivator named Zhang Lan finally gained a moment of respite.

Seizing the opportunity, he flew backward, taking out a pill and swallowing it to refine its energy. His injuries instantly recovered by a significant margin.

Meanwhile, the giant ape that had originally been beaten to the ground also stemmed its bleeding and crawled to its feet.

What was originally a one-sided situation had been balanced once again after the two golden threads pierced the battlefield, causing the fiends' besieging offensive to stall.

"Which fellow Daoist wishes to meddle in affairs here? Why not come out and show yourself?" The eagle-headed fiend made no further moves. Instead, it descended from the sky and stood upon the giant ape's shoulder.

"Roar!" Having just recovered, the giant ape bellowed furiously. Hefting its stone club, it smashed aimlessly at its surroundings, preparing to charge at Zhang Lan. But after the eagle-headed fiend stood on its shoulder, it had to forcibly suppress its rage. Baring its teeth, it glared angrily in all directions.

At this moment, a dragon colt descended from the clouds above. Seated upon it was a young man with slightly tanned skin, wearing neat clothing and exuding a sharp, alert aura. He cupped his hands in greeting toward the three Rank 8 powerhouses. "Three seniors, this is our first meeting. This junior pays his respects."

"Rank 9? Seeing that your dharma treasure is Rank 8, I thought you were Rank 8 as well. A mere Rank 9 dares to meddle here? Are you not afraid of death?" The eagle-headed fiend frowned, its expression displeased, though it still refrained from attacking.

The giant ape fiend, however, was much more straightforward. It merely glared resentfully at Zhang Lan, as if it cared about nothing else in the world.

When Zhang Lan saw Li Qi appear, he was initially somewhat delighted. But after seeing Li Qi's cultivation base, he sighed weakly. "Little brother... you, *sigh*. You'd best leave quickly. With me here, they won't be able to break through for a while. Take advantage of this opportunity and bring as many people away as you can."

Li Qi, however, was in no rush. He urged Old Ma forward on the clouds. Moving a few steps closer, he spoke to the eagle-headed fiend, "Senior, why are you so calm? Are you not afraid I have backup behind me?"

"Haha, you're an interesting junior. If we lacked confidence, do you think we would dare stand here?" the eagle-headed fiend laughed.

It was in no hurry to strike, as the giant ape's injuries were still healing. Since it was already waiting, it might as well wait until the giant ape had completely recovered its breath.

However, during the conversation between the four, the situation on the ground battlefield below grew increasingly dire. The ground that the army had just reclaimed with Li Qi's help was once again being pushed back by the fiends.

"Oh, I understand." Li Qi clapped his hands, a look of sudden realization dawning on his face. "For you seniors to dare attack, there must certainly be more than just the two of you present, right? There should be an even stronger Rank 8 fiend keeping watch nearby, securing your flanks and monitoring for any approaching experts. If an expert arrives, or if any other unexpected variables occur, you would immediately retreat, correct?"

"You are quite perceptive, but it's of no use. As a mere Rank 9, you have no right to speak here. Scram." The eagle-headed fiend seemed to sense that the giant ape's aura had mostly recovered, and it waved a hand dismissively at Li Qi.

Li Qi instantly felt a heavy pressure descend upon him. He hurriedly spurred his horse to dodge, but the burst of qi followed him like a shadow, chasing him the entire way.

Forced to jump all over the place, Li Qi had no choice but to summon his two streams of metal qi. Risking injury, he managed to block the attack. His metal qi slightly dimmed, having sustained some damage; it would require two or three days of gentle nourishment to fully recover.

The disparity was simply too massive. A mere casual wave of a hand had forced Li Qi to go all out just to defend himself.

However, this actually caused the other three to be greatly astonished.

A mere Rank 9 could actually block an attack from a Rank 8?

Even if it was just a casual blow.

With such ability, no wonder the others had mistaken him for another Rank 8 joining the battle.

"Hm, you have some skill. Then what about this?" His interest piqued, the eagle-headed fiend readied itself to strike again.

Zhang Lan had already finished recovering his qi and immediately moved to intercept. Yet, as he struck, he suddenly froze for a split second, slowing down by a beat and narrowly failing to block the attack.

If he hadn't blocked it, there was a high probability Li Qi would have been swatted directly into the pile of fiends below and besieged by several Rank 9 fiends.

But Li Qi was unhurried, showing absolutely no sign of fear. Cupping his hands again, he asked, "Senior, look behind you. Is that your trump card?"

The eagle-headed fiend was still secretly laughing at Zhang Lan's lapse, but upon hearing Li Qi's question, it instantly frowned. Almost simultaneously with the giant ape, it turned its head and glanced backward.

There, behind them, hung a waning moon radiating a cold, clear luminescence.

Within that clear light, a beautiful woman with an elegant aura stood bathed in the moonbeams, holding a silver-furred rat by the scruff of its neck.

The expressions of the eagle-headed fiend and the giant ape fiend drastically shifted into absolute horror!

Without the slightest hesitation, the two immediately formed hand seals. The eagle-headed fiend transformed into an escape light, vanishing in the blink of an eye.

The giant ape, which had been so arrogant just moments before, directly dove to the ground, burrowing into the earth to perform an earth traversal art. Its massive body melted into a streak of yellow qi, fleeing rapidly along the earth veins.

Zhang Lan wanted to pursue them, but Li Qi stopped him. "Senior, hold on. Do not pursue a desperate enemy. The commoners are more important."

Zhang Lan halted. Taking a glance at the battlefield below, he nodded. Without saying another word, he dove straight down and began massacring the fiends.

Without the support of the Rank 8 fiends, and with the equivalent human army entangling the enemy's military qi, the battlefield instantly turned into a slaughterhouse.

Not thinking too much of it, Li Qi also descended. Manipulating his metal qi, the two golden threads danced and zipped across the battlefield, weaving loop after lethal loop.

Zhang Lan was even faster. With a flick of his finger, countless streaks of sword qi crisscrossed the area. In the blink of an eye, he had plowed through the entire battlefield. These ordinary fiends were completely incapable of putting up a fight.

If these fiends had simply condensed their military qi, using it to somewhat resist an attack wouldn't have been impossible. But now, with the fiends' military qi entangled by the human army's military qi, they essentially had no protective layer. Facing a Rank 8 expert, their only fate was to be slaughtered.

As for Shen Shuibi, she hadn't moved much since she appeared, maintaining a blank, dazed expression.

Less than a few minutes later, the battle was over. All that remained was to clean up the battlefield.
