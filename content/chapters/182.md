# Chapter 182: Beating the Tiger

Hearing the other party's words, Lord Huangshan panicked and subconsciously let out a low growl.

_What is going on? Who is this sacred being? How did he see through my schemes with just a single glance?!_

He had plotted this matter in secret for ten long years, exhausting all his efforts to establish a connection with Vast Yang City. It was only because the other party needed a mountain god's support to construct a grand array that they finally relented and granted him this cultivation method.

Yet, to think that someone would come knocking on his door just as he had set it up?!

After a brief moment of panic, killing intent surged in his heart!

As a fierce tiger guarding the mountain, Lord Huangshan's word had always been absolute within a radius of a hundred miles. A mere glance from him would leave countless minor demons trembling in fear. When had anyone ever come to his door to interrogate him like this?

Besides, wasn't the one who came knocking just a Rank 9?!

As a fierce tiger of the mountains, having been imparted a Gu Shaman cultivation method by the Minister of State of Vast Yang, and combining it with Divine Dao methods to form his own path, he was considered an expert among those at Rank 9. Facing someone who did not even know the art of riding the wind, what was there to fear!

With this thought in mind, Lord Huangshan steadied his nerves and bared his fangs at Li Qi. "Who are you? What business is it of yours? Get out of Tiger Roar Cliff, and this Lord might still spare your life!"

Seeing Lord Huangshan's outward fierceness hiding his inner cowardice, Li Qi shook his head. Then, he mobilized the qi of the Five Great Mountains within his body, seeming to prepare for something.

However, seeing this, Lord Huangshan assumed he was about to strike and immediately let out a roar. Waves of sound surged through the air, creating shockwaves!

As the sound waves spread, the fierce tiger lunged directly, opening its bloodthirsty maw in an attempt to bite Li Qi.

Li Qi frowned slightly at the sight, then lowered his stance and threw a punch.

The punch carried the intent of the Five Great Mountains, perfectly countering a mountain god.

A great mountain was the highest peak among mountains, capable of suppressing a mountain god in terms of status.

Suppressed by this strike, Lord Huangshan's imposing manner weakened by thirty percent, but he still pounced forward ferociously.

In Lord Huangshan's eyes, a human, and a shaman at that, would certainly be at a disadvantage in close combat. Their physique was bound to be frail, completely incomparable to a fierce beast of the mountains like himself.

Therefore, by lunging close with the momentum of a fierce tiger descending the mountain, even if this person had other methods hidden, he would have to be forced back before he could use them. That single retreat would be his chance!

But what he had not expected was that the human showed not a shred of fear, swinging both fists to meet him head-on.

Moreover, instead of being crushed by the immense strength of the fierce tiger, the human directly suppressed the tiger in a frontal clash!

Wrestling a fierce tiger with strength, yet the tiger's strength fell short!

The double power unleashed by the Carp Stream Intent temporarily suppressed Lord Huangshan!

In that fleeting moment of suppression, Li Qi launched another attack.

Two more punches smashed down.

One punch landed squarely on the tiger's nose, while the other punched directly into its throat. The fierce tiger descending the mountain had instantly lunged itself into a foolish predicament.

The fist pierced deeply into its throat, grabbing its windpipe and pulling outward. With its nose taking a heavy blow as well, Lord Huangshan howled in pain, trying to snap his sharp teeth shut to bite the man's hand off!

But as he bit down hard, a metallic clang rang out. Unbeknownst to him, two streams of metal qi had already protected the other party's hand. That forceful bite instead snapped two of his large fangs!

The two streams of metal qi did not stop; with two swift slashes, they also trimmed Lord Huangshan's sharp claws.

A fierce tiger was feared by all beasts because of its claws and fangs.

Once its claws and fangs were ruined, even a lone pig or a stray dog could become its match.

Taking him by surprise, Li Qi directly pulled teeth from the tiger's mouth and severed its claws. Lord Huangshan's capabilities were instantly reduced by eighty percent. Moreover, his nose was bleeding profusely from the beating. He howled incessantly, and a crying tone could be heard amidst the tiger's roars.

Yet, Li Qi did not let go. He pulled his hand out of the tiger's mouth, grabbed Lord Huangshan's ears, mounted his back, and smashed a punch onto the tiger's head.

With Li Qi's arm strength, even a skull forged from copper and iron would be dented by a single punch. However, this demon tiger's skull was harder than copper or iron. The punch not only failed to take effect but also sent a sharp pain through his hand, tearing the webbing between his thumb and forefinger and drawing blood.

Thus, Li Qi turned ruthless and changed directions. Instead of smashing the skull, he focused entirely on the back of the neck, right where the head connected to the cervical spine.

The tiger demon also knew that was a weak point and thrashed about madly, roaring and clawing.

The tiger bounded through the mountain forest, rolling everywhere, shifting around, and howling incessantly. It desperately tried to throw Li Qi off, roaring like thunder as if to strike and devour him. Countless minor demons outside the mountain were frightened out of their wits by the sound, their ethereal and corporeal souls scattering, wondering which ruthless individual had dared to provoke Lord Huangshan.

Especially Li Zhufang up above, who was so terrified she reverted to her true form. A butterfly hid in the bushes, shivering uncontrollably.

But no matter how much Lord Huangshan thrashed, Li Qi's legs clamped around the tiger's waist like a vice. Several veins popped on his neck as he relentlessly pummeled its back.

Looking at his posture, it truly was a case of a fierce tiger meeting a vicious beast, suffering in unspeakable misery.

Clamping down firmly, Li Qi threw punches with both hands. His left hand gripped the fur on the nape of its neck, while his right hand rained down blows. After a flurry of punches and kicks, it wasn't long before Lord Huangshan was coughing up blood from his mouth and nose. Unable to move, he could only lie panting on the ground, lacking even the strength to speak.

Gripping the tiger's ears, Li Qi was also panting heavily. "Haa... Lord Huangshan, could you listen to a word I say?"

Hearing these words, tears streamed down Lord Huangshan's face.

_Damn it, you were clearly the one who came knocking and started the fight..._

But having suffered such a beating, Lord Huangshan also felt fear in his heart. Thus, he did not dare to say much and could only nod repeatedly.

A fierce tiger deep in the mountains struck terror into all beasts, but once trapped in a cage, it would wag its tail and beg for food.

Being ridden beneath Li Qi at this moment was no different from being trapped in a cage.

Seeing Lord Huangshan nod, Li Qi let out a long sigh of relief. "That is good. Look at you. If you had listened to advice earlier, you could have avoided this severe beating. Why go through the trouble?"

Lord Huangshan felt a sourness in his nose, his broken nasal bone throbbing with pain, and felt endlessly wronged in his heart.

_Did you fucking... ever try to advise me?_

But circumstances were stronger than the person. Lord Huangshan still repeatedly said, "Sir's words are reasonable. It was this petty god who lacked manners..."

Li Qi dismounted from the fierce tiger, which was even larger than a water buffalo, and said to him, "I apologize, Lord Huangshan. It is just that I heard someone over here wanted to construct a landscape grand array, so I came specifically to inquire. Is this true?"

Lord Huangshan hurriedly lowered his head and transformed into human form, turning into a boorish, large man. His features were ferocious, with a large head and a round face, appearing ugly and unrefined. Purple flesh bulged all over his body, but his face was bruised and swollen, looking as if he had been brutally beaten.

No, not 'as if'—he had absolutely been beaten up by someone.

He hastily bowed to Li Qi. "Sir is perceptive. The matter of the landscape grand array is indeed true. I just do not know what Sir intends to do?"

Li Qi also cupped his hands and said, "I wish to see the formation disk on your end. Please lead the way, Lord Huangshan."

He spoke politely, but his words directly ordered him to lead the way, leaving no room for refusal.

Naturally, Lord Huangshan understood the underlying meaning. He pulled a bitter face. "Sir, please follow me. It is just that I must clarify with Sir first: this formation disk was set up by a Rank 8 Great Shaman. That is not someone you or I can afford to provoke. Sir must not do anything foolish. If something happens, not only will I suffer disaster, but Sir will definitely be held accountable as well."

Li Qi smiled. "You need not worry about that. Since I came, how could I not know the great name of the Vast Yang Minister of State? Besides, hasn't Lord Huangshan realized that I am also a Shaman?"

"Ah, so a flood washed away the Dragon King's temple—our own people fighting our own people! Then why didn't Sir say earlier that you were sent by the Lord Minister of State?" Lord Huangshan suddenly realized.

No wonder this person saw through the location where the bone demon was being nourished the moment he arrived, and even asked about the landscape grand array.

These things were extremely secretive. Ordinary people shouldn't be able to recognize them.

It turned out he knew from the very beginning!

Figuring this out, Lord Huangshan felt even more wronged.

Didn't that mean he suffered this beating for nothing?

"Because I was not sent by him to begin with. I just... have a bit of a connection, that is all. Alright, enough idle chatter. Take me to see the formation disk," Li Qi urged.

"Yes, I will lead the way for Sir immediately!" Lord Huangshan quickly stood up, then led Li Qi all the way toward the center of the mountain.

Li Qi followed.

Soon, the man and the tiger arrived at the base of Tiger Roar Cliff.

Having traveled all the way here, Lord Huangshan pointed at a large boulder and said, "Sir, please look. This is the formation disk here, personally arranged by the Lord Minister of State."

"I know. Let me take a look." Li Qi stepped forward and touched the stone with his hand.

It was cold to the touch and looked like nothing more than a very ordinary stone. Aside from that, he could hardly sense anything special about it.

However, Li Qi had his own methods.

He brought out a 'thread'.

It was precisely the thread he had plucked from Li Zhufang. Li Qi had not thrown it away; rather, he had kept it in his hand the entire time.

Right where the formation disk was located, Li Qi gently tugged at the thread.

Suddenly, those previously almost imperceptible threads were tugged and surfaced one after another.

The dense web was subtle and intricate; pulling a single hair moved the whole body. The entire network was temporarily exposed because of Li Qi's action.

He saw countless thin filaments—tens of thousands of them—spreading outward with this stone as the center, like a spider's web.

These filaments were woven together into a network one by one, constantly rippling. It was unknown where these filaments connected to, but tens of thousands of them spread outward. Every single second, every single filament vibrated at an extremely high frequency, surging with rippling traces, conveying its own fluctuations to the formation disk and transmitting wisps of essence qi.

The countless, densely packed filaments spread to various minor demons or earth veins, and from those objects, they continued to extend until they were completely invisible.

"Ah, this—" Lord Huangshan seemed to be seeing this sight for the first time as well and was dumbfounded for a moment.

Meanwhile, Li Qi silently memorized all the various details of this formation disk. Although he actually couldn't understand this thing either, he could go back and flip through his books.

_This is the formation disk of the landscape grand array, so..._ Having witnessed all this, Li Qi felt more confident in his heart.

In truth, he always had a scale in his heart.

Regarding the overall situation in Vast Yang City.

First was Wang Boyan, who had helped him.

Naturally, Wang Boyan was untrustworthy. There were far too many suspicious points about him, and most crucially, these suspicious points were clearly exposed to him on purpose.

That was why Li Qi hadn't made a fuss or shown any reaction.

Because, since the other party deliberately exposed those suspicious points, who was to say they weren't bait? What he needed to do was not to distinguish whether these things were true or false, but to save the common people, so Li Qi didn't bother dwelling on them.

Second, regarding the blood sacrifice and the landscape grand array, Li Qi had always had a plan in mind.

To save those hundred thousand plus people, just providing food was definitely not enough. Food was the foundation, a necessity, but it wasn't everything.

Even if all the food was provided, if Vast Yang City remained stubborn and insisted on a blood sacrifice, the food wouldn't be of any use. Food couldn't help these commoners withstand destructive power; they would still be killed as sacrifices.

Li Qi's idea was that the food would help them survive, and then he would help migrate them to that minor world.

Wasn't the Golden Wave Sect planning to open up an island there as a plantation ground in the minor world? These hundred thousand plus people should be just right. When the time came to negotiate with Wang Boyan, the Golden Wave Sect wouldn't need to dispatch extra manpower; they could just directly use this group of people to farm the land there. It would solve both the Golden Wave Sect's manpower problem and the commoners' livelihood problem.

If Wang Boyan refused, then Li Qi wasn't prepared to reason with him either.

That minor world had no master. If they didn't go to help the Golden Wave Sect farm, couldn't they farm it themselves? Migrating over there would suffice.

Although doing so would have some future troubles and side effects—migrating from All Under Heaven to another minor world was like moving from a world-class bustling metropolis to a primeval forest in Africa, losing many opportunities and prospects, and that minor world might also bear an excessive burden.

But no matter what, it was better than waiting for death.

Secretly memorizing the characteristics of the landscape grand array, Li Qi slapped a palm onto it.

Countless thin filaments snapped, and the entire formation disk was directly and forcibly destroyed by him!

This had no effect on the grand array as a whole. The landscape grand array was formed by the collection of countless mountains and waters. At most, Li Qi had only destroyed this one minor node; it wouldn't even be noticed.

However—

Countless minor demons in the mountain felt their bodies lighten, suddenly feeling as though they had relaxed a lot. Many demonic entities didn't know what had happened; they just felt that their fatigue had been greatly alleviated.

Meanwhile, Lord Huangshan's eyes practically split open in rage. He looked angrily at Li Qi. "Sir, why?! You destroyed this formation disk, so what about my bone demon!? Hasn't it also been destroyed along with it!?"

If it wasn't because he couldn't win in a fight, Lord Huangshan would have probably pounced on him at this moment.

But Li Qi immediately said, "Lord Huangshan, do not panic. I naturally have compensation for you. How could I let you suffer a loss for nothing?"

Hearing this, Lord Huangshan's anger turned to joy. "Ah, so that is how it is. It was I who misunderstood Sir."

"Come over here and reveal your golden body," Li Qi said.

Hearing this, Lord Huangshan immediately became vigilant. "Why does Sir want me to reveal my golden body?"

The golden body condensed by the Divine Dao was the core of cultivation and would not be casually shown to others.

"I have an item that can make up for your lost bone demon, but it requires a drop of divine blood from your golden body to acknowledge you as its master," Li Qi said. "If you are worried that I mean you harm, well, I could mean you harm right now. You are no match for me anyway."

Lord Huangshan pondered for a moment and found that this logic was indeed sound. The other party didn't seem to have anything to covet from him.

As for fighting, he couldn't beat the man. It wouldn't be hard for the man to kill him, and since they had no past grudges, killing him would be meaningless.

Thinking this, he then revealed his golden body.

However, just as he exposed his weak point, two streaks of metal qi flashed past like a guillotine, directly severing the golden body's head along with the divine soul, shredding them completely.

Li Qi stood nearby without the slightest fluctuation in his demeanor.

He had never intended to leave this tiger alive to begin with. It was just that fighting him to the death might attract the attention of other mountain gods nearby, so he employed a slight trick to make him expose his weak point, nothing more.

Lord Huangshan was a tiger. Eating people or eating beasts—Li Qi didn't mind any of it. He was just born that way.

But this demon had participated in the blood sacrifice of the landscape grand array, and was a minion of the Vast Yang Minister of State. He even planned to drain the essence qi and sacrifice the lives of the minor demons of an entire mountain to nourish a bone demon for himself.

Then he had no choice but to eradicate the evil completely. Keeping this kind of mountain god around would only be a disaster.

Furthermore, if he were allowed to live, wouldn't the matter be leaked if he reported the situation here to the Vast Yang Minister of State?

Having killed Lord Huangshan, Li Qi walked out of the cave.

As for what to do after silencing the witness, he already had an idea.
