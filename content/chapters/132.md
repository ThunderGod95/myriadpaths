# Chapter 132: Unexpected

Suddenly, Li Qi was in grave danger.

A formless shadow was attacking him relentlessly.

Invisible, inaudible, and completely scentless, the assailant seemed as if they did not even exist in this world.

Yet, the attacks were undeniably real!

A sharp, buzzing sound suddenly pierced the air from behind!

Li Qi's pupils contracted. He ducked his head and rolled across the ground.

An invisible forcefield swept through the space he had just occupied, cleaving the earth, shattering rocks, and plunging deep into the desert sands. 

Relying purely on brute force, this strike easily reached the level of splitting monuments, shattering boulders, and cleaving through solid iron and gold.

Li Qi reacted in an instant!

The qi within his body surged, and he thrust out a finger!

Wood and fire intertwined in radiant splendor; the fire burned bright while the wood flourished.

The Azure Dragon divine qi ignited, and the Vermillion Bird divine qi erupted into roaring flames, unleashing devastating power!

The intense heat distorted the air into shimmering threads. In the vast, empty desert night, it was as if a new star had been born. A massive firestorm engulfed everything in its path, melting the barren soil into magma and reducing withered wood to ash!

Even the stones ignited, burned, and melted as easily as paper. They merged into the lake of magma, solidifying once the flames died down, as if they had never existed at all.

This was Li Qi's strongest attack at his current level!

It was the combined strike of the Vermillion Bird divine qi and the Azure Dragon divine qi. The blast tore a massive rift into the earth, leaving a small river of flowing magma in its wake.

The moment the strike landed, Li Qi whipped his head around.

Another dark silhouette was already lunging at him!

The previously invisible shadow had now split into two figures—one black and one white—launching a pincer attack!

The white figure pulsed with a burning, intense Yang energy, dragging a long, spectral tail behind it.

The black figure seemed to harbor a deep gloom within its body, extending two black wings from its back. With crimson eyes that looked like burning coals, it was starkly different from the white shadow—frantic, aggressive, and relentlessly attacking Li Qi.

"It didn't land?" Li Qi frowned.

With the sheer power of that last attack, not even a Rank 9 could emerge completely unscathed, right?

A second later, Li Qi realized exactly why.

"Splitting yourself into the Yin and Yang dual qi of sun and moon? Good technique," Li Qi praised, scrambling and rolling to evade the next wave of assaults.

Although Rank 9 of the Martial Dao lacked extraordinary techniques, it granted him enhanced physical prowess and reaction speed. So, while this Child of Destiny had him scrambling miserably, his life was not in immediate danger, and he still had the breath to keep talking.

However, Li Qi also noticed that when she split her qi, this person could shift her body into a hazy state made of pure shadow and pure light, perfectly dodging his energy blasts.

This forced Li Qi into a passive state, leaving him with no choice but to continuously dodge.

The woman's attack patterns were equally bizarre. Her movements were incredibly agile. With every assault, she would split her form, frequently turning invisible before striking again.

Using this cloaking ability, she closed the distance to Li Qi and launched sneak attacks, trying to carve wounds into his flesh.

Furthermore, as the battle dragged on, Li Qi realized that her already blindingly fast ambush speed was continuously accelerating!

Li Qi floundered, barely able to defend himself.

Yet, he wasn't anxious in the slightest. He merely sighed again. "Miss, you have no chance of winning."

The opponent remained dead silent, continuing to circle Li Qi and slamming into him time and time again.

Li Qi also stopped speaking. He silently endured the onslaught, rolling across the ground and occasionally leaving traces of internal qi in the dirt.

This bitter struggle lasted for about two minutes.

Finally, the figure's endless barrage of attacks came to a halt.

Li Qi caught a moment to breathe, no longer forced to roll around in such undignified postures.

Then, the paused figure confidently brought her hands together and whispered, "Rise!"

After that, absolutely nothing happened.

Li Qi took advantage of the lull to explain. "Miss, using your avatars of the Yin and Yang dual qi of sun and moon to constantly attack while secretly setting up a Yin-Yang Strangling Formation... the idea was good."

The attacks resumed immediately.

However, a prepared Li Qi easily dodged once more and continued speaking.

"However, in the shifting of Yin and Yang, when Yang is born, Yin dies; when Yang dies, Yin is born. This cycle of reversal and progression makes the transformations clear. The division of life and death is like a mother giving birth to a child—when the child matures, the mother ages and dies. It is the natural principle."

"But just now, when I bombarded the wood qi with the Vermillion Bird's Li Fire, it looked like I was just trying to create an explosion. In truth, the wood above Li is Yin Wood. Its roots are severed, its branches are cut—it is known as dead wood. The wood qi of death holds profound, bone-chilling cold. By combining it with the vibrant, life-filled Great Forest Wood qi I had in reserve, life and death became mutually dependent. This prevented your Yin qi from extinguishing and your Yang qi from being born. Your Yin-Yang cycle stagnated, and your formation broke itself."

Li Qi explained his spell art.

In a battle of spell arts, victory ultimately went to whoever possessed deeper knowledge and greater versatility.

How could a Child of Destiny from some backwater realm possibly know more than Li Qi, who spent all his days buried in books?

Her supposedly hidden lethal strike had been exposed from the very beginning. As an Invocator, observing qi was Li Qi's foundational skill. Without a specialized technique to conceal her qi, her sneak attacks were completely useless.

The offensive paused once again.

Seeing this, Li Qi cupped his hands and said, "Can you listen to me now, Miss? I truly have a way that might just make this world a little better."

The Yin and Yang dual qi of sun and moon merged. Her figure became entirely transparent once more, erasing her presence completely. Only her voice could be heard. "Can you make the Exalted Immortals abandon this world?"

"No." Li Qi shook his head. Having sobered up from his earlier arrogance, he knew his limits perfectly well. He had no right to interfere with the decisions of the Great Deer Kingdom's ruler.

"Then we have nothing to discuss," the voice replied, bracing to attack anew!

Even if this young master was stronger than her.

"Hold on! You have no chance of winning!" Li Qi shouted again. "You still don't understand the gap between yourselves and the Exalted Immortals!"

But these words failed to stop her renewed assault.

"Sigh," Li Qi breathed out.

This Child of Destiny was struggling just to fight him, yet she wanted to rebel against the Great Deer Kingdom?

Truly... had she still not realized the disparity in their strength?

With that thought, Li Qi cupped his hands. "Senior Brother, please make your move."

The moment the words left his mouth, the Child of Destiny plummeted straight out of the sky, crashing into the ground without the slightest ability to resist.

It was a young girl. Her face turned a purplish-red, as if an invisible hand were crushing her throat. She had instantly lost all ability to move!

Li Qi was secretly alarmed, as he hadn't sensed a thing.

Was this a Diviner's curse art? Was this one of the two deadly traps that Diviner had secretly laid down?

Soon after, a mirror materialized in the air.

It was the Moon-Mirror. It seemed this Diviner young master was highly proficient in using this kind of spell art.

The Moon-Mirror could project the bright moon from hundreds of thousands of li away right before one's eyes; naturally, it could project a person's image as well.

An ethereal illusion of a person appeared in the sky. It was none other than the Diviner young master.

Meeting again, he chuckled softly. "Do you see now, Junior Brother? We have an utterly irreconcilable conflict with these natives. You wanting to make things easier for this world is rooted in righteousness and justice, but in my eyes, it is unwise."

"If this world desires freedom, it must break away from the Great Deer Kingdom. But they cannot do it. Even if they sacrifice everything, they will fail. Acting in such a manner is both powerless and foolish, which is why I scoff at this world."

"The path you have pondered, Junior Brother, is one of righteousness and justice, whereas my path is one of wisdom and power. This is the difference between us. But looking at it now, my path seems far more suited to our current reality."

The Diviner spoke, then waved his hand lightly. The Child of Destiny was instantly bound by shadows, completely immobilized.

Li Qi smiled bitterly. "Senior Brother, you misunderstand. I am not suddenly overwhelmed by a bleeding heart to save this world."

"Oh? Then what are your thoughts, Junior Brother?" the Diviner asked curiously.

Both of them understood a fundamental truth.

Whether it was righteousness or justice, both had to be built upon a foundation of equality between the two parties. And right now, no matter what happened, the Earth Oil World could never stand as an equal to the Great Deer Kingdom.

Righteousness meant remaining impartial, not taking sides. Fairness was not absolute equality. Therefore, the Earth Oil World could never escape its fate. If Li Qi tried to help them break free from the Great Deer Kingdom, that would be unrighteous, because it would damage the Great Deer Kingdom's interests in order to favor the Earth Oil World.

If the pitiful sheep were saved, the mighty wolf would starve.

Li Qi spoke earnestly. "The Dao of Righteousness I have contemplated does not demand ignoring individual differences in pursuit of absolute equality. Doing so would only be unrighteous toward the strong."

"Though there are some fallacies in your words, they are not entirely wrong," the Diviner evaluated.

Li Qi ignored the critique and continued. "However, the Great Deer Kingdom has also stripped the Earth Oil World of something it inherently deserves: opportunity."

"True fairness is not absolute equality, but equal opportunity for all. The Great Deer Kingdom rules the Earth Oil World using wicked methods of the Human Dao, leaving the people destitute and severing all vitality. Therefore, dominating the Earth Oil World isn't inherently unrighteous; the true injustice lies in the Great Deer Kingdom depriving the Earth Oil World of its opportunities."

"Indeed. The Great Deer Kingdom is intentionally smothering the birth of cultivators in this land. But when cutting weeds, one must remove the roots. There is nothing wrong with acting this way," the Diviner said.

"Therefore, the Great Deer Kingdom's approach is good for the wolf, but unrighteous to the sheep. This will cause the sheep to weaken day by day until they eventually die out. Can such an approach truly be considered good for the Heavenly Dao? In the end, if the weak perish and the strong grow frail, can that be considered a good thing for All Under Heaven?" Li Qi asked.

"If I am to uphold righteousness and justice, I must leave seeds for the sheep right now. By doing so, the sheep can multiply, the wolf will not grow weak, and the cycle of the Heavenly Dao will remain flawless. This is what it means to walk the righteous Dao," Li Qi concluded.

Upon hearing this, the Diviner fell into deep thought.

Clearly, Li Qi's words had profoundly enlightened him.

After pondering for a moment, he bowed to Li Qi for the very first time.

The bow was deeply formal. It was evident that he had been swayed by Li Qi's argument.

Afterward, the Diviner continued, "Your words hold great reason. However, there is still one thing I do not entirely understand. If the wolf constantly gives the sheep opportunities, what happens if the sheep eventually gain power, cultivate into demons, and return for revenge? What should the wolf do then?"

Li Qi replied, "A wolf is a wolf not simply because it eats sheep."

"The Great Deer Kingdom manages to rule countless minor outer worlds, and even holds a firm place within All Under Heaven, because of its own intrinsic strength."

"As long as the Great Deer Kingdom maintains its might, the sheep will continue to breed, and the wolf will forever remain the wolf. But if the Great Deer Kingdom wanes and the Earth Oil World grows strong, the roles of wolf and sheep will swap. Just like the turning of Yin and Yang, what does it matter? As long as the new sheep still possess opportunities, the heavens and the earth will forever maintain a flawless cycle. It will never wither and die," Li Qi explained.

"When Yin reaches its extreme, Yang is born; when Yang reaches its extreme, Yin is born. It is just like the Vermillion Bird's Li Fire, which represents absolute, blazing Yang, and the Azure Dragon divine qi, which overflows with boundless vitality. Yet, when these two intense energies of life and Yang clash, they spawn the Yin Wood above Li, radiating a profound Yin qi of death. This is the ultimate principle of the great cycle."

"I have been taught," the Diviner bowed deeply, offering another formal salute.

He was thoroughly convinced.

If this was true *righteousness*, then although it seemed unfeeling, it was the fundamental principle by which the Heavenly Dao operated. This disciple of the Grand Invocator had already surpassed him in his understanding of the Heavenly Dao. This bow was an expression of gratitude for clearing his doubts, as well as genuine admiration for Li Qi's intellect.

"My name is Bo Yue. May I know Junior Brother's esteemed name?" the Diviner named Bo Yue asked.

"My surname is Li, and my given name is Qi," Li Qi replied.

"Meeting you here today has truly benefited me immensely. Perhaps this is exactly why our Master sent us out to gain experience," Bo Yue said with a deep sigh of emotion. "Having reaped such a harvest, this journey to the Earth Oil World has not been in vain."

"Then I ask Senior Brother to release this Child of Destiny. I have a few words I wish to say to her," Li Qi requested.

"While you and I can understand each other, it is because our perspectives are aligned at this height. I am afraid the narrow vision of those from minor worlds will find it exceedingly difficult to comprehend the two of us."

"Upholding fairness is simply the way I walk my Dao. It is for no other reason, nor do I seek any reward. It is merely to practice my Dao," Li Qi said with a smile, sensing nothing wrong with his approach.

Upon finishing, the two young masters of Shaman God Mountain exchanged a glance and burst into loud laughter. Following that, they both bowed in unison toward the distant horizon and shouted with one voice, "We request Senior to show yourself! The two of us are but mere Rank 9s, so our words may seem somewhat arrogant. But if viewed from the perspective of Shaman God Mountain, surely these words are worthy of the Kingdom Lord's deep consideration?"

As the two young masters of Shaman God Mountain spoke in unison, their combined qi resonated, allowing their voices to pierce straight through the firmament.

Indeed, both of them were highly intelligent men, and intelligent men always knew how to use their brains.

Two noble young masters from Shaman God Mountain—would the Great Deer Kingdom dare turn a blind eye and just let them play around on their own?

If an accident were to befall these young masters, how could the Kingdom possibly justify it?

Therefore, both of them had long been certain that someone was watching this place.

Having done this, Bo Yue released his bindings.

Li Qi fixed his gaze upon the Child of Destiny and said, "Miss, watch closely. This is exactly why I tried to persuade you earlier, why I told you that you had absolutely no chance of winning."

The moment Li Qi's words fell, a massive hand descended from the sky!

A dharma body abruptly manifested. Its frame was incomprehensibly colossal, and its dharma power was so majestic that it instantly filled the entire starry sky!

If one were to gaze upon it from a great distance, they would see that the entire planet looked as miniature as a basketball in front of this dharma avatar.

However, aside from specific individuals, no one else could even perceive the existence of this dharma body.

Bo Yue, on the other hand, was deeply astonished. "Such a dharma avatar! This is undeniably a Rank 5—it is the ruler of the Great Deer Kingdom himself!"

Li Qi was even more horrified, for he caught a glimpse of a tiny figure standing behind the immense dharma body.

"Teacher!"
