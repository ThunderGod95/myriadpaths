# Chapter 109: Clash of Arts (Part 2)

The precious canopy stood tall, thick dark clouds gathering all around it.

Li Qi racked his brain on the sidelines. While calming Old Ma down, he pondered his next move, analyzing what cards he had left to play, the potential dangers, and how to execute a plan.

Yes, he hadn't given up and accepted his fate yet.

Even though the two terrifying entities before him could easily crush him with a mere shudder, he still refused to lie down and wait for death.

Li Qi had never been someone willing to resign himself to fate. Even back when he was a boat tracker, he had desperately fought to climb higher. Whether it was self-study or physical training, he had never once given up.

Just as Li Qi was racking his brains, trying to accomplish something—even if it was entirely insignificant—he suddenly heard another sound.

A very loud sound.

It sounded like...

Like... an army?

Countless war cries and shouts of slaughter swept over.

Old Ma restlessly kicked the ground. He nudged Li Qi and nervously bit into his clothes, pulling him toward the edge of the cliff.

Li Qi stood high and gazed into the distance, channeling qi to enhance his vision. To his shock, there was an army a hundred li away!

It wasn't a human army, but an army composed entirely of demons!

There were countless minor demons. Most hadn't assumed human form and essentially still looked like wild beasts, but they acted remarkably human. They wore ill-fitting armor, lacked weapons, and primarily relied on their claws and fangs to attack.

Occasionally, there were minor demons acting as officers who had assumed human form. They were clearly all at Rank 9, wielding weapons, surrounded by divine abilities, and leading the troops forward.

There were over a hundred thousand minor demons. Among them were roughly a thousand Rank 9 transformed demons, and dozens of Rank 8 demons summoning wind and rain while driving wicked shadows. It whipped up gusts of demonic wind. With over a hundred thousand demons gathered together, black qi dotted the area like stars, blotting out the sky and sun, emitting an unbearable, bloody stench!

Then, the black wind above this demonic army billowed and converged, transforming into yet another dharma body!

This dharma body was also a hundred zhang tall. It possessed the head of a bear and the body of a man. Crimson qi rested over its eyes, yellow qi coiled around its four sides, and black qi fluttered about like flying birds. Crimson-black qi, patterned like leopard fur, draped across its armor.

It stepped forward, followed closely by two Rank 7 lieutenant generals. Military qi swirled around its body, white above and crimson below. The qi gathered like towering clouds, resembling a mass of human heads. The bloody aura was chilling, as if mimicking death and flowing blood, yet one could also feel the arrival of rain, fog, and deathly winds. The sunlight grew dim, giving it the majestic, unyielding aura of a martial pheasant descending upon a city!

The scene drained the color from Li Qi's face.

He might not understand much else, but this overwhelmingly intense qi perfectly aligned with the records in the [Book of Invocation]!

This was the one thing he could decipher. What stood before him was undeniably malevolent military qi. Just looking at the sight—the moon obscuring the stars, twisting like a snake, radiating lawless slaughter, and sunlight scattering like spilled blood—there was no doubt. It was the manifestation of 'disobeying the four seasons, intersecting in reverse, resulting in chaotic yin and yang qi.'

This newly arrived demon had disrupted the surrounding heavenly timing using nothing but its own military qi!

Unfortunately, Li Qi's shock was entirely useless.

The bear-headed, human-bodied dharma body forged of malevolent military qi took two steps forward. It stood in a three-way standoff with the other two dharma bodies, opening its mouth to laugh. "Haha! Let alone a mere sun essence Yang Ning, even if you were truly a wisp of Lady Luofu's remnant soul, you would still perish in despair before the 'Two Kings of White Snake Mountain'!"

"For ten thousand li around White Snake Mountain, we two brothers have the final say. Even that Governor is forced to lock down the city and hide. If you don't obediently spill Lady Luofu's whereabouts, prepare to suffer my brother's curses!"

After the bear-headed demon spoke, the sky-covering wicked shadow chimed in, "I am the 'Curse Devil King,' and he is the 'Bear Slaughter King.' With the two of us joining forces, we can even battle a Rank 5. Even in your prime, you were only a Rank 5, much less now that you are so heavily injured!"

"Just obediently hand over the clues to Lady Luofu, and you can still keep your life!" the Curse Devil King said grimly.

The Bear Slaughter King said no more. Instead, he stomped his foot. Instantly, the over a hundred thousand demon soldiers tidied their ranks and marched forward in formation. Their military discipline was solemn and organized. This was clearly an elite force of hundred-battle veterans, not some ragtag mob!

However, the female phantom moved.

Whether it was Lady Luofu or the sun essence Yang Ning controlling the dharma body was unknown, but it reached out a hand once more and shouted with a soft voice, "Courting death!"

Although the voice was very soft, it struck like an exploding thunderclap!

"Heavenly Soldiers and Heavenly Generals, Fierce Thunder Envoys, Eight Trigrams Generals, Valiant Troops of the Five Thunders, heed my edict! Manifest!"

With a single edict, an entire army abruptly materialized.

An army formed of light unfurled. Golden scales covered their armor, the spears in the soldiers' hands were indestructible, their great shields were sturdy and thick, and their warhorses were robust and powerful.

The army brimmed with murderous intent. Countless soldiers brandished weapons and wore full battle regalia, projecting a ferocious momentum. Flags waved and war drums beat, their battle intent soaring to the heavens.

The military bearing was majestic. Every heavenly soldier possessed a broad face and heavy jowls, a tiger-like torso, and a wolf-like waist. Wearing golden armor, they wielded the eighteen martial weapons—drawn bows and stiff crossbows, silver spears and golden swords, square sky halberds, and tiger-eye whips. Standing in the rear, they naturally formed an imposing array.

Heavenly generals also appeared. First came a billowing gale and surging purple mist. Then, divine generals over ten zhang tall marched through the air. Brimming with vigor, divine light burst from their bodies, illuminating everything with stark clarity as it shot straight toward the stellar mansions!

These heavenly generals had broad foreheads and wide faces, tiger-like bodies and bear-like waists. Gripping halberds and hanging whips by their sides, they stood at the very front with awe-inspiring majesty. Long dragons with golden scales and crimson whiskers coiled around their bodies, while colorful-feathered luan birds and phoenixes carried them through the air. The sheer pressure summoned rolling wind and thunder. Men and horses surged forward, leading the army into battle!

The might of these heavenly soldiers and generals alone was no less than that of the Bear Slaughter King and Curse Devil King!

Li Qi had no doubt that he couldn't handle a single one of these heavenly soldiers. As for the heavenly generals... they could probably skewer him to death with a single thrust of a spear, leaving him absolutely no room to resist.

Not to mention, there had to be a full hundred thousand of these heavenly soldiers and generals right now!

A single edict possessed such terrifying might!

As Li Qi watched in shock, the Bear Slaughter King burst into laughter, completely unbothered. "A pewter spearhead painted silver! All flash and no substance! My children! Tear these worthless things to shreds!"

At his command, the demon soldiers unleashed guttural howls. Led by a Rank 7 lieutenant general, they formed a formation and launched a charge!

Both armies struck out.

The military qi above the demon soldiers resembled a black cloud, piercing straight into the Army Breaker star[^1]. The qi radiating from it was as ferocious as a bear. Within the murderous aura, it looked as if violent troops were rising up, taking on the appearance of fire and smoke.

Above the heavenly soldiers, the qi moved like a dragon and stood firm like a mountain, with a magnificent canopy nestled within the misty aura. The army was brave and the soldiers fierce. The qi beneath their camp was yellow-white, thick, heavy, and lustrous, while the qi above blazed like firelight, with flood dragons soaring through it.

Both sides had manifested military qi!

The two different forms of military qi violently collided, slaughtering each other.

At the same time, the Curse Devil King also struck.

He didn't have a massive retinue of demon soldiers, but he possessed far more treacherous methods.

The sky-covering wicked shadow abruptly retracted, and countless snakes, insects, rats, and ants instantly swarmed over the heavenly soldiers and generals. They gnawed at their armor and dragged down their weapons, forcing the heavenly forces onto the back foot in an instant.

But right at that moment, Shen Shuibi returned.

[^1]: In traditional Chinese astronomy and astrology, Army Breaker star (破军 | pò jūn) is the name of the seventh star of the Big Dipper. It is a highly aggresive star associated with war, destruction, and military might.
