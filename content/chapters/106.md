# Chapter 106: The Precious Canopy

Although Shen Shuibi was thrilled, Li Qi pushed his vigilance to the absolute limit.

He knew it was likely useless; if anything were to happen, Shen Shuibi would undoubtedly sense it long before he did.

After all, she was a rabbit who could hear the slightest movement from ten li away.

Even so, he could not afford to let his guard down.

The fat that Old Ma had managed to put on a few days ago was entirely gone. It was even worse this time. His eye sockets were sunken, and his ribs were protruding. Another fifteen days of relentless running had thoroughly exhausted him, causing him to lose a drastic amount of weight.

However, all the hardship was worth it.

After such a long and arduous trek, they had finally found Lady Luofu.

They traveled another twenty li. It did not take long, perhaps ten minutes, before a gorge appeared before them.

Walking through the gorge, white clouds surrounded them, blurring the scenery into a single hue. Verdant cliffs rose on both sides like a sacred mountain range. The valley was serene and otherworldly, its silence broken only by the gentle murmur of water.

Yet, the cliffs were dangerously steep. The two sides of the gorge stood close together, looking as if they had been cleaved by a blade. Shards of rock jutted out at angry angles, and the opposing peaks seemed to hang suspended in the air. The rock face was a stark, vertical canvas for waterfalls that cascaded down, dissolving into a fine mist that never reached the ground. The water mist obscured the sun, and a rainbow hung in the sky. The sight was dizzying, inspiring both terror and awe.

"This place... doesn't look like a natural gorge, does it?" Li Qi asked as he walked through it, his heart pounding with trepidation.

The lands of Baiyue had many mountains, but none so steep and imposing. The gorge walls were nearly a thousand zhang high and almost perfectly vertical. What kind of mountain grew like this?

Calling it a mountain was inaccurate... it looked more like a sword scar.

A sword a thousand zhang wide and dozens of li long had left a scar upon the earth, creating this terrifying gorge.

“Yes! She’s in here!” Shen Shuibi, however, was ecstatic.

The entire gorge was suffused with Lady Luofu's presence!

"That’s right! This is the music of the earth! The ancients said: 'The Great Earth exhales its breath, and its name is wind. So long as it does not stir, all is still, but when it blows, ten thousand hollows howl in fury. Have you not heard its roaring? Within the awe-inspiring mountain forests, the hollows of massive trees a hundred spans around sing... The gentle breeze makes a soft chorus, while the gale makes a grand harmony. When the fierce wind passes, all the hollows are left empty. Have you not seen the lingering sway and tremble?'"

"Look at this flora and the water vapor on both sides. The plants sway with the wind, and the water drops dance with it. Just like the hollows of those massive trees—resembling noses, mouths, ears, jugs, rings, mortars, and pools—the leading wind sings out, and the following wind answers! Roaring, whistling, scolding, gasping, shouting, wailing, and echoing—this is the very proof that an enlightened one is here!"

Shen Shuibi gave her joyful explanation without caring whether Li Qi understood or not. She pointed straight into the depths of the gorge. "Lady Luofu is definitely just ahead! Let's hurry."

"Careful. Take it slow. We are at the final stretch; let's not stumble on the last step," Li Qi said, urging Shen Shuibi to slow her pace.

Fortunately, Shen Shuibi was obedient. She suppressed her urge to sprint, pricked up her ears to carefully listen to their surroundings, and slowly moved forward while identifying the path.

Li Qi was right. It was the final step; they absolutely could not afford any unexpected complications.

The two humans and one horse pressed on. The stone path ahead was winding, shadowed by thick trees and rocks. Gnarled branches and ancient vines coiled around precarious boulders leaning against the sheer cliffs. Flying springs cascaded from the sky, scattering water across the entire valley.

Looking up at the towering cliffs, the mist fell like rain, and rainbows were everywhere. Though the path was treacherous, the breathtaking scenery made one reluctant to leave.

Fortunately, they had Old Ma. He could leap thirty zhang in a single bound and briefly take flight by treading on cloud qi. Thus, despite the difficult terrain, nothing could hinder the duo's progress.

They traveled all the way to the end of the gorge, only to discover a palace waiting for them.

A genuine, magnificent palace!

Not far into the gorge lay a vast lake. Beside the lake sat dozens of palace buildings with emerald tiles and soaring eaves, stretching for six or seven li like a small city. Radiant light danced in enchanting hues around them. Low canopies were draped elegantly, crimson railings provided delicate protection, and fragrant incense smoke filled the pavilions.

The halls and observation towers were incredibly luxurious. Their lanterns and candlelights illuminated heaven and earth, while the window curtains were made of the most precious and beautiful fabrics. Among them stood a towering pavilion with a glazed foundation. Its entire structure was forged from a hundred treasures and adorned with precious pearls, emitting a brilliant light. It was as if eighty-four thousand colors had merged into a dazzling, iridescent glaze, so bright that Li Qi could not even look directly at it.

The pavilion stood eight stories high. The radiance dispersed at its edges, transforming into motes of light that resembled stars and the moon, making the grand tower look as if it were suspended in the void. It was utterly majestic.

Eight types of refreshing breezes drifted from within, dispelling the cold mist and rejuvenating the spirit.

The murals painted on the canopy tower resembled heavenly art. The paintings featured wild geese and mandarin ducks, lotus flowers and koi fish, curling trails of fragrant smoke, black apes offering fruit, elk carrying flowers in their mouths, dancing green luan birds, singing colorful phoenixes, spiritual lions presenting treasures, and immortal cranes holding spirit mushrooms. They were all incredibly lifelike—they even moved. It felt as though a gentle touch would allow them to leap out of the murals and become real.

The entire palace complex was opulent and awe-inspiring, shining with overwhelming majesty like a gathering of molten gold. Li Qi was dumbfounded just looking at it, entirely at a loss for words to describe the magnificent scenery before him.

Even in his modern era, he had never seen such a grandiose structure, so intricate and beautiful. It was truly a shimmering sea of gems, dazzling and resplendent.

Just the scattering specks of light looked to Li Qi like the Milky Way pouring backward, cascading down from the top of the tower in an unimaginably beautiful display.

Every grand estate he had ever seen before now seemed like a humble hovel.

Even the Azure Splendor Palace he had seen in the Li River no longer felt as shocking.

And yet, this was merely Lady Luofu's temporary shelter while fleeing for her life?

_My god, what on earth..._

_This... this._

Li Qi was so staggered that he could not even form a coherent sentence. Although they were still about two or three li away from the great lake and the palace, his feet were rooted to the spot.

_I was so foolish,_ he thought, _to try and judge a true immortal with the perspective of a lowly ant..._ Only now, seeing this with his own eyes, did he realize how vastly he had underestimated Lady Luofu's true grandeur.

However, while Li Qi halted in absolute awe, Shen Shuibi's face instantly darkened.

It was the first time Li Qi had ever seen Shen Shuibi make such an expression. She was even exuding killing intent.

"Lady Luofu is in danger! We have to go, now!" She grabbed Li Qi, trying to drag him forward.

Li Qi shuddered, a cold sweat instantly breaking out across his back. The shock immediately snapped him back to reality.

He quickly grabbed Shen Shuibi, tightly restricting her movements, and forcefully dragged her back.

Then, he lowered his voice and roared, "Are you crazy?! If it's dangerous for Lady Luofu, wouldn't the two of us just be serving ourselves up on a silver platter if we go?!"

Just as he finished shouting, Li Qi suddenly felt a tremor. An unnatural flush of crimson spread across his face, and his heart pounded so painfully it felt as if it would split open.

As it turned out, in front of the palace, layers of dark clouds were gathering. The demonic atmosphere was incredibly thick, as if heralding the presence of some wicked entity.

The scene of the precious canopy had been so majestic and gorgeous that Li Qi had completely failed to notice the sight behind it.

_Crap, Lady Luofu really is in danger!_
